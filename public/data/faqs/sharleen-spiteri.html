FAQs: Sharleen Spiteri

Background and Early Life
Where was Sharleen Spiteri born and what are her early influences?
Sharleen Eugene Spiteri was born on 7 November 1967 in Bellshill Maternity Hospital, Bellshill, Scotland. Her parents were Eddie, a guitar-playing merchant seaman, and Vilma, a singing window-dresser. Spiteri is of Maltese, Egyptian, French, Italian, Irish, and German descent. Her teenage years were influenced by pop culture and musicians like Siouxsie Sioux. She was involved in music and had a passion for records, which she used as an escape. She was nicknamed 'Spit the Dog,' referencing a character from the TV show 'Tiswas.'
Related topics: Bellshill, Maltese Egyptians, Siouxsie Sioux, Spit the Dog
Musical Journey with Texas
What role did Sharleen Spiteri play in the band Texas and how did the band achieve success?
Sharleen Spiteri is best known as the lead singer of the rock band Texas, which she co-founded while working as a hairdresser. The band gained international success with their debut single "I Don't Want a Lover" in 1989. Their album 'White on Blonde' in 1997 was particularly successful, featuring hits like "Say What You Want" and "Halo." Texas's albums sold over 40 million copies worldwide by 2013.

Solo Career
What did Sharleen Spiteri do during Texas's hiatus and how was her solo career?
During Texas's hiatus from 2005 to 2013, Sharleen Spiteri pursued a solo career. She released her debut solo album 'Melody' in 2008, which reached No. 3 on the UK Album Charts. Her second solo album, 'The Movie Songbook,' was released in 2010 and featured covers of songs from films.
Related topics: Melody (Sharleen Spiteri album), The Movie Songbook
Return to Texas
How did Sharleen Spiteri contribute to Texas's return to the music scene?
In 2013, Texas returned from their hiatus with the album 'The Conversation,' which debuted at No. 4 on the UK Album Chart. The band continued their success with subsequent albums, celebrating their 25th anniversary with 'Texas 25' in 2015 and releasing 'Jump on Board' in 2017 and 'Hi' in 2021. Spiteri was integral in leading the band through these successful comebacks.
Related topics: The Conversation (Texas album), Texas 25
Personal Life
What is known about Sharleen Spiteri's personal life and family?
Sharleen Spiteri had a long-term relationship with Ashley Heath, with whom she has a daughter named Misty Kyd, born in 2002. She later married celebrity chef Bryn Williams in September 2018. Despite being married to a chef, Spiteri does the cooking at home.
