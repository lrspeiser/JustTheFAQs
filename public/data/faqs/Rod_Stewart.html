FAQs: Rod Stewart

Family and Personal Life
How many children does Rod Stewart have and from how many different relationships?
Rod Stewart has eight children from five different partners. These relationships include his marriages to Alana Stewart, Rachel Hunter, and Penny Lancaster, as well as relationships with Susannah Boffey and Kelly Emberg. His children include notable names such as Kimberly Stewart and Sean Stewart.
Health Challenges
Has Rod Stewart faced any significant health challenges?
Yes, Rod Stewart has faced significant health challenges including a diagnosis of thyroid cancer in 2000, which required surgery and vocal rehabilitation. In 2017, he was diagnosed with prostate cancer, from which he has since recovered. He actively raises funds for cancer charities, especially those focusing on children's cancers.
Awards and Recognition
What are some of the major awards that Rod Stewart has received?
Rod Stewart has received numerous awards and accolades throughout his career. He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame twice, once as a solo artist in 1994 and again as a member of Faces in 2012. He received a Grammy Award for Best Traditional Pop Vocal Album for 'Stardust...The Great American Songbook Vol. III' and was knighted in 2016 for his services to music and charity.
Career and Music
What is Rod Stewart best known for in his musical career?
Rod Stewart is best known for his distinctive raspy singing voice and being one of the best-selling music artists of all time, with over 120 million records sold worldwide. He began his music career in 1962, initially taking up busking with a harmonica, and later joining bands like the Dimensions and the Jeff Beck Group. His solo career took off with the release of albums such as 'Every Picture Tells a Story' which included hits like 'Maggie May.' Stewart's music spans a variety of genres, including rock, pop, blue-eyed soul, and traditional pop.
Early Life and Influence
Who were some of Rod Stewart's musical influences during his early life?
During his early life, Rod Stewart was influenced by artists like Little Richard, Al Jolson, and later, rhythm and blues singers such as Otis Redding and Sam Cooke. Stewart's family were fans of Al Jolson, and this influenced his performing style and audience engagement. He was also drawn to rock and roll after seeing Bill Haley & His Comets perform live.
Recent Activities
What are some recent projects and activities Rod Stewart has been involved with?
Recently, Rod Stewart released his 31st studio album 'The Tears of Hercules' in 2021. He also announced an upcoming collaboration with Jools Holland, 'Swing Fever,' set for release in 2024. Stewart continues to tour, having performed at events like the Platinum Party at the Palace in 2022, and is scheduled to play the Glastonbury Legends slot in 2025.
