Biography
Who is Charlie Hunnam and what is he best known for?

Early Life
What was Charlie Hunnam's early life like?
Charlie Hunnam was born to Jane Bell, a ballet dancer and business owner, and William Hunnam. He grew up in Newcastle upon Tyne, England, and later moved to Cumbria at age 12 when his mother remarried. He was expelled from Queen Elizabeth Grammar School in Penrith for fighting but eventually pursued his interest in performing arts at the Cumbria College of Art and Design.
Related topics: Newcastle upon Tyne, Cumbria College of Art and Design
Career Beginnings
How did Charlie Hunnam begin his acting career?
Charlie Hunnam began his acting career at 17 when he was discovered on Christmas Eve in a branch of JD Sports. He was cast in the teen drama "Byker Grove" for three episodes. His first major role was as Nathan Maloney in "Queer as Folk," a Channel 4 drama produced by Russell T Davies.
Related topics: Byker Grove, Queer as Folk (British TV series)
Sons of Anarchy
What was Charlie Hunnam's role in "Sons of Anarchy"?
Charlie Hunnam played Jax Teller, the leader of the SAMCRO motorcycle club in the fictional town of Charming, California. His portrayal earned him several award nominations, including a Critics' Choice Television Award and two EWwy nominations for Best Lead Actor in a Drama Series. The role was pivotal in shaping his career and popularity.
Related topics: Jax Teller, SAMCRO
Film Career
In which notable films has Charlie Hunnam appeared?
Charlie Hunnam has appeared in several notable films, including "Pacific Rim" (2013), where he starred as Raleigh Becket, "Crimson Peak" (2015), "The Lost City of Z" (2016) where he portrayed Percy Fawcett, and "King Arthur: Legend of the Sword" (2017) as the titular character. He has also worked with renowned directors like Guillermo del Toro and Guy Ritchie.
Related topics: Pacific Rim (film), Crimson Peak, King Arthur: Legend of the Sword
Personal Life
What is known about Charlie Hunnam's personal life?
Charlie Hunnam was married to actress Katharine Towne from 1999 to 2002. As of now, he has been in a long-term relationship with artist Morgana McNelis since 2005. Notably, he began training in Brazilian jiu-jitsu in 2016 and earned his blue belt in 2018.
Related topics: Katharine Towne, Brazilian jiu-jitsu
