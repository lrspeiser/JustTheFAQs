FAQs: Charles III

Marriage to Diana Spencer
How did Charles III's relationship with Diana Spencer begin and what was the outcome?
Charles III first met Lady Diana Spencer in 1977 while visiting her home. They began dating in mid-1980 and were married on July 29, 1981. The marriage produced two sons, William and Harry, but was fraught with difficulties leading to their separation in 1992 and eventual divorce in 1996. Diana died in a car crash in 1997.

What are some key aspects of Charles III's health challenges?
Charles III has undergone various health treatments over the years, including keyhole surgery on his knee and surgery for a non-cancerous growth on his nose. Early Life, Family, and Education
Who is Charles III and what is his background?
Charles III, born Charles Philip Arthur George, is the King of the United Kingdom and 14 other Commonwealth realms. He was born on November 14, 1948, at Buckingham Palace during the reign of his maternal grandfather, King George VI. He became the heir apparent at the age of three when his mother, Elizabeth II, acceded the throne. Educated at Cheam and Gordonstoun schools, he later attended Trinity College, Cambridge, becoming the first British heir apparent to earn a university degree.

When did Charles III become King?
Charles III became King on September 8, 2022, following the death of his mother, Queen Elizabeth II. He was the longest-serving heir apparent in British history and was formally proclaimed king by the Accession Council on September 10, 2022.

Accession to the throne
When did Charles III become King of the United Kingdom?
Charles III became King of the United Kingdom on 8 September 2022 following the death of his mother, Queen Elizabeth II. At 73, he became the oldest person to ascend to the British throne, surpassing William IV, who became king at the age of 64 in 1830.
Reign
What significant actions and events have marked Charles III's reign?
Charles III's reign has seen his coronation in 2023 and a notable shift in financial policies regarding the monarchy, such as requesting profits from Britain's offshore windfarms to be used for public good. His reign has also focused on fostering a diverse representation and working to promote Commonwealth unity.

Early Life
Where was Charles III born and who are his parents?
Charles III was born as Prince Charles of Edinburgh on 14 November 1948 at Buckingham Palace, London, England. He is the eldest son of Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh. He was christened by the Archbishop of Canterbury in the Music Room of Buckingham Palace.
Reign
What major events have occurred during the reign of Charles III?
During his reign, Charles III witnessed significant events including his coronation on May 6, 2023, and a cancer diagnosis that temporarily suspended public engagements in 2024. Public Perception and Media
What has been the public and media perception of Charles III?
Public perception of Charles has varied over time, often influenced by his relationships, particularly with Diana and Camilla.
