FAQs: Vijay (actor)

Overview
Who is Vijay and why is he significant in Tamil cinema?
Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar, known as Vijay, is an Indian actor and playback singer renowned for his work in Tamil cinema. With a career spanning over three decades, he has appeared in 68 films and is one of the most commercially successful actors in Tamil cinema. His films are among the highest-grossing Tamil films of all time, and he is one of the highest-paid actors in India. Vijay has a significant fan following and is fondly referred to as 'Thalapathy' ('commander').

Early life and family
What was Vijay's early life like?
Vijay was born on June 22, 1974, in Madras, Tamil Nadu. His father, S. A. Chandrasekhar, is a film director, and his mother, Shoba Chandrasekhar, is a playback singer. Vijay had a sister who passed away at a young age. He completed his schooling at Fathima School and Balalok School, both in Chennai, and later pursued a bachelor's degree in visual communication at Loyola College, Chennai.
Related topics: Madras, S. A. Chandrasekhar, Shoba Chandrasekhar, Loyola College, Chennai
Film career
How did Vijay transition from a child actor to a leading star?
Vijay debuted as a child actor in the Tamil film 'Vetri' in 1984, produced by P. S. Veerappa. He continued to work as a child actor in several films before taking on his first lead role in 'Naalaiya Theerpu' in 1992. Vijay gained recognition with hits like 'Poove Unakkaga', 'Love Today', and 'Kadhalukku Mariyadhai'. By 2004, with films like 'Ghilli', he was established as a leading actor.
Related topics: Vetri (film), Naalaiya Theerpu, Ghilli, Vasantha Raagam
Political career
When did Vijay commence his political career, and what party is he affiliated with?
Vijay launched his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam, on February 2, 2024. Prior to that, he had formed his fan club 'Vijay Makkal Iyakkam' in 2009, which supported political campaigns and contested in local body elections successfully. His entry into politics marks a significant transition from cinema to public service, with potential expansion of his political influences beyond Tamil Nadu.
Related topics: Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam, Vijay Makkal Iyakkam
Awards and accolades
What are some notable awards received by Vijay?
Vijay has received numerous accolades throughout his career, including the Kalaimaamani award in 1998 from the Government of Tamil Nadu, and an honorary doctorate from Dr. M.G.R. Educational Institute in 2007 for his contributions to the film industry. He was also named Best International Actor in 2018 at the IARA awards in the UK.
Related topics: Kalaimaamani, Dr. M.G.R. Educational Institute
