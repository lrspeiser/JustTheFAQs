FAQs: Jawan (film)

Overview
What is the film 'Jawan' about?
Jawan is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language vigilante action thriller film. Directed by Atlee in his Bollywood debut, it stars Shah Rukh Khan in a dual role as a father and son, alongside Nayanthara, Vijay Sethupathi, and Deepika Padukone in a special appearance. The plot follows Azad, the jailer of a women's prison, who recruits inmates to expose corruption in India. Azad plans to use ransom money to aid impoverished farmers and demands public service improvements.
Related topics: Vigilante film, Action film, Bollywood, Corruption in India
Cast and Crew
Who are the main stars of 'Jawan'?
The film stars Shah Rukh Khan in a dual role as a father and son, with Nayanthara, Vijay Sethupathi, Priyamani, and Sanya Malhotra playing significant roles. Deepika Padukone makes a special appearance. The film is directed by Atlee and produced by Gauri Khan and Gaurav Verma under Red Chillies Entertainment.

Production
What were the main locations for the filming of 'Jawan'?
The principal photography for Jawan began in September 2021 in Pune and included locations like Mumbai, Goregaon, Hyderabad, Chennai, Rajasthan, and Aurangabad. Some scenes were re-shot for the Tamil version, particularly involving the song 'Zinda Banda'.
Related topics: Pune, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Chennai
Release
What was significant about the release of 'Jawan'?
Jawan was released on 7 September 2023 in standard, IMAX, 4DX, and other premium formats. It premiered on over 10,000 screens worldwide, the largest for a Hindi film, and was the first Indian film to release at the IMAX Leonberg, the largest IMAX theatre in the world.
Related topics: IMAX, 4DX
Music
Who composed the music for 'Jawan', and what are some key tracks?
The soundtrack for Jawan was composed by Anirudh Ravichander, marking his debut as a solo composer in Bollywood. The soundtrack features seven original songs such as 'Zinda Banda', 'Chaleya', and 'Not Ramaiya Vastavaiya'. The music was well-received and became popular on social media.
Related topics: Anirudh Ravichander
Themes & Influences
What themes does 'Jawan' explore?
Jawan deals with themes such as women's empowerment, corruption, and social justice. It also contains references to real-life incidents like the Bofors scandal and draws inspiration from films like 'Chak De! India'. The character of Dr. Eeram is inspired by Dr. Kafeel Khan's real-life story.
Related topics: Women's empowerment, Chak De! India, Bofors scandal
Sequel
Is there a sequel planned for 'Jawan'?
Yes, following the film's success, a sequel to Jawan has been confirmed by director Atlee. There's also a potential spin-off centered around the character Vikram Rathore, showcasing further development of the story.
