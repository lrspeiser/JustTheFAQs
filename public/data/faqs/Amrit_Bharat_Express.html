FAQs: Amrit Bharat Express

Services
What services are provided on the Amrit Bharat Express?
The Amrit Bharat Express offers sleeper class and general unreserved seating. On-board services include electric outlets, reading lights, CCTV cameras, bio-vacuum toilets, sensor-based water taps, and a passenger information system, making it convenient and comfortable for long-distance travel.

History
When did the Amrit Bharat Express first begin service?
The Amrit Bharat Express was first announced in July 2023, with the initial train being flagged off on December 30, 2023, by then Prime Minister Narendra Modi. It started commercial service on January 1, 2024. The trainsets are manufactured by Integral Coach Factory, Chennai. Production for 200 more such trains is underway.

Technical Details
What rolling stock and facilities does the Amrit Bharat Express use?
The Amrit Bharat Express uses the Amrit Bharat trainset with non-air-conditioned coaches. The trains are powered by locomotives from Chittaranjan Locomotive Works and feature coaches from the Integral Coach Factory. They operate on tracks with a gauge of 1,676 mm (5 ft 6 in) and are electrified via a 25 kV, 50 Hz AC overhead line system.

What are some notable routes of the Amrit Bharat Express?
The Amrit Bharat Express operates notable routes such as Darbhanga to Anand Vihar Terminal, covering 1,137 km, and Malda Town to SMVT Bengaluru, which spans 2,272 km. The services vary in frequency, with some being bi-weekly and others weekly. These routes highlight the focus on long-distance connectivity across India.

Overview
What is the Amrit Bharat Express and where does it operate?
The Amrit Bharat Express is a superfast express service operated by Indian Railways. It is designed to be a non-air-conditioned, low-cost service with sleeper and unreserved accommodations. The trains connect cities that are more than 800 km (500 mi) apart or take more than ten hours to travel with existing services. The Amrit Bharat Express began commercial service on January 1, 2024. The trainset consists of two locomotives, one on either side, and 22 coaches, with a maximum operating speed of 110–130 km/h (68–81 mph).

Route and Speed
What is the average and maximum speed of the Amrit Bharat Express?
The maximum operating speed of the Amrit Bharat Express is 130 km/h (81 mph), while its design speed can reach up to 160 km/h (99 mph). The average operating speed is generally lower, reflecting its long-distance travel nature across various terrains.
