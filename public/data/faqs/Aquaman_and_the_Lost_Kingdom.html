FAQs: Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom

Plot and Storyline
What are the main plot points of Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom?
In 'Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom,' Arthur Curry, now king of Atlantis, must work with his half-brother Orm to stop Black Manta from using the cursed Black Trident to destroy Atlantis and the world. As they struggle against environmental perils and family betrayal, they uncover the secrets of the lost seventh kingdom of the seas.
Related topics: Aquaman, Black Manta, Orm Marius (DC Extended Universe), Atlantis (Aquaman)
Release and Reception
How was Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom received critically and commercially?
The film premiered at The Grove in Los Angeles and had a worldwide box office gross of $439.4 million, making it the highest-grossing DCEU film since the first Aquaman. However, it received negative reviews, with critics pointing out its lack of originality and failure to live up to its predecessor despite strong performances by Jason Momoa and Patrick Wilson.

Production Controversies
What controversies affected the production of Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom?
The production faced controversies primarily related to Amber Heard's involvement, due to ongoing legal issues with her ex-husband Johnny Depp. A petition sought to have her removed from the film, although she remained part of the cast with a reduced role. There was also some tension regarding Heard's chemistry with Jason Momoa, according to studio testimonies.
Related topics: Amber Heard, Johnny Depp
Future of Aquaman
Is there potential for another Aquaman film after the Lost Kingdom?
Despite mixed reviews, discussions about continuing the Aquaman series are ongoing. Director James Wan has expressed interest in another sequel, particularly exploring Arthur's relationship with his son. Jason Momoa is confirmed to play the character Lobo in the DC Universe, suggesting potential shifts in his participation in future Aquaman projects.
Related topics: James Wan, Lobo (DC Comics), DC Universe (franchise)
Production and Direction
How did James Wan approach the sequel differently compared to the first Aquaman film?
James Wan returned as director, choosing to expand on the first film's world-building with a more serious tone and themes such as climate change. The sequel also incorporated elements from the Silver Age of Comic Books and was inspired by retro science fiction and horror films, characterizing the sequel as both a buddy comedy and adventure film.
Related topics: James Wan, Silver Age of Comic Books, Aquaman (film)
Characters and Cast
Who are the main characters in the film and who plays them?
The main characters include Jason Momoa as Arthur Curry/Aquaman, Patrick Wilson as Orm Marius, Yahya Abdul-Mateen II as Black Manta, Amber Heard as Mera, and Nicole Kidman as Atlanna. The film focuses on the 'bromance' between Aquaman and Orm as they join forces to save their kingdom.
