FAQs: List of highest-grossing Indian films

Overview
How have Indian film markets expanded globally over the years?
Indian films have reached global markets since the early 20th century. Since 2003, Indian movies have been screened in over 90 countries, resulting in a global presence. The increase in global audiences also saw a rise in ticket prices, the number of theatres, and film availability, leading to enhanced box office collections during the early 21st century.

Box Office Dynamics
Which Indian film currently holds the record for the highest global box office earnings?
The film 'Dangal' holds the record, with worldwide earnings reported between ₹1,914–2,500 crore. Its impressive showing underscores the global appeal of Indian cinema.

Industry Growth
Which Indian film industry contributes the most to the domestic box office?
As of 2023, the Hindi film industry leads the domestic box office with 44% of revenue, followed by the Telugu industry at 19% and the Tamil industry at 16%. Other regional industries, such as Kannada, Malayalam, and Punjabi, contribute to the rest along with foreign films.

Franchise Success
Which film franchise first crossed the ₹100 crore club and the ₹1000 crore club?
The Khiladi franchise was the first to cross the ₹100 crore mark, while the Baahubali series was the first to surpass ₹1000 crore at the global box office.
