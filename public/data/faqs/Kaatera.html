FAQs: Kaatera

Release and Reception
How did 'Kaatera' perform at the box office and with critics?
'Kaatera' received positive reviews from critics upon its release on 29 December 2023. Critics praised the direction, performances, and the film's message. It became the highest-grossing Kannada film of 2023, though box office figures varied significantly among different reports, with some estimates reaching ₹200 crore. The film also performed well internationally with releases in countries like Dubai, Germany, and the USA.

Production
Who were involved in the making of 'Kaatera'?
'Kaatera' was directed by Tharun Sudhir and co-written with Jadesh Kumar Hampi. It was produced by Rockline Venkatesh under Rockline Entertainments, with cinematography by Sudhakar S. Raj and editing by K. M. Prakash. The music was composed by V. Harikrishna.

Plot Details
What challenges does the character Kaatera face in the film?
In the 1970s, Kaatera, played by Darshan, is a blacksmith who battles feudal landlords Devaraya and Kaalegowda to implement the Land Reforms Act and help farmers escape exploitation. After learning about a plot against him and his love interest, Prabhavathi, Kaatera engages in a violent struggle. Despite achieving some success, he faces personal tragedy and eventual imprisonment, but continues to inspire hope for change in his village.

Awards and Nominations
What awards and nominations did 'Kaatera' receive?
'Kaatera' garnered multiple award nominations, including seven at the 69th Filmfare Awards South for categories such as Best Film, Best Director (Tharun Sudhir), and Best Actor (Darshan). It won awards at the 12th South Indian International Movie Awards for Best Film, Best Debutant Actress (Aradhana Ram), and Best Music Director (V. Harikrishna), among others.

General Information
What is the movie 'Kaatera' about?
'Kaatera' is a 2023 Indian Kannada-language action drama film directed by Tharun Sudhir. It is inspired by a real-life incident in a village in Karnataka during the 1970s. The film tells the story of Kaatera, a blacksmith who fights against feudal landlords to implement land reforms. It stars Darshan, Aradhana Ram, and Jagapathi Babu, among others. The movie was praised for its engaging plot and performances, eventually becoming the highest-grossing Kannada film of 2023.

Plot
What is the story behind 'Kaatera'?
Set in the 1970s and 1989, 'Kaatera' tells the story of a blacksmith who fights to implement land reforms to protect farmers from feudal landlords. It involves themes of love, betrayal, and justice as Kaatera tackles caste discrimination and seeks justice for the death of his beloved.
