Who are the main actors in Silent Night?
The main cast includes Joel Kinnaman as Brian Godlock, Kid Cudi as Detective Dennis Vassel, Harold Torres as Playa, and Catalina Sandino Moreno as Saya.

Plot Details
How does the plot of Silent Night unfold?
Silent Night follows Brian Godlock, an electrician who plans to avenge his son Taylor's death by targeting the gang members involved in his son's drive-by shooting on Christmas Eve. Brian's journey involves bodybuilding, familiarizing himself with weapons, and gathering evidence against the gangsters, leading to a dramatic and violent confrontation.

Production Insights
What unique production aspects are notable in Silent Night?
The film was announced as an action movie without spoken dialogue and involved innovative filmmaking techniques by director John Woo to convey story and emotions through visuals and music. Joel Kinnaman, who plays the lead role, tried method acting by not speaking throughout the filming.
Related topics: John Woo, Joel Kinnaman, Action film
General Overview
What is the 2023 film Silent Night about?
Silent Night is a 2023 American action thriller directed by John Woo. The film features minimal spoken dialogue and follows an aggrieved father, Brian Godlock, avenging the death of his son who was killed by local gang members in a drive-by shooting on Christmas Eve. This marks Woo's return to American filmmaking since Paycheck in 2003.
Related topics: John Woo, Action thriller film, Drive-by shooting
Release Information
When was Silent Night released, and how was it received?
Silent Night was released in theaters by Lionsgate on December 1, 2023, and later on digital platforms on December 19, 2023. The film received mixed reviews, with a 59% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a 53 out of 100 score on Metacritic. Some critics praised its action sequences, while others felt the lack of dialogue was a drawback.
