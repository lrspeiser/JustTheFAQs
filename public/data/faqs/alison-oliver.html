FAQs: Alison Oliver

General Information
Who is Alison Oliver?
Alison Oliver is an Irish actress born on June 3, 1997, in Ballintemple, Cork, Ireland. She is known for her work in television, film, and theatre, with a significant debut in the BBC Three and Hulu miniseries 'Conversations with Friends'.
Related topics: BBC Three, Hulu, Conversations with Friends (TV series)
Early Life
Where did Alison Oliver grow up and study?
Alison Oliver was born in Ballintemple, Cork, and later moved to Blackrock. She attended Scoil Mhuire, a private school in Cork, and took drama and dance lessons at The Performer's Academy and the Cork School of Dance. She later attended The Lir Academy at Trinity College Dublin, graduating with a BA in Acting in 2020.
