FAQs: Ryan Gosling

Early Life
Where was Ryan Gosling born?
Ryan Gosling was born on November 12, 1980, at St. Joseph's Hospital in London, Ontario, Canada. He is the son of Thomas Ray Gosling, a traveling salesman for a paper mill, and Donna, a secretary. Gosling's family moved around a lot because of his father's work, and he lived in Cornwall and Burlington, Ontario, after his parents divorced when he was 13. Gosling and his older sister Mandi lived with their mother. He attended several schools, including Gladstone Public School, Cornwall Collegiate and Vocational School, and Lester B. Pearson High School.
Related topics: London, Ontario
Acting Career
How did Ryan Gosling start his acting career?
Ryan Gosling began his acting career at the age of 13 on Disney Channel's 'The All New Mickey Mouse Club' from 1993 to 1995. He was given a two-year contract as a Mouseketeer and moved to Orlando, Florida. Though he appeared on-screen infrequently, Gosling described the job as the greatest two years of his life. Fellow cast members included Britney Spears, Christina Aguilera, and Justin Timberlake, with whom he became close friends.
Related topics: The All New Mickey Mouse Club, Disney Channel
Acting Career
What was Ryan Gosling's breakthrough role?
Ryan Gosling's breakthrough role was playing a Jewish neo-Nazi in the 2001 film 'The Believer.' His performance was praised as electrifying and terrifyingly convincing. The film won the Grand Jury Prize at the Sundance Film Festival and is considered pivotal in shaping his subsequent career choices.
Related topics: The Believer (2001 film), Sundance Film Festival
Acting Career
What are some of Ryan Gosling's most critically acclaimed performances?
Ryan Gosling has received critical acclaim for his performances in: - 'Half Nelson' (2006), where he played a drug-addicted teacher, earning him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor. - 'Lars and the Real Girl' (2007), for his portrayal of an introvert who falls in love with a sex doll, allowing him to showcase his range as an actor. - 'La La Land' (2016), where he played a jazz musician, earning him a Golden Globe and a second Academy Award nomination.
Related topics: Half Nelson (film), Lars and the Real Girl, La La Land (film)
Personal Life
Is Ryan Gosling married?
Ryan Gosling has been in a relationship with actress Eva Mendes since September 2011. They have two daughters, born in 2014 and 2016. While there has been speculation about their marriage status, Mendes has referred to it as 'mysterious,' leaving their official status publicly unconfirmed.
Related topics: Eva Mendes
Charity Work
What charitable activities is Ryan Gosling involved in?
Ryan Gosling supports several social causes. He has worked with PETA to promote better conditions for animals, such as encouraging the use of improved methods for chicken slaughter by fast-food chains. He volunteered in Biloxi, Mississippi, to aid in cleanup efforts following Hurricane Katrina. Additionally, he supports Invisible Children, Inc., and has traveled to countries like Uganda and eastern Congo with the Enough Project to raise awareness about conflicts in the regions.
Related topics: PETA, Invisible Children, Inc., Hurricane Katrina, Enough Project
Music Career
Does Ryan Gosling have a music career?
Yes, Ryan Gosling is part of an indie rock band called 'Dead Man's Bones,' which he formed with his friend Zach Shields. They released an album in 2009 and even performed a tour, incorporating local children's choirs in their concerts. Gosling has contributed vocals, piano, guitar, bass guitar, and cello to the band's debut album.
Related topics: Dead Man's Bones
Acting Career
What is Ryan Gosling's highest-grossing film?
Ryan Gosling's highest-grossing film is 'Barbie' (2023), in which he played Ken. The film was both a commercial success and critically acclaimed, contributing to its status as his highest-grossing release to date.
