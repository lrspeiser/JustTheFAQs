FAQs: Wikipedia: Featured Pictures

Nominating Featured Pictures
How can I nominate an image to become a featured picture on Wikipedia?
To nominate an image for consideration as a featured picture, use the Featured Picture Candidates nomination process. This includes checking the featured picture criteria and submitting the image for review.
Related topics: Wikipedia:Featured picture candidates, Wikipedia:Featured picture criteria, Wikipedia:Featured picture candidates/Nomination procedure
Introduction to Featured Pictures
What qualifies an image to become a featured picture on Wikipedia?
To become a featured picture, an image must be freely licensed or in the public domain, meet high technical standards, and significantly enhance at least one Wikipedia article. There are currently 8,157 featured pictures, organized by topics such as animals and artwork.
Related topics: Wikipedia:Featured picture criteria, Free content, Public domain
Directory of Featured Picture Categories
What are some of the categories for featured pictures on Wikipedia?
Featured pictures are categorized into several topics, including Animals, Artwork, Culture, Entertainment, and Lifestyle, among others. Each category can be further explored through its subpages, providing a detailed list of images under specific subcategories.
Related topics: Wikipedia:Featured pictures#Animals, Wikipedia:Featured pictures#Artwork, Wikipedia:Featured pictures#Culture, entertainment, and lifestyle
Examples of Featured Pictures
Can you provide examples of featured pictures from different categories?
Examples of featured pictures include a starfish from the Animals category, Rembrandt's 'Return of the Prodigal Son' from Artwork, and a famous tornado image from the Natural Phenomena category. Each category showcases images that excel in quality and encyclopedic value.

Exploring Featured Pictures
How can I explore featured pictures by subject or country?
Featured pictures can be explored by subject or by country through Wikimedia Commons. This allows users to discover images relevant to specific topics or geographic regions, showcasing the wide variety of visually stunning content available.
Related topics: Wikimedia Commons, Category:Featured pictures by subject, Category:Featured pictures by country
Picture of the Day (POTD)
How can I display the Picture of the Day on my user page?
To display the Picture of the Day on your user page, insert the text {{pic of the day}} or {{POTD}} at the desired location. This feature allows users to showcase one or more featured pictures daily.
Related topics: Wikipedia:Picture of the day, Template:Pic of the day, Template:POTD
