FAQs: Manchester United F.C.

General Information
What is the full name of Manchester United F.C. and where are they based?
The full name of the club is Manchester United Football Club. It is based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England.
Related topics: Manchester United W.F.C., Old Trafford
History and Achievements
How did Manchester United F.C. get its nickname 'The Red Devils'?
Manchester United's nickname 'The Red Devils' was inspired by the Salford Rugby Club, which was known as 'The Red Devils.' This nickname was adopted and incorporated into their crest in the 1960s.
Related topics: Salford Rugby Club
History and Achievements
What are some of Manchester United's major domestic and international successes?
Manchester United has won a record 20 top-flight league titles, 13 FA Cups, 6 League Cups, and a record 21 FA Community Shields. Internationally, they have won the European Cup/UEFA Champions League three times, as well as the UEFA Europa League, UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, UEFA Super Cup, Intercontinental Cup, and the FIFA Club World Cup each once.

Notable Managers
Why is Sir Alex Ferguson famous in Manchester United's history?
Sir Alex Ferguson is famous for being Manchester United's longest-serving and most successful manager. He managed the team from 1986 to 2013, winning 38 trophies, including 13 league titles, five FA Cups, and two Champions League titles.

Rivalries and Support
Who are Manchester United's main rivals?
Manchester United's main rivals include Liverpool, Manchester City, Arsenal, and Leeds United. The rivalry with Liverpool is particularly famous, rooted in industrial competition and football successes of both clubs.
Related topics: Liverpool F.C., Manchester City F.C., Arsenal F.C., Leeds United F.C.
Home Grounds
What is the historical significance of Old Trafford, Manchester United's home ground?
Old Trafford has been Manchester United's home ground since 1910. It is known for being one of the most famous stadiums in the world and has been redeveloped several times, including post-World War II when it was damaged by bombing. Old Trafford has a current capacity of 74,310.

Fan Base
How large is Manchester United's fan base and global reach?
Manchester United has one of the largest fan bases worldwide, with over 82 million Facebook followers as of July 2023 and more than 200 officially recognized branches of Manchester United Supporters Clubs in at least 24 countries.
Related topics: FC Barcelona, Real Madrid
Ownership
Who currently owns Manchester United, and how has the ownership evolved?
Manchester United is primarily owned by the Glazer family, who purchased the club in 2005 using a leveraged buyout, which incurred significant debts. As of late 2023, Sir Jim Ratcliffe's company, Ineos, holds a 25% stake and is in charge of football operations, while the Glazers remain majority shareholders.
