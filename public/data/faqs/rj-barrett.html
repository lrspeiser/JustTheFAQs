FAQs: RJ Barrett

General Information
Who is RJ Barrett?
Rowan Alexander "RJ" Barrett Jr. is a Canadian professional basketball player who plays for the Toronto Raptors in the NBA. He was born on June 14, 2000, in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Barrett is known for his versatility on the court, playing as a shooting guard and small forward. He was the third overall pick by the New York Knicks in the 2019 NBA draft after playing one year of college basketball at Duke.

Early Life and Career
What was RJ Barrett's early life like?
RJ Barrett was born in Toronto, Canada. He is the son of former basketball player Rowan Barrett. As a child, Barrett was interested in basketball and played with a mini-hoop in France where his father was playing professionally. He began playing organized basketball in France before moving to Mississauga, Ontario, where he excelled in multiple sports including basketball. By age 12, after struggling in a basketball game, Barrett decided to focus on basketball. He trained with the Canada Basketball junior academy and was coached by his godfather, Steve Nash.

High School Career
Where did RJ Barrett play high school basketball?
RJ Barrett played high school basketball at St. Marcellinus Secondary School in Mississauga and later transferred to Montverde Academy in Florida, which is known for its strong basketball program. At Montverde, Barrett excelled and led his team to multiple victories, earning numerous accolades.

College Career
What achievements did RJ Barrett have in college?
RJ Barrett attended Duke University for one season, during which he posted impressive stats. He averaged 22.6 points, 7.6 rebounds, and 4.3 assists per game. Barrett was named All-ACC First Team, ACC All-Freshman team, and a Sporting News All-American. He also set a Duke freshman scoring record in his debut game and had a triple-double performance against North Carolina State.

Professional Career
How has RJ Barrett's NBA career progressed?
RJ Barrett was drafted by the New York Knicks as the third overall pick in the 2019 NBA Draft. He started his NBA career with strong performances, including a debut game with 21 points. Barrett has improved his stats over the years and had significant career highlights with the Knicks. In 2023, Barrett was traded to his hometown team, the Toronto Raptors, where he continues to showcase his skills.

National Team Career
What impact has RJ Barrett had on Canada's national basketball team?
RJ Barrett played a key role on Canada's national teams, starting with the under-16 team that won silver at the 2015 FIBA Americas U16 Championship. Barrett led Canada to a gold medal at the 2017 FIBA Under-19 Basketball World Cup, earning MVP honors. He debuted for the senior national team in 2018 and qualified Canada for the 2024 Olympics. Barrett was instrumental in Canada's historic bronze medal win at the 2023 FIBA World Cup.
