FAQs: Thanksgiving (2023 film)

Production
How did the 'Thanksgiving' film come into production?
The idea for the 'Thanksgiving' film originated from Eli Roth's fake trailer for the film Grindhouse in 2007. Roth collaborated with screenwriter Jeff Rendell and, amidst various developments and cast decisions in 2023, finalized the script. Patrick Dempsey and Addison Rae were prominent cast additions in early 2023. Filming took place in Toronto and Hamilton, Ontario.
Related topics: Eli Roth, Grindhouse (film), Patrick Dempsey, Addison Rae

Who are the main actors in 'Thanksgiving (2023 film)'?
The main actors include Patrick Dempsey as Sheriff Eric Newlon, Nell Verlaque as Jessica, Addison Rae as Gaby, Jalen Thomas Brooks as Bobby, Milo Manheim as Ryan, Rick Hoffman as Thomas Wright, and Gina Gershon as Amanda Collins.
Related topics: Patrick Dempsey, Addison Rae, Milo Manheim
Plot
How does the plot of the movie unfold?
The film begins with a chaotic Black Friday event gone wrong at a store called RightMart. Jessica Wright, the owner's daughter, and others become targets a year later by a masked killer named "John Carver." The killer is linked to those involved in the tragic incident, posting gruesome scenes on social media. As the story progresses, several characters fall victim to the chilling revenge spree.
Related topics: Black Friday (shopping)
Reception
How was 'Thanksgiving (2023 film)' received by critics and audiences?
'Thanksgiving' received generally positive reviews, with an 84% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a Metacritic score of 63. Critics praised its mix of humor with horror, although some felt it didn't fully achieve cult status. Audiences gave it a CinemaScore of 'B−'.

General Information
What is 'Thanksgiving (2023 film)' about?
'Thanksgiving' is a 2023 American slasher film directed by Eli Roth and written by Jeff Rendell. It is based on a story by Roth and Rendell, who also produced it with Roger Birnbaum. The film follows a small Massachusetts town that is terrorized by a killer in a John Carver mask around the Thanksgiving holiday one year after a Black Friday riot ended in tragedy. The film stars Patrick Dempsey, Addison Rae, Milo Manheim, Jalen Thomas Brooks, Nell Verlaque, Rick Hoffman, and Gina Gershon.
Related topics: Eli Roth, Jeff Rendell

Production
How did 'Thanksgiving' come to be a feature film?
'Thanksgiving' originated from a fake trailer created by Eli Roth for the film 'Grindhouse' in 2007. Following interest in the concept, scripts were developed over several years. In 2023, Spyglass Media Group produced the film, and Eli Roth departed from another project to direct it. Principal photography took place in Toronto and Hamilton, Ontario, from March to May 2023.
Related topics: Grindhouse (film), Eli Roth
Plot
What happens in the movie 'Thanksgiving'?
In 'Thanksgiving,' a mob gathers outside a Massachusetts superstore, RightMart, before a Black Friday sale. Chaos ensues, leading to multiple deaths. A year later, the town faces a new threat when a masked killer known as 'John Carver' targets residents involved in the prior year's turmoil. The killer, revealed to be the town sheriff, confesses his motive stems from a personal vendetta linked to the riot death of his lover and unborn child.
