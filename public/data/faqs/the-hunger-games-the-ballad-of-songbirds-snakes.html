FAQs: The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes

Film Overview
What is 'The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes' about?
'The Hunger Games: The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes' is a 2023 American dystopian action film directed by Francis Lawrence. It serves as a prequel to the 2012 film 'The Hunger Games' and is the fifth installment in the franchise. The film focuses on Coriolanus Snow, a mentor for the 10th Annual Hunger Games tribute, Lucy Gray Baird. Set in the war-ravaged nation of Panem, Snow aims to restore his family's fortunes by winning the Games.
Related topics: The Hunger Games (film), The Hunger Games (franchise)

Who directed the film?
The film was directed by Francis Lawrence, who also directed the previous three installments in 'The Hunger Games' film series.
Related topics: Francis Lawrence
Plot
What is the plot of 'The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes'?
In Panem, Coriolanus Snow mentors Lucy Gray Baird, a tribute in the 10th Annual Hunger Games. With a desire to win a scholarship to save his family's financial status, Snow navigates challenges as Lucy Gray captivates audiences and navigates threats during the Games. Themes of survival, betrayal, and ambition are explored as Snow faces moral dilemmas while Lucy Gray fights for her life.
Related topics: Coriolanus Snow, The Hunger Games, Panem
Cast
Who are the main cast members of the film?
The main cast includes Tom Blyth as Coriolanus Snow, Rachel Zegler as Lucy Gray Baird, Hunter Schafer as Tigris Snow, Jason Schwartzman as Lucretius 'Lucky' Flickerman, Peter Dinklage as Casca Highbottom, Josh Andrés Rivera as Sejanus Plinth, and Viola Davis as Dr. Volumnia Gaul.
Related topics: Tom Blyth, Rachel Zegler, Hunter Schafer, Jason Schwartzman, Peter Dinklage, Viola Davis
Production
Where was 'The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes' filmed?
The film was shot across several locations, including Wrocław, Berlin, and Leipzig. Notable filming sites include the Monument to the Battle of the Nations in Leipzig and the Centennial Hall in Wrocław.
Related topics: Wrocław, Berlin, Leipzig
Reception
How was the film received critically and commercially?
The film grossed $349 million worldwide against a $100 million budget. Critics offered mixed reviews, praising the cast and storyline but criticizing the execution. On Rotten Tomatoes, it has a 64% approval rating, with Metacritic scoring it 54 out of 100. Audience feedback was more positive, with a B+ CinemaScore and 87% positivity on PostTrak.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic, CinemaScore
Music
Who composed the music for the film?
The score for 'The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes' was composed by James Newton Howard, continuing his involvement from the previous 'Hunger Games' films.

Sequel
Is there a sequel planned for 'The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes'?
Yes, a sequel is planned based on the novel 'Sunrise on the Reaping.' Scheduled for release on November 20, 2026, it will be set forty years after 'The Ballad of Songbirds & Snakes.' Francis Lawrence is in talks to return as the director.
