FAQs: Featured Pictures

General Questions
What are featured pictures on Wikipedia?
Featured pictures on Wikipedia are recognized as the finest images in terms of technical quality and relevance to articles. They must be freely licensed or in the public domain, and they should significantly enhance at least one article. There are currently 8,175 featured pictures available across various categories.
Related topics: Wikipedia:Featured picture criteria, Free content, Public domain, Category:Featured pictures
Submitting Pictures
How can I nominate an image to become a featured picture?
To nominate a specific image as a featured picture, you can submit it at the 'Featured Picture Candidates' page on Wikipedia. This process allows users to present the image for consideration based on the established criteria.
Related topics: Wikipedia:Featured picture candidates
Daily Highlights
What is the Picture of the Day (POTD)?
The Picture of the Day (POTD) is a featured image that is displayed on the Wikipedia main page each day. One or more featured pictures are selected as the POTD, showcasing their importance and quality to the broader community. You can include a template in your user page to display the current POTD.
Related topics: Wikipedia:Picture of the day
Browsing Featured Pictures
How can I browse featured pictures from Wikimedia projects?
You can browse featured pictures from all Wikimedia projects by subject or by country via Wikimedia Commons, which is a repository of free-use images, sounds, and other media files. This makes it easy to find high-quality images across diverse topics.
Related topics: Wikimedia Commons, Category:Featured pictures by subject, Category:Featured pictures by country
Categories
What categories are included among featured pictures?
Featured pictures on Wikipedia are categorized into several topics, such as Animals, Artwork, Culture, Food, Natural Phenomena, History, and more. Each category includes subpages that further classify the images based on specific subjects or themes.

Featured Pictures Tools
What tools are available for contributing to featured pictures?
Wikipedia provides several tools for contributing to featured pictures, including criteria for nominations, candidate procedures, and archived lists of past candidates. There are also specific categories for finding featured picture thumbs and access to the Picture of the Day archive.
