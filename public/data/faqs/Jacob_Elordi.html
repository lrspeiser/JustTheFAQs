FAQs: Jacob Elordi

Filmography
What notable films and TV series has Jacob Elordi appeared in?
Jacob Elordi has appeared in a variety of films and TV series. His film credits include 'The Kissing Booth' series, '2 Hearts', 'The Mortuary Collection', 'Deep Water', 'Saltburn', and 'Priscilla'. On television, he is best known for his main role as Nate Jacobs in the series 'Euphoria'.
Related topics: The Kissing Booth (film series), Euphoria (American TV series)
Early Life
What inspired Jacob Elordi to pursue acting?
Jacob Elordi was inspired to become an actor by Heath Ledger, particularly due to Ledger's role in the 2008 film 'The Dark Knight'. Elordi began performing in school musicals at age 12 and took acting classes to further his interest. He was also influenced by actors like Marlon Brando, Laurence Olivier, Daniel Day-Lewis, and Christian Bale, whose biographies he read and whose behaviors he tried to emulate.
Related topics: Heath Ledger, The Dark Knight
Career
How did Jacob Elordi rise to fame, and what roles is he best known for?
Jacob Elordi rose to fame with his role as Noah Flynn in Netflix's 'The Kissing Booth' film series (2018–2021), despite its negative critical reception. He further gained prominence for his portrayal of Nate Jacobs, a troubled high school football player, in HBO's teen drama series 'Euphoria' (2019–present). Elordi has been recognized for his role as Elvis Presley in the film 'Priscilla' and as a wealthy university student in 'Saltburn', which earned him a BAFTA nomination.
Related topics: Netflix, The Kissing Booth (film series), Euphoria (American TV series), BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role
Public Image
What controversies has Jacob Elordi faced in the media?
Jacob Elordi faced controversy in 2024 due to an alleged altercation with a radio show producer who claimed Elordi got physical after a controversial request. This incident led to an investigation by the New South Wales Police. Additionally, his relationships with various high-profile figures have been a subject of media scrutiny.
Related topics: New South Wales Police Force
Public Image
Why is Jacob Elordi considered a heartthrob for Generation Z?
Jacob Elordi is considered a heartthrob for Generation Z largely due to his leading man looks and tall stature, standing at 1.96 meters (6 ft 5 in). He gained a reputation as a sex symbol through his roles in shows like 'Euphoria' and 'The Kissing Booth'. Elordi's relationships with high-profile celebrities have also contributed to his image as a modern-day heartthrob.
Related topics: Generation Z, Euphoria (American TV series), The Kissing Booth (film series)
Accolades
Has Jacob Elordi received any awards or nominations for his work?
Jacob Elordi has received several nominations for his work, including a BAFTA nomination for Best Actor in a Supporting Role for his performance in 'Saltburn'. He has also been nominated for AACTA Awards for his roles in 'Euphoria' and 'Saltburn'.
Related topics: BAFTA Award for Best Actor in a Supporting Role, AACTA Awards
Career
What was Jacob Elordi's approach to his role in 'Euphoria', and how was it received?
Jacob Elordi played Nate Jacobs, a portrayal he described as a narcissistic and sociopathic high school football player. Critics saw his role as Nate Jacobs in 'Euphoria' as an impressive career pivot and a breakout role for Elordi. This role significantly contributed to his reputation as a serious actor beyond the teen romance genre.
