FAQs: Ryan Reynolds

Biography and Career
How did Ryan Reynolds start his acting career?
Ryan Reynolds began his acting career at the age of thirteen with a starring role as Billy Simpson in the Canadian teen soap opera "Hillside" (1991-1993). He later played the lead role in the sitcom "Two Guys and a Girl" from 1998 to 2001, which helped him transition into more prominent film roles.
Related topics: Hillside (TV series), Two Guys and a Girl
Biography and Career
What are some of Ryan Reynolds's most successful films?
Ryan Reynolds achieved significant commercial success with the "Deadpool" film series, starring in "Deadpool" (2016), "Deadpool 2" (2018), and "Deadpool & Wolverine" (2024). His portrayal of Deadpool broke several box office records and earned him critical acclaim and award nominations.
Related topics: Deadpool (film), Deadpool 2, Deadpool & Wolverine
Personal Life
Who are Ryan Reynolds's prominent relationships and family details?
Ryan Reynolds has been married to actress Blake Lively since 2012, with whom he has four children. Before Lively, he was married to actress Scarlett Johansson from 2008 to 2011 and was engaged to singer Alanis Morissette.
Related topics: Blake Lively, Scarlett Johansson, Alanis Morissette
Business Ventures
What business ventures has Ryan Reynolds undertaken?
Ryan Reynolds is a successful businessman with stakes in several companies, including a significant ownership in Mint Mobile and past ownership of Aviation American Gin, which he sold to Diageo for $610 million. He is also a co-owner of the Welsh football club Wrexham A.F.C., alongside actor Rob McElhenney.
Related topics: Mint Mobile, Aviation American Gin, Wrexham A.F.C., Rob McElhenney
Public Image and Recognition
What awards has Ryan Reynolds won for his work?
Over his career, Ryan Reynolds has received several awards, including the Saturn Award for Best Actor and the People's Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actor. His role in "Deadpool" earned a Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actor in a Comedy.
Related topics: Saturn Award, People's Choice Award, Critics' Choice Movie Award for Best Actor in a Comedy
