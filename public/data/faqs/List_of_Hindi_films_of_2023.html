Which were the highest-grossing Hindi films of 2023?
The highest-grossing Hindi films of 2023 included 'Jawan', 'Pathaan', 'Animal', 'Gadar 2', and 'Dunki'. 'Jawan' topped the list with worldwide grosses ranging from ₹1,148.32 to ₹1,159 crore. 'Pathaan' followed with collections between ₹1,050.30 and ₹1,052.50 crore. Other notable films include 'Animal' with ₹917.82 crore, 'Gadar 2' with ₹687 to ₹691.08 crore, and 'Dunki' with ₹470.6 crore.
