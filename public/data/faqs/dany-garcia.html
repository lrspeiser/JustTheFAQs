FAQs: Dany Garcia

Early Life and Education
Where was Dany Garcia born, and what is her educational background?
Dany Garcia was born in Belleville, New Jersey, to Cuban immigrants. She grew up in New Jersey and has one brother, Hiram, and one sister. Garcia majored in international marketing and finance at the University of Miami, earning a Bachelor of Business Administration degree from the Miami Herbert Business School in 1992. After graduation, she worked at Merrill Lynch and eventually started her own wealth management firm, JDM Partners LLC.
Related topics: Belleville, New Jersey, Hiram Garcia, University of Miami, Miami Herbert Business School, Merrill Lynch
Career
How did Dany Garcia transition from finance to film production?
After working at Merrill Lynch, Dany Garcia founded her own wealth management firm, JDM Partners LLC, in 2002. In 2008, she transitioned from finance into managing her ex-husband Dwayne Johnson's career, becoming his global strategic advisor. In 2012, she co-founded Seven Bucks Productions with Johnson, producing several successful films and expanding into reality TV and docu-series projects, such as 'Finding Justice' and 'The Titan Games'.
Related topics: Merrill Lynch, Dwayne Johnson, Seven Bucks Productions, Reality television, Docu-series
Blockbuster Success: 2017–2019
What are some notable films produced by Dany Garcia during her blockbuster success phase?
During 2017-2019, Dany Garcia produced films like 'Baywatch' (2017), 'Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle' (2017), 'Rampage' (2018), and 'Skyscraper' (2018). Despite mixed critical responses, these films were commercially successful, with 'Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle' grossing $962.1 million worldwide. Garcia also produced 'Shazam!' (2019) and 'Hobbs & Shaw' (2019), expanding her portfolio in high-grossing action and fantasy films.
Related topics: Baywatch (film), Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, Rampage (2018 film), Skyscraper (2018 film), Shazam! (film)
Business Expansion: 2020–Present
How did Dany Garcia make history with her involvement in professional sports leagues?
In August 2020, Dany Garcia and Dwayne Johnson led a consortium to purchase the XFL. She became the first woman to own an equal or majority ownership stake in a major professional sports league in the United States. Under her leadership, the XFL relaunched in 2023 and later merged with the Fox Corporation-owned USFL to form the United Football League in 2023, with Garcia retaining partial ownership.
Related topics: XFL (2020), United Football League (2024), Professional sports leagues in the United States
Personal Life
What is Dany Garcia's connection with Dwayne Johnson beyond their professional collaboration?
Dany Garcia was married to Dwayne Johnson from 1997 until their amicable divorce in 2008. They have one daughter, Simone Johnson, a professional wrestler. Garcia remains Johnson's global strategic advisor and continues to collaborate with him professionally through her ventures, including Seven Bucks Productions.
Related topics: Dwayne Johnson, Simone Johnson, Seven Bucks Productions
Interests and Hobbies
How does bodybuilding influence Dany Garcia's business pursuits?
Dany Garcia began bodybuilding in 2011 and credits the sport with shaping her relentless business approach. She earned her International Fitness and Bodybuilding Federation pro card in 2014 and was the first-ever Women's Physique Division athlete signed to the Weider roster, underscoring her discipline and determination, which translate into her business endeavors.
Related topics: Bodybuilder, International Fitness and Bodybuilding Federation
