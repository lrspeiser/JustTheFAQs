FAQs: Vikrant Massey

Introduction
Who is Vikrant Massey?
Vikrant Massey is an Indian actor known for his work in Hindi films and television. He is celebrated for his versatility and has received a Filmfare Award for his performances. Born on April 3, 1988, in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, Vikrant began his career in television and ventured into films with prominent roles. His education was completed at R. D. National College in Mumbai.
Related topics: Filmfare Awards, R. D. National College
Early Life
What is Vikrant Massey's family background?
Vikrant Massey was born to a Sikh mother and a Christian father, Meena and Jolly Massey. He has an older brother who converted to Islam at the age of 17. Despite his diverse religious background, Vikrant identifies as non-religious, believing that religion is man-made. His parents were childhood sweethearts who eloped. Raised in Mumbai, he emerged from a middle-class Christian family originally from Shimla, Himachal Pradesh.
Related topics: Mumbai, Middle class, Shimla, Himachal Pradesh
Career
What are some notable television roles played by Vikrant Massey?
Vikrant Massey started his television career in 2007 with 'Dhoom Machaao Dhoom.' He became widely known for his roles in 'Dharam Veer' as Prince Dharam, 'Balika Vadhu' as Shyam Singh, and 'Qubool Hai' as Ayaan Ahmed Khan. He later hosted 'V The Serial' and 'Yeh Hai Aashiqui,' with 'Ajab Gajab Ghar Jamai' marking his final appearance on television before fully transitioning to films.
Related topics: Dhoom Machaao Dhoom, Dharam Veer (TV series), Balika Vadhu, Qubool Hai
Career
What are some notable film roles of Vikrant Massey?
Vikrant Massey debuted in Hindi films with 'Lootera' in 2013. His career took a significant turn with his lead role in 'A Death in the Gunj' (2017), earning him critical acclaim and a Filmfare Critics Award nomination. Other notable films include 'Dil Dhadakne Do', 'Chhapaak', and '12th Fail', where he played Manoj Kumar Sharma, which became a sleeper hit earning him the Filmfare Critics Award for Best Actor.
Related topics: Lootera, A Death in the Gunj, Dil Dhadakne Do, Chhapaak, 12th Fail
Personal Life
Who is Vikrant Massey married to?
Vikrant Massey is married to actress Sheetal Thakur. They began dating in 2015, became engaged in November 2019, and were married in a traditional ceremony in Himachal Pradesh in February 2022. The couple has one child together.
Related topics: Sheetal Thakur, Himachal Pradesh
Artistry and Media Image
How is Vikrant Massey perceived in the media?
Vikrant Massey is highly regarded in the media for consistently delivering impressive performances and choosing unconventional roles that resonate with audiences. He is considered one of the finest actors of his generation, earning praise from other filmmakers and audiences alike for his ability to portray relatable characters.
