Welcome to Wikipedia
What is Wikipedia?
Wikipedia is the free encyclopedia that anyone can edit. It allows users to contribute and create articles, aiming to provide reliable information on a vast range of topics. Currently, Wikipedia has over 6 million articles in English and around 116,430 active editors globally.

Wikipedia's Sister Projects
What are some of Wikipedia's sister projects?
Wikipedia's sister projects include Wikimedia Commons (a free media repository), Wikibooks (free textbooks), Wikidata (a free knowledge base), and Wikinews (free-content news). These projects extend the reach of information sharing beyond traditional articles.
Related topics: Commons, Wikibooks, Wikidata, Wikinews
