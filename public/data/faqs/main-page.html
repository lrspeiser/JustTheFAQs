What are the Albona-class minelayers?
The Albona-class minelayers were mine-warfare ships used by the Italian Regia Marina and the Royal Yugoslav Navy. Originally laid down for the Austro-Hungarian Navy, these ships were completed post-World War I and used in both Italian and Yugoslav naval services.
Related topics: Albona-class minelayer, Minelayer, Regia Marina, Royal Yugoslav Navy
Did You Know...
Who is Saint Amalberga and why is she significant?
Saint Amalberga of Temse is the patron saint of upper-limb injuries due to a legend where Charlemagne broke her arm when she refused to marry him.
Related topics: Amalberga of Temse, Charlemagne

What is the Hugo Award for Best Professional Editor?
The Hugo Award for Best Professional Editor is granted annually for editorial work in science fiction or fantasy. Established in 1973, it was split into Best Editor (Short Form) and Best Editor (Long Form) in 2007. Gardner Dozois has won it a record 15 times.
Related topics: Hugo Award for Best Professional Editor, Gardner Dozois

What is unique about the KiMo Theater in Albuquerque?
The KiMo Theater in Albuquerque is noted for its Pueblo Deco architecture, a distinctive blend of Pueblo Revival and Art Deco styles. Allegedly haunted, the theater's name, suggested by Pablo Abeita, means 'mountain lion.'
Related topics: KiMo Theater, Albuquerque, New Mexico
