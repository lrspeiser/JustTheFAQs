FAQs: Von Erich family

Members
What tragedies have affected the Von Erich family?
The Von Erich family is infamously known for a series of tragedies, leading to the myth of a 'family curse.' Fritz Von Erich's five sons (Jack Jr., David, Mike, Chris, and Kerry) died before him, with the causes ranging from accidents to suicides. These tragic events have overshadowed their wrestling legacy, making their story well-known beyond the wrestling community.
Related topics: Family curse, Jack Jr., David Von Erich, Mike Von Erich, Chris Von Erich, Kerry Von Erich

Who was Fritz Von Erich and what was his impact on wrestling?
Fritz Von Erich, born Jack Adkisson, was the patriarch of the Von Erich wrestling family. Trained by Stu Hart, Fritz became a top star in many National Wrestling Alliance promotions and also served as the president of WCCW and briefly as NWA President. He was influential in Japanese wrestling as well, earning the nickname "The Iron Claw." His family became synonymous with wrestling throughout the 1970s and 1980s.
Related topics: Fritz Von Erich, National Wrestling Alliance
WWE Hall of Fame
When was the Von Erich family inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame?
The Von Erich family, known for their legacy and tragic history, was collectively inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame in 2009. This honor commemorated the contributions of Fritz and his sons to the world of professional wrestling.
Related topics: WWE Hall of Fame
Overview
Who are the Von Erich family and why are they famous?
The Von Erich family is an American professional wrestling family originally from Texas, known for their significant contributions to the wrestling industry and their tragic personal history. All members of the family used the ring name "Von Erich" after Fritz Von Erich, the patriarch. Fritz and his sons became famous as wrestlers, but the family's legacy is also marked by numerous untimely deaths, often referred to as the "Von Erich curse."
Related topics: Fritz Von Erich, Professional wrestling
Third Generation
Who is carrying on the Von Erich wrestling legacy today?
The Von Erich wrestling legacy continues with Kevin's sons, Ross and Marshall, who perform as a tag team called "The Von Erichs." They debuted in Pro Wrestling Noah and later appeared in major wrestling promotions like TNA and MLW. Kerry's daughter, Lacey, also wrestled for TNA before her retirement.
Related topics: Marshall and Ross Von Erich, Lacey Von Erich, Major League Wrestling

What is Kevin Von Erich known for in his wrestling career?
Kevin Von Erich, the last surviving son of Fritz Von Erich, was known as "The Golden Warrior" in the wrestling world. His career was mainly tied to World Class Championship Wrestling, where he faced notable opponents like The Fabulous Freebirds and Ric Flair. Unique for wrestling barefoot, Kevin retired in 1995 and remains a respected figure in wrestling.
Related topics: Kevin Von Erich, World Class Championship Wrestling
Fictional Relatives
Who are the fictional relatives associated with the Von Erich family?
Several characters have been billed as Von Erich relatives, including Waldo, Lance, Rip, and Mark Von Erich. These were mainly storylines or ring names with no actual familial connection to the Adkisson family (the real surname of the Von Erichs).
Related topics: Waldo Von Erich, Lance Von Erich
