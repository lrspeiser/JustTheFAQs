FAQs: Arbaaz Khan

Introduction
Who is Arbaaz Khan?
Arbaaz Salim Abdul Rashid Khan, born on 4 August 1967, is a prominent Indian actor and film producer associated with the Bollywood film industry. Khan has also worked in Telugu, Urdu, and Malayalam cinema. He is best known for his acting debut in 1996 and for launching the blockbuster film series 'Dabangg' through his production company, Arbaaz Khan Productions.
Related topics: Indian actor, Film producer, Bollywood, Telugu cinema, Malayalam cinema
Early life and family
Who are Arbaaz Khan's family members in the Bollywood industry?
Arbaaz Khan hails from a family with strong Bollywood connections. His father, Salim Khan, is a renowned screenwriter, and his stepmother, Helen, is a legendary Bollywood actress and dancer. His brothers are Salman Khan, a superstar actor, and Sohail Khan, an actor-producer. He also has a sister, Alvira Khan Agnihotri, and an adopted sister, Arpita Khan. His brother-in-laws include actors Atul Agnihotri and Aayush Sharma.
Related topics: Salim Khan, Helen (actress), Salman Khan, Sohail Khan, Alvira Khan Agnihotri
Career
What was Arbaaz Khan's first successful film as an actor and producer?
Arbaaz Khan's successful debut as an actor came with the film 'Daraar' (1996), for which he won the Filmfare Award for Best Performance in a Negative Role. As a producer, Khan achieved success with his first production 'Dabangg' in 2010, starring his brother Salman Khan. The film became one of the highest-grossing Bollywood films of its time.
Related topics: Daraar (film), Filmfare Best Villain Award, Dabangg
Personal life
Who has Arbaaz Khan been married to?
Arbaaz Khan was first married to model and actress Malaika Arora from 1998 until their divorce in 2017. The couple has a son named Arhaan Khan, born in 2002. In 2023, Arbaaz married make-up artist Shura Khan.
Related topics: Malaika Arora
Controversies
Has Arbaaz Khan been involved in any controversies?
Yes, Arbaaz Khan has been involved in several controversies. In 2012, his family's refusal to pay compensation for an accident involving his car attracted attention. Furthermore, in 2018, Khan confessed to betting on IPL cricket matches. He and his family were also accused by director Abhinav Kashyap of sabotaging his career, though Khan mentioned they were pursuing legal action.
Related topics: Abhinav Kashyap, Besharam (2013 film)
Awards and honors
What are some of the awards that Arbaaz Khan has won?
Arbaaz Khan has received several awards, including the Filmfare Award for Best Villain for 'Daraar' in 1997 and the National Film Award for Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment for 'Dabangg' in 2011. 'Dabangg' also won the Filmfare Award for Best Film and the IIFA Award for Best Film.
Related topics: Filmfare Best Villain Award, National Film Award for Best Popular Film Providing Wholesome Entertainment
