FAQs: YouTube

Acquisition by Google
When did Google acquire YouTube and for how much?
Google acquired YouTube on October 9, 2006, for $1.65 billion. The acquisition facilitated the expansion of YouTube's business model and integrated it with Google's AdSense program to increase revenue.
Related topics: Google AdSense, Advertising
Monetization and Revenue
How does YouTube generate revenue?
YouTube generates revenue primarily through advertisements, including Google's AdSense program. It offers a paid subscription service called YouTube Premium, which allows for ad-free viewing. YouTube's revenue also includes earnings from content subscriptions, YouTube Music, and YouTube TV.
Related topics: Google AdSense, YouTube Premium, YouTube Music, YouTube TV
Growth and Popularity
How popular is YouTube and what are some usage statistics?
YouTube is the second-most-visited website globally, after Google Search. In January 2024, it had over 2.7 billion monthly active users. Users collectively watch more than one billion hours of videos daily. Videos were uploaded at a rate of more than 500 hours per minute as of 2019.
Related topics: Google Search, Monthly active user, Content (media)
Controversies and Criticism
What are some criticisms and controversies faced by YouTube?
YouTube has been criticized for spreading misinformation, violating privacy, infringing on copyrights, excessive censorship, and inconsistent guideline enforcement. The platform has faced scrutiny over its impact on children, and has been involved in legal issues regarding content moderation.
Related topics: Misinformation, YouTube and privacy, YouTube copyright issues, Censorship by YouTube, Child protection
Introduction to YouTube
What is YouTube and who are its founders?
YouTube is an American social media and online video sharing platform owned by Google. It was founded on February 14, 2005, by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karim, who were former employees of PayPal.
Related topics: Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, Jawed Karim, PayPal, Google
Impact and Influence
What is the social impact of YouTube?
YouTube has had significant social impact by influencing popular culture, creating internet trends, and making celebrities out of YouTubers. It is a platform for user-generated content, educational resources, and news dissemination, changing how people interact with media and information.
Related topics: Social impact of YouTube, YouTuber, User-generated content
Features and Updates
What features and updates has YouTube introduced over the years?
YouTube has introduced features such as live streaming, custom thumbnails, and YouTube Shorts for short-form content. It has evolved its UI to enhance user experience and added new products like YouTube Kids for safer content access for children.
Related topics: YouTube Shorts, YouTube Kids
