FAQs: Dak Prescott

Early Life and Background
Where was Dak Prescott born, and what is known about his early life?
Dak Prescott was born in Sulphur, Louisiana, and is the youngest of three sons. His parents divorced when he was in elementary school, and he was primarily raised by his mother, Peggy Prescott, who worked as a manager at a truck stop. Prescott attended Haughton High School in Haughton, Louisiana, where he excelled in football, leading his team to championships and eventually earning a scholarship to Mississippi State.
Related topics: Haughton High School, Sulphur, Louisiana
College Career
What notable achievements did Dak Prescott accomplish during his college career?
During his college career at Mississippi State, Dak Prescott shattered 38 school records and received numerous accolades. Notably, in 2014, he led the Bulldogs to their first #1 ranking in program history and set records for single-season passing yards and total touchdowns. He was also named MVP of the 2013 Liberty Bowl and was a two-time winner of the Conerly Trophy.
Related topics: Mississippi State Bulldogs football, 2014 Orange Bowl (December)
NFL Career Beginnings and Achievements
How did Dak Prescott start his career in the NFL, and what were his initial achievements?
Dak Prescott was drafted by the Dallas Cowboys in the 4th round of the 2016 NFL Draft. Originally intended as a backup, he became the starting quarterback after Tony Romo suffered an injury. Prescott had a standout rookie season, setting multiple rookie records, including the highest passer rating by a rookie quarterback. He led the Cowboys to a 13-3 record and was named the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year.
Related topics: Dallas Cowboys, Quarterback, 2016 NFL draft
Professional Highlights
What have been some career highlights and records set by Dak Prescott in the NFL?
Dak Prescott has been recognized for several achievements throughout his career. He has been selected for the Pro Bowl three times and was named the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year in 2016. Prescott holds several NFL rookie records such as the highest completion percentage and passer rating. He also led the NFL in passing touchdowns in 2023 and holds multiple franchise records with the Dallas Cowboys.
Related topics: Pro Bowl, NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year
Recent Seasons
How has Dak Prescott performed in recent NFL seasons?
In recent seasons, Dak Prescott has continued to be a key player for the Cowboys. In the 2023 season, he led the league with 36 passing touchdowns. In 2024, Prescott signed a record-breaking contract extension with the Cowboys. However, his season was cut short due to injury. Despite these challenges, Prescott maintains a strong position in the NFL's statistical rankings.

Personal Life
What are some personal aspects of Dak Prescott's life outside of football?
Outside of football, Dak Prescott is known as a Christian and has been active in charitable endeavors, establishing the Faith Fight Finish Foundation in honor of his mother, who died of colon cancer in 2013. Prescott's personal life faced tragedies, including the loss of his brother, Jace, in 2020. He resides in Frisco, Texas, and is expecting a baby girl with his partner, Sarah Jane Ramos.
