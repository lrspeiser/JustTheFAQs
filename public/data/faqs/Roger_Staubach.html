FAQs: Roger Staubach

Professional Football Career
Which NFL team did Roger Staubach play for and what were his notable achievements?
Roger Staubach played for the Dallas Cowboys throughout his entire NFL career from 1969 to 1979. He led the team to five Super Bowls, winning two as the starting quarterback, and was named Super Bowl MVP in Super Bowl VI. Staubach was a six-time Pro Bowl selection and led the NFL in passer ratings four times.

Post-Retirement
What did Roger Staubach do after retiring from football?
After retiring from professional football, Roger Staubach founded The Staubach Company, a successful commercial real estate business, which he sold to Jones Lang LaSalle for $613 million in 2008. He remained active in business and philanthropy, serving as executive chairman of Jones Lang LaSalle until 2018.

Early Life and Education
Where did Roger Staubach grow up?
Roger Staubach grew up in Silverton, a northeastern suburb of Cincinnati, Ohio. He was an only child and participated in the Boy Scouts as a youth. He attended St. John the Evangelist Catholic School and graduated from Purcell High School in Cincinnati in 1960.

Legacy and Accolades
What legacy did Roger Staubach leave in football?
Roger Staubach is remembered for his incredible career as a quarterback, renowned for his fourth-quarter comebacks and game-winning drives. Beyond his records and awards, including his six Pro Bowl selections and two Super Bowl wins, Staubach's legacy is cemented with his induction into the Pro Football Hall of Fame and his award of the Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2018.
Related topics: Pro Football Hall of Fame, Presidential Medal of Freedom
General Overview
Who is Roger Staubach?
Roger Thomas Staubach, born on February 5, 1942, in Cincinnati, Ohio, is a former professional American football quarterback and a retired Navy officer. He played 11 seasons for the Dallas Cowboys in the NFL and is regarded as one of the greatest quarterbacks of all time. Staubach is known for his scrambling and passing abilities, earning nicknames such as "Roger the Dodger," "Captain America," and "Captain Comeback."
