General Information
What is the purpose of the 'Current events' portal on Wikipedia?
The 'Current events' portal on Wikipedia is intended to provide readers with access to news stories related to ongoing and recent global events. It serves as a resource for quickly locating information about what's happening in the world, along with providing cross-links to related Wikipedia pages for more in-depth coverage.
