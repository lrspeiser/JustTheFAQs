FAQs: Alia Bhatt

Philanthropy
What philanthropic initiatives is Alia Bhatt involved in?
Alia Bhatt supports various causes, such as PETA and the welfare of street animals through her ecological initiative, CoExist. She also launched Mi Wardrobe is Su Wardrobe to auction her clothes for charitable causes. Additionally, she collaborates with organizations like ActionAid to aid communities affected by COVID-19.
Related topics: PETA, CoExist, ActionAid
Early Life and Background
What is Alia Bhatt's family background?
Alia Bhatt was born into the Bhatt family on 15 March 1993 in Bombay (now Mumbai), India. She is the daughter of Indian filmmaker Mahesh Bhatt and British actress Soni Razdan. Her father is of Gujarati descent, and her mother has Kashmiri-Pandit and German-British ancestry. Alia holds British citizenship and has an elder sister, Shaheen, and two half-siblings, Pooja and Rahul Bhatt.
Related topics: Mahesh Bhatt, Soni Razdan
Career Beginnings
How did Alia Bhatt start her acting career?
Alia Bhatt began her acting journey as a child in the 1999 thriller film 'Sangharsh' where she played the younger version of Preity Zinta's character. She got her first leading role in 2012 with Karan Johar's 'Student of the Year'.
Related topics: Sangharsh (1999 film), Student of the Year
Accolades
What significant awards has Alia Bhatt won?
Alia Bhatt has won the National Film Award for Best Actress for her role in 'Gangubai Kathiawadi'. She also holds the record-tying five Filmfare Awards for Best Actress for her performances in 'Udta Punjab', 'Raazi', 'Gully Boy', 'Gangubai Kathiawadi', and 'Rocky Aur Rani Kii Prem Kahaani'.
Related topics: National Film Award for Best Actress, Filmfare Awards
Major Works
What are some of Alia Bhatt's most acclaimed performances?
Alia Bhatt won Filmfare Awards for Best Actress for her roles in 'Udta Punjab' (2016), 'Raazi' (2018), 'Gully Boy' (2019), and 'Gangubai Kathiawadi' (2022). Her portrayal as the title character in 'Gangubai Kathiawadi' earned her critical acclaim and a National Film Award for Best Actress.
Related topics: Udta Punjab, Raazi, Gully Boy, Gangubai Kathiawadi
Established Actress (2022–Present)
How did Alia Bhatt further establish her career from 2022?
In 2022, Alia Bhatt starred in 'Gangubai Kathiawadi', earning her a National Film Award for Best Actress. She also expanded into film production with 'Darlings' and achieved her biggest commercial success with 'Brahmāstra: Part One – Shiva'. Additionally, Bhatt ventured into international cinema with 'Heart of Stone', marking her debut in Hollywood alongside Gal Gadot.
Related topics: Gangubai Kathiawadi, Brahmāstra: Part One – Shiva, Darlings (film), Heart of Stone (2023 film)
Introduction
Who is Alia Bhatt?
Alia Bhatt is a British actress of Indian descent, known for her work in Hindi films. She is recognized for her portrayals of women in challenging situations and has won numerous accolades, including a National Film Award and six Filmfare Awards. Bhatt is one of India's highest-paid actresses and was named one of Time magazine's 100 most influential people in the world in 2024.

Career
What are some of Alia Bhatt's notable film roles?
Alia Bhatt's notable film roles include playing a victim of drug abuse in 'Udta Punjab' (2016), an undercover spy in 'Raazi' (2018), a possessive girlfriend in 'Gully Boy' (2019), and the title role of a prostitute in 'Gangubai Kathiawadi' (2022). Her performances in these films have earned her several prestigious awards.
Related topics: Udta Punjab, Raazi, Gully Boy, Gangubai Kathiawadi, Alia Bhatt filmography
Entrepreneurship
What business ventures has Alia Bhatt embarked on?
In 2014, Alia Bhatt designed her own clothing line and later launched a handbag line. She co-founded the sustainable clothing brand Ed-a-Mamma, which was awarded PETA's Vegan Fashion Award for Best Vegan Kidswear Brand. The brand was recently valued at ₹1.5 billion with Reliance Retail acquiring a majority stake.
Related topics: Sustainable fashion, PETA India, Reliance Retail
Personal Life
Who is Alia Bhatt married to and where does she live?
Alia Bhatt is married to actor Ranbir Kapoor. The couple got married on 14 April 2022 in a traditional Hindu ceremony. Bhatt primarily lives in Mumbai and also has a home in London. The couple shares a daughter named Raha, born on 6 November 2022.
Related topics: Ranbir Kapoor, Mumbai
