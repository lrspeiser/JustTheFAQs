FAQs: 12th Fail

Release
When was '12th Fail' released and in what languages?
The film was released theatrically on October 27, 2023, in Hindi and Kannada. Due to its success, it was also dubbed in Tamil and Telugu, with releases in these languages on November 3, 2023.
Related topics: Hindi cinema, Kannada cinema
Production
How was '12th Fail' developed and filmed?
'12th Fail' was announced in November 2022 by filmmaker Vidhu Vinod Chopra, inspired by the real-life story of IPS Officer Manoj Kumar Sharma. The filming took place in locations such as Agra, Chambal, Delhi, and Mumbai, with much of the shooting done in education hubs like Rajendra Nagar and Mukherjee Nagar. Filming wrapped up in December 2022.
Related topics: Vidhu Vinod Chopra, Agra, Chambal (region)
Soundtrack
Who composed the music for '12th Fail'?
The film's music was composed by Shantanu Moitra with lyrics by Swanand Kirkire and Raftaar. The background score was inspired by Pandit Ravi Shankar's work in the Bengali film 'Pather Panchali'.
Related topics: Shantanu Moitra, Pandit Ravi Shankar, Pather Panchali
Cast and Characters
Who are the primary actors in '12th Fail'?
The film features Vikrant Massey as Manoj Kumar Sharma, Medha Shankar as Shraddha Joshi, Anant V Joshi as Pritam Pandey, and Priyanshu Chatterjee as DSP Dushyant Singh. Notably, Vikrant Massey received praise for his performance in the lead role.
Related topics: Vikrant Massey, Medha Shankar, Anant V Joshi, Priyanshu Chatterjee
Reception
How was '12th Fail' received by critics?
'12th Fail' received widespread critical acclaim for its authentic storytelling and inspiring narrative. It earned a 91% rating on Rotten Tomatoes and was praised for its portrayal of perseverance against adversity. The film won five awards at the 69th Filmfare Awards, including Best Film and Best Director for Vidhu Vinod Chopra.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Filmfare Awards
Overview
What is '12th Fail'?
'12th Fail' is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language biographical drama film directed, produced, and written by Vidhu Vinod Chopra. It is based on the 2019 non-fiction book by Anurag Pathak about Manoj Kumar Sharma, who overcame poverty to become an Indian Police Service officer. The film stars Vikrant Massey and was released on October 27, 2023, receiving critical acclaim and achieving commercial success.
Plot
What is the plot of the movie '12th Fail'?
The film follows the story of Manoj Kumar Sharma, a son of a clerk from Chambal, who faces extreme poverty and systemic corruption. Despite these challenges, he manages to pass his 12th exams honestly and pursues a path to become a Deputy Superintendent of Police. The story unfolds his journey through setbacks and perseverance, ultimately achieving his dream of becoming an Indian Police Service officer.
Related topics: Manoj Kumar Sharma, Indian Police Service, Chambal (region)
