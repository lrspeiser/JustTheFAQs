FAQs: Jerry Jones

Introduction
Who is Jerry Jones?
Jerral Wayne Jones Sr. is an American businessman known for being the owner, president, and general manager of the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League (NFL). He became the owner after purchasing the team in 1989, and since then, he has been a pivotal figure in the team's success and the NFL's growth.

Early Life
Where did Jerry Jones grow up?
Jerry Jones was born in Los Angeles, California, but he moved to North Little Rock, Arkansas, in 1945. His early life was shaped by his family's business ventures and relocations to other cities like Springfield, Missouri, where his father managed a successful insurance company.

College Football Career
What was Jerry Jones' role in the 1964 Arkansas Razorbacks football team?
Jerry Jones was a co-captain of the 1964 Arkansas Razorbacks football team, which won the national championship that year. He played as an offensive lineman under the guidance of College Football Hall of Fame coach Frank Broyles.

Business Ventures
How did Jerry Jones start his business career?
After college, Jerry Jones borrowed money to open a string of Shakey's Pizza Parlor restaurants in Missouri, though this venture failed. He later joined his father's insurance company, began a successful oil and gas exploration business, and eventually co-founded Legends Hospitality.
Related topics: Shakey's Pizza Parlor, Legends Hospitality
Dallas Cowboys Ownership
What significant changes did Jerry Jones make to the Dallas Cowboys after purchasing the team?
Jerry Jones purchased the Dallas Cowboys in 1989 and immediately made significant changes, including firing legendary coach Tom Landry and hiring Jimmy Johnson as the new head coach. His leadership led the Cowboys to win three Super Bowl titles and significantly increased the team's financial value.
Related topics: Dallas Cowboys, Tom Landry, Super Bowl
Criticism
What controversies has Jerry Jones faced as the Dallas Cowboys owner?
Jerry Jones has faced criticism for being highly visible and making contentious decisions, such as firing popular team personnel like Tom Landry and Jimmy Johnson. His hands-on management style as the owner and general manager, as well as his involvement in NFL team relocations, have often sparked debate.
Related topics: Tom Landry, Jimmy Johnson (American football coach)
Jones in Popular Culture
In what forms of popular culture has Jerry Jones appeared?
Jerry Jones has appeared in several forms of popular culture, including as the inspiration for a character in the film 'BASEketball' and in cameo roles in TV movies like 'Dallas: War of the Ewings'. He has also appeared in advertising campaigns alongside sports stars such as Deion Sanders.

Awards and Honors
What are some of Jerry Jones' notable achievements and recognitions?
Jerry Jones is a three-time Super Bowl champion as the owner of the Dallas Cowboys, and he was named the 2014 NFL Executive of the Year. He was also inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2017, among other awards.
Related topics: Super Bowl, Pro Football Hall of Fame
Personal Life
What is known about Jerry Jones' family?
Jerry Jones married Eugenia Chambers in 1963, and they have three children who are involved in the Dallas Cowboys organization. His son, Stephen Jones, serves as the Cowboys' chief operating officer, while his daughter, Charlotte Jones Anderson, is the executive vice president and chief brand officer.
