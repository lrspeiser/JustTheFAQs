FAQs: New Year's Eve (2011 film)

General Information
What is 'New Year's Eve' (2011 film) about?
'New Year's Eve' is a 2011 American romantic comedy film directed by Garry Marshall. It follows multiple characters and their experiences during New Year's Eve in New York City. The film features intertwined stories of love, hope, forgiveness, second chances, and fresh starts in the hectic hours leading up to the New Year's Day.
Related topics: Romantic comedy, Garry Marshall
Cast and Characters
Who are some of the key actors in the film 'New Year's Eve'?
The film boasts an ensemble cast including Halle Berry, Jessica Biel, Jon Bon Jovi, Abigail Breslin, Ludacris, Robert De Niro, Josh Duhamel, Zac Efron, Katherine Heigl, Ashton Kutcher, Sarah Jessica Parker, and Sofia Vergara, among others. Each actor portrays different characters whose lives intersect over the course of the evening in New York City.
Related topics: Halle Berry, Robert De Niro
Plot Details
What are the main events that happen on New Year's Eve in the film?
The film presents multiple interconnected stories: Claire Morgan is handling the Times Square ball drop; Ingrid Withers completes her New Year's resolutions with help from Paul Doyle; Griffin and Tess Byrne compete to be the first parents of the New Year; and Sam Ahern, Jr. attempts to reconnect with a past love. As chaos unfolds, stories of love and redemption emerge against the backdrop of a glittering New York City's New Year's Eve.
Related topics: Times Square, New York City
Production
Who directed 'New Year's Eve' and what is its production background?
'New Year's Eve' was directed by Garry Marshall. It was produced by New Line Cinema, Wayne Rice Films, and Karz Entertainment. The screenplay was written by Katherine Fugate, and it is considered part of an unofficial trilogy of romantic comedies directed by Garry Marshall, alongside 'Valentine's Day' (2010) and 'Mother's Day' (2016).
Related topics: Garry Marshall
Reception
How was 'New Year's Eve' received by critics and audiences?
The film received generally negative reviews, holding a 7% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a Metacritic score of 22, indicating "generally unfavorable reviews." Critics like Roger Ebert and various publications harshly criticized the film, while CinemaScore audiences gave it a 'B+'. Despite critical reviews, it grossed $142 million worldwide.
Related topics: Roger Ebert, Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic
Box Office Performance
How did 'New Year's Eve' perform financially?
Despite poor critical reception, 'New Year's Eve' topped the box office with an opening weekend of $13 million. It grossed $54.5 million in the United States and Canada, and $87.5 million internationally, totaling $142 million worldwide.
Related topics: Box Office Mojo
Accolades
Did 'New Year's Eve' receive any award nominations?
The film was nominated for five Golden Raspberry Awards, including Worst Picture, Worst Director for Garry Marshall, Worst Actress for Sarah Jessica Parker, Worst Screenplay, and Worst Screen Ensemble. It won the Alliance of Women Film Journalists award for 'Actress Most in Need of a New Agent'.
Related topics: Golden Raspberry Award, Sarah Jessica Parker
