FAQs: Wish (film)

Music and Soundtrack
Who created the music for 'Wish'?
The music for 'Wish' was composed by Dave Metzger with songs written by Julia Michaels and Benjamin Rice. The score also pays homage to classic Disney music like 'When You Wish Upon a Star.'

Plot Details
Who is the main antagonist in 'Wish'?
The main antagonist in 'Wish' is King Magnifico, the ruler of the kingdom of Rosas, who keeps his subjects' wishes sealed away, granting them one by one and using them to maintain control over the kingdom.
Related topics: King Magnifico
General Overview
What is the 2023 film 'Wish' about?
'Wish' is a 2023 American animated musical fantasy film produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios. The story follows a 17-year-old girl named Asha, who makes a wish upon a star in a moment of need. This leads her to meet a living, magical fallen star, and together they confront the ruler of the kingdom of Rosas, the sorcerer King Magnifico.
Related topics: Asha, King Magnifico
Box Office Performance
What was the box office performance of 'Wish'?
'Wish' grossed approximately $255 million worldwide on a production budget of $175–200 million but was considered a box-office bomb due to its inability to recoup the anticipated returns, resulting in an estimated loss of $131 million for the studio.
Related topics: Box-office bomb
Plot Details
Who are the main characters in the film 'Wish'?
The main characters in 'Wish' include Asha, a 17-year-old girl voiced by Ariana DeBose, King Magnifico voiced by Chris Pine, Valentino the goat voiced by Alan Tudyk, and Queen Amaya voiced by Angelique Cabral. Supporting characters include Sabino, Asha's grandfather voiced by Victor Garber, and Asha's mother Sakina voiced by Natasha Rothwell.
Related topics: Asha (character), King Magnifico, Ariana DeBose, Chris Pine, Alan Tudyk
Box Office and Reception
How was 'Wish' received at the box office and by critics?
'Wish' grossed $255 million worldwide but was considered a box office bomb due to its high production costs. It got mixed reviews from critics, with a 48% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a score of 47 on Metacritic. Some critics praised it for its nostalgia and animation, while others felt it lacked genuine storytelling magic.

Development
How was 'Wish' developed?
The development of 'Wish' began in 2018, but it wasn't publicly revealed until January 2022. The film was officially announced in September 2022 with Jennfer Lee, Chris Buck, and Allison Moore playing key roles in its creation. The animation style is a mix of traditional and computer animation, and the film is noted as being an origin story for the 'wishing star.'

Production Insights
What inspired the production of 'Wish'?
The production of 'Wish' was inspired by Disney's centennial, with the film serving as an origin story for the iconic 'wishing star' that appears in many Disney films. The art style combines computer animation with the look of traditional animation.
Reception
How was 'Wish' received by audiences and critics?
'Wish' received mixed reviews, with a Rotten Tomatoes score of 48% and a Metacritic score of 47 out of 100. While some praised its nostalgic elements and voice performances, others felt it did not live up to storytelling expectations associated with Disney's legacy.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic
