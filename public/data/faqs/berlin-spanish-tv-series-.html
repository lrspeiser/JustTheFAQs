FAQs: Berlin (Spanish TV series)

General Information
What is the premise of the Spanish TV series 'Berlin'?
'Berlin' is a Spanish television series that serves as a prequel to 'Money Heist'. It focuses on the life of the character Andrés de Fonollosa, aka 'Berlin', before the events of seasons 1 and 2 of 'Money Heist'. The series showcases Berlin leading a criminal gang and executing a heist that involves stealing €44 million in jewels, while falling in love with the wife of someone he's planning to frame. The series is primarily set in Paris.
Related topics: Money Heist, Spanish language
Cast and Characters
Who are the main characters in the series 'Berlin'?
The main character of the series is Andrés de Fonollosa, or 'Berlin', portrayed by Pedro Alonso. Other significant characters include Keila played by Michelle Jenner, Damián portrayed by Tristán Ulloa, Cameron by Begoña Vargas, Bruce played by Joel Sánchez, Roi by Julio Peña Fernández, and Susi portrayed by Maria Isabel 'Masi' Rodríguez.
Related topics: Pedro Alonso, Michelle Jenner, Tristán Ulloa, Begoña Vargas, Julio Peña Fernández
Production Details
Who created the TV series 'Berlin' and what is its connection to 'Money Heist'?
The series 'Berlin' was created by Álex Pina and Esther Martínez Lobato. It is a spin-off and prequel of the popular series 'Money Heist', exploring the backstory of the character Berlin. The series gives insight into Berlin's life prior to the events of 'Money Heist', offering a different tone with more comedic elements.
Related topics: Álex Pina, Esther Martínez Lobato, Money Heist

Critical Reception
What were the critical reactions to the series 'Berlin'?
The series 'Berlin' received mixed reviews from critics. Some felt that the plot and new characters did not live up to the standard of the original 'Money Heist'. Critics like Noel Murray from 'The Daily Beast' noted the series needed more heist elements and less focus on romance.
Related topics: The Daily Beast
Future of the Series
Has 'Berlin' been renewed for another season?
Yes, 'Berlin' was renewed for a second season on February 19, 2024. Filming for the new season is set to begin in 2025.
