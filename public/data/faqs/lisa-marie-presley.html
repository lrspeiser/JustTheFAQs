FAQs: Lisa Marie Presley

General Information
Who were Lisa Marie Presley's parents?
Lisa Marie Presley was the only child of singer and actor Elvis Presley and actress Priscilla Presley.
Related topics: Elvis Presley, Priscilla Presley
Early Life
Where was Lisa Marie Presley born?
Lisa Marie Presley was born on February 1, 1968, at Baptist Memorial Hospital-Memphis in Memphis, Tennessee.
Related topics: Memphis, Tennessee, Baptist Memorial Hospital-Memphis
Musical Career
What were some of Lisa Marie Presley's musical achievements?
Lisa Marie Presley released three studio albums: 'To Whom It May Concern' (2003), 'Now What' (2005), and 'Storm & Grace' (2012). Her album 'To Whom It May Concern' was certified gold by the Recording Industry Association of America.
Related topics: To Whom It May Concern (Lisa Marie Presley album), Now What (Lisa Marie Presley album), Storm & Grace, Recording Industry Association of America
Personal Life
How many times was Lisa Marie Presley married?
Lisa Marie Presley was married four times. Her spouses were Danny Keough, Michael Jackson, Nicolas Cage, and Michael Lockwood.
Related topics: Danny Keough, Michael Jackson, Nicolas Cage, Michael Lockwood (guitarist)
Personal Life
Who are Lisa Marie Presley's children?
Lisa Marie Presley had four children, including Riley Keough. Her other three children are Benjamin Storm Keough, and fraternal twin girls Harper Vivienne Ann Lockwood and Finley Aaron Love Lockwood.
Related topics: Riley Keough
Legacy
What role did Lisa Marie Presley have in managing her father's estate?
Lisa Marie Presley inherited her father's estate at age 25. She later sold 85% of Elvis Presley's business holdings, excluding Graceland. Her children are expected to inherit Graceland.
Related topics: Elvis Presley
Death
What was the cause of Lisa Marie Presley's death?
Lisa Marie Presley died of cardiac arrest caused by a small bowel obstruction, reportedly related to prior bariatric surgery. The opioids in her system were not listed as a contributing factor to her death.

Relationships
Describe Lisa Marie Presley's relationship with Michael Jackson.
Lisa Marie Presley married Michael Jackson in 1994, only 20 days after her divorce from Danny Keough. Although they divorced in 1996, they attempted to reconcile over the next four years. Presley provided significant emotional support to Jackson during child molestation allegations against him.
