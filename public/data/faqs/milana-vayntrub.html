FAQs: Milana Vayntrub

Introduction
Who is Milana Vayntrub?
Milana Aleksandrovna Vayntrub is an American actress, comedian, and activist known for her prominent roles in AT&T television commercials as Lily Adams and her appearances in series like 'This Is Us' and 'Other Space.' Vayntrub has also voiced the character Squirrel Girl in the Marvel Rising franchise.
Related topics: AT&T, This Is Us, Other Space, Squirrel Girl, Marvel Rising
Early Life and Education
What is Milana Vayntrub's background and education?
Milana Vayntrub was born in Tashkent, Uzbekistan, in a secular Ashkenazi Jewish family and immigrated to the United States as a refugee from antisemitism. She settled in West Hollywood, California. Vayntrub began acting in commercials at a young age, dropped out of Beverly Hills High School, and earned a Bachelor of Arts in Communication from the University of California, San Diego. She also studied improv comedy with the Upright Citizens Brigade.
Related topics: Tashkent, Ashkenazi Jewish, University of California, San Diego, Upright Citizens Brigade
Career
What are some notable works in Milana Vayntrub's acting career?
Milana Vayntrub first appeared on television in the NBC series 'ER' in 1995. She gained fame portraying Lily Adams in AT&T commercials, was part of the Yahoo! Screen series 'Other Space,' and played Sloane Sandburg in 'This Is Us.' Vayntrub was also cast as Squirrel Girl for a Marvel television pilot, which was not picked up, but continued voicing the character in the Marvel Rising franchise. Her other works include the film 'Mother's Little Helpers' and the series 'That Moment When.'
Related topics: ER, AT&T, Other Space, This Is Us, Squirrel Girl, Marvel Rising
Activism
What are Milana Vayntrub's activist efforts?
Milana Vayntrub co-founded 'Can't Do Nothing,' a movement addressing the European migrant crisis after visiting Greece and meeting Syrian refugees. She is also vocal about her support for abortion rights, sharing her personal experience with abortion due to financial concerns during college.
Related topics: Can't Do Nothing, European migrant crisis, Syrian refugees
Personal Life
What personal challenges has Milana Vayntrub faced?
In 2020, Milana Vayntrub faced a wave of online sexual harassment, sparked by manipulated images shared on social media. AT&T, where she plays Lily in commercials, defended her and disabled comments. Vayntrub publicly addressed the harassment and spoke about the emotional impact it had on her.
