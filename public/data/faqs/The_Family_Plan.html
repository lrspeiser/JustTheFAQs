FAQs: The Family Plan

Production
Where was 'The Family Plan' filmed?
Principal photography for 'The Family Plan' began in October 2022 in Atlanta, Georgia. Filming locations included a car dealership in Canton, Georgia, and aerial scenes were shot over Buffalo, New York. Additional scenes were filmed in Las Vegas at The Strat casino resort.
Related topics: Atlanta, Canton, Georgia, Buffalo, New York, Las Vegas
Reception
How was 'The Family Plan' received by audiences and critics?
'The Family Plan' received generally negative reviews from critics. On Rotten Tomatoes, it holds a 25% approval rating based on 36 critic reviews, and on Metacritic, it scored 39 out of 100, indicating "generally unfavorable" reviews. Critics were divided, with some praising Mark Wahlberg's performance and the action scenes, while others criticized the storyline for its predictability.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic
Plot
How does Dan Morgan's past catch up with him in 'The Family Plan'?
Dan Morgan, who lives a quiet suburban life, is outed after a photo of him goes viral on social media. Recognizing the danger, he takes his family on the run to Las Vegas to meet an old associate. However, they are pursued by former colleagues, leading to a series of confrontations and revealing Dan's true identity to his family. The film culminates in a dramatic showdown where Dan and his family must confront their pursuers and redefine their trust in each other.
Related topics: Buffalo, New York, Las Vegas, Stanford University

Who stars in 'The Family Plan'?

Overview
What is 'The Family Plan' about?
'The Family Plan' is a 2023 American action comedy film directed by Simon Cellan Jones. It features Mark Wahlberg as Dan Morgan, a suburban car salesman with a hidden past as a government assassin. When his secret is exposed, he must go on the run with his unsuspecting family. The film delves into themes of family, secrets, and personal identity, mixing action with comedy.
Related topics: Simon Cellan Jones, Action comedy film, Mark Wahlberg
Sequel
Is there going to be a sequel to 'The Family Plan'?
Yes, a sequel to 'The Family Plan' is in the works. Announced in 2024, the sequel will see Simon Cellan Jones, David Coggeshall, and Mark Wahlberg return as director, writer, and producer, respectively. Kit Harington is confirmed to join the cast, along with the leading actors who are expected to reprise their roles.
Related topics: Simon Cellan Jones, Mark Wahlberg, Kit Harington
