FAQs: Parking (2023 film)

Music
Who composed the music for 'Parking'?
The music for 'Parking' was composed by Sam C. S., a noted composer in the Tamil film industry. The soundtrack includes both lyrical and instrumental tracks.

Release
When and where was 'Parking' released?
'Parking' was released theatrically in India on December 1, 2023. The film was well-received and became a commercial success. Its digital rights were acquired by Disney+ Hotstar, and it was scheduled for streaming on December 30, 2023.
Related topics: Disney+ Hotstar, Star Vijay
General Information
What is the film 'Parking' about?
'Parking' is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language thriller-drama film directed by Ramkumar Balakrishnan in his directorial debut. The film stars Harish Kalyan, M. S. Bhaskar, and Indhuja Ravichandran. It revolves around Eshwar, a young IT employee, and his newly wed pregnant wife Aadhika dealing with conflicts over parking space after moving to a new house.
Related topics: Harish Kalyan, M. S. Bhaskar, Indhuja Ravichandran
Remakes
Is 'Parking' going to be remade in other languages?
Yes, 'Parking' is set to be remade in several languages, including Telugu, Malayalam, Hindi, Kannada, and English, although the production team has not formally confirmed the remake plan.
Plot
What is the central conflict in the plot of 'Parking'?
The central conflict in 'Parking' arises when Eshwar, a young 28-year old IT professional, and his wife, Aathika, have disagreements with their landlord Ilamparuthi over a parking space. This turns into a series of retaliatory acts between Eshwar and Ilamparuthi, culminating in a serious confrontation that endangers their personal lives.
Related topics: Information technology, Technology specialist
Critical Reception
How was 'Parking' received by critics?
'Parking' received positive reviews from critics. M. Suganth of The Times of India rated it 4 out of 5 stars, noting its thriller elements in a domestic drama setting. Gopinath Rajendran of The Hindu complimented its intriguing plot and cast performance, while Raisa Nasreen of Times Now rated it 3 out of 5 stars, praising Harish Kalyan's performance.
Related topics: The Times of India, The Hindu, Times Now
Production
Who directed 'Parking' and what is notable about this production?
'Parking' was directed by Ramkumar Balakrishnan, marking his directorial debut. He previously worked as an assistant director on the film 'Balloon'. The film was entirely shot in Chennai and had its title revealed by actor Harish Kalyan through social media.
