FAQs: Brandon Aubrey

General Information
Who is Brandon Aubrey and what is he known for?
Brandon Aubrey is an American multi-sport athlete born on March 14, 1995, in St. Louis, Missouri. He is known for his career as a placekicker for the Dallas Cowboys in the National Football League (NFL). He also played professional soccer as a center back, with a history in Major League Soccer (MLS) and the USL Championship.
Related topics: National Football League, Dallas Cowboys, Major League Soccer, USL Championship
Soccer Career
What achievements did Brandon Aubrey have in his youth soccer career?
During his youth soccer career, Aubrey played for Dallas Texans SC, where he captained the team to multiple titles, including five State Cups and victories in prestigious tournaments like the Gothia Cup, Dallas Cup, Disney Showcase, and Tivoli Cup. He was also named to the USSDA All-Select Team.
Related topics: Dallas Texans SC, Gothia Cup, Dallas Cup, USSDA
Soccer Career
Which college did Brandon Aubrey attend, and what were his accomplishments there?
Brandon Aubrey attended the University of Notre Dame, where he played college soccer from 2013 to 2016. His achievements include being named NSCAA Third Team All-American, First Team All-ACC, and NSCAA Academic All-American in 2016.
Related topics: University of Notre Dame, NSCAA, Atlantic Coast Conference
Professional Soccer Career
What were some key moments in Aubrey's professional soccer career?
Aubrey was drafted in the first round of the 2017 MLS SuperDraft by Toronto FC and played for their reserve team, Toronto FC II. Subsequently, he signed with Bethlehem Steel FC in 2018 but was released at the end of that season.
Related topics: Toronto FC, Toronto FC II, Bethlehem Steel FC
American Football Career
How did Brandon Aubrey transition from soccer to American football?
After his release from Bethlehem Steel FC in 2018, Aubrey decided to become a software engineer but later switched careers after his wife suggested he try American football. He trained extensively with a former collegiate kicker before joining the Birmingham Stallions in the USFL in 2022, later signing with the Dallas Cowboys in 2023.
Related topics: Birmingham Stallions (2022), United States Football League (2022), Dallas Cowboys
American Football Career
What records did Brandon Aubrey set in the NFL?
In the NFL, Aubrey set records for most consecutive field goals made to start a career (36 in 2023) and most field goals of 50+ yards without a miss in a single season. He also recorded the longest field goal in Cowboys franchise history at 65 yards.
Related topics: Field goal, Dallas Cowboys
