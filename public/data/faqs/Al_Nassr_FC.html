FAQs: Al Nassr Football Club

Achievements and Legacy
How has Al Nassr contributed to Saudi Arabian football?
Al Nassr has played a crucial role in advancing Saudi Arabian football, notably by having many of its players contribute to the national team. Legendary striker Majed Abdullah, Al Nassr's all-time leading goal-scorer, is also the all-time top scorer for the Saudi national team. The club's progressive vision has influenced the sport's growth in the region.

General Information
What is Al Nassr Football Club?
Al Nassr Football Club is a professional football club based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. It competes in the Saudi Pro League and is known for being one of only three clubs to have participated in every season of the league since its inception. In addition to football, Al Nassr is a multi-sports club with teams in sports like basketball, handball, esports, and more, catering to both men and women of all ages.

History
When was Al Nassr FC established and what are its major achievements?
Al Nassr FC was established on 24 October 1955. Throughout its history, it has won 28 official titles, including nine Saudi Pro League titles, three Crown Prince's Cup titles, and others across domestic and continental competitions. The club also boasts significant achievements in the Asian Cup Winners' Cup and Asian Super Cup.

Stadium and Facilities
Where does Al Nassr FC play its home games?
Al Nassr FC plays its home games at Al-Awwal Park, previously known as King Saud University Stadium, with a capacity of 25,000 seats. Additionally, a new stadium, the Prince Mohammed bin Salman Stadium in Qiddiya, is planned to open in 2029 with a capacity of 46,979 seats.
Related topics: Prince Mohammed bin Salman Stadium, King Saud University Stadium, Qiddiya, Riyadh
Rivalry
What is the main rivalry of Al Nassr FC?
Al Nassr FC has a long-standing rivalry with city rivals Al-Hilal, with whom they contest the Riyadh derby. This match is among the most followed football events in Saudi Arabia.
Related topics: Al-Hilal FC, Riyadh derby
Mascot and Identity
What is the mascot of Al Nassr FC and what does it represent?
The mascot of Al Nassr FC is "Knight," symbolizing the historic Knights of Najd, which aligns with their nickname "Faris Najd" (Knight of Najd). The club's identity is rooted in its Arabian heritage, reflected in its logo and team colors.
