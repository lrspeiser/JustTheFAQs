FAQs: Samoa Joe

Early Life and Background
What is Samoa Joe's real name and where was he born?
Samoa Joe was born Nuufolau Joel Seanoa on March 17, 1979, in Orange County, California, USA. He is of American nationality and is known for being an accomplished professional wrestler, voice actor, and actor.
Related topics: Orange County, California
Professional Wrestling Career
What are some highlights of Samoa Joe's early wrestling career?
Samoa Joe began his wrestling career in the late '90s. Notable early highlights include performances in Ultimate Pro Wrestling (UPW), where he feuded with John Cena and held titles like the UPW No Holds Barred Championship and the UPW Heavyweight Championship. His early career was marked by an impressive feud with wrestling greats and appearances on prominent platforms like WWF Jakked.
Related topics: Ultimate Pro Wrestling, John Cena
Professional Wrestling Career
What significant achievements did Samoa Joe have in Ring of Honor?
Samoa Joe was a major figure in Ring of Honor (ROH), holding the ROH World Championship for a record 21 months and having a memorable title defense trilogy against CM Punk. In 2004, he also became the ROH Pure Champion after defeating Jay Lethal. His match against Kenta Kobashi was highly acclaimed, receiving a 'Match of the Year' award.
Related topics: Ring of Honor, CM Punk, Jay Lethal, ROH World Championship, ROH Pure Championship, Kenta Kobashi
Professional Wrestling Career
How did Samoa Joe's time with TNA (Impact Wrestling) impact his career?
Samoa Joe's tenure in TNA was pivotal, where he held numerous titles including the TNA World Heavyweight, X Division, and Tag Team Championships. His unbeaten streak lasted 18 months and he had a legendary feud with Kurt Angle which was named 'Feud of the Year.' Joe's high-profile matches and varied storylines solidified his reputation in professional wrestling.
Related topics: TNA X Division Championship, Kurt Angle, TNA World Heavyweight Championship
Professional Wrestling Career
What was Samoa Joe's role and achievements in WWE?
In WWE, Samoa Joe was a prominent competitor, being the first-ever three-time NXT Champion and a two-time United States Champion. His critical feuds included those against Brock Lesnar and Roman Reigns. Joe also transitioned into a color commentator position in WWE before his eventual release in 2021.
Related topics: NXT Championship, Brock Lesnar, Roman Reigns
All Elite Wrestling (AEW) and Ring of Honor Return
What has Samoa Joe achieved since joining All Elite Wrestling?
Since joining AEW, Samoa Joe has become a significant figure holding both the ROH World Television Championship and the AEW TNT Championship simultaneously, earning the moniker 'King of Television'. Joe also became the longest-reigning ROH World Television Champion and later won the AEW World Championship, making him the first wrestler to have held the AEW, ROH, and TNA World Championships.
Related topics: AEW TNT Champion, ROH World Television Championship, AEW World Championship
Professional Wrestling Style and Persona
What is distinctive about Samoa Joe's wrestling style?
Samoa Joe is known for his 'technician' and 'powerhouse' wrestling style, famed for his blistering, MMA-inspired offense. His signature moves include the Coquina Clutch, Muscle Buster, and the STJoe. His style and persona have earned him the nickname 'Samoan Submission Machine,' and his attire typically includes shorts and a towel draped around his neck.
Related topics: Coquina Clutch, Muscle Buster
Other Media and Personal Life
What roles has Samoa Joe played outside of wrestling?
Outside of professional wrestling, Samoa Joe has done voice acting for video games such as 'Dota 2' and 'Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League'. He also physically portrayed the character Sweet Tooth in the TV series 'Twisted Metal'. Joe is married to his long-time wife, Jessica, and maintains close ties with fellow wrestlers like CM Punk and AJ Styles.
Related topics: Dota 2, Suicide Squad: Kill the Justice League, Twisted Metal (TV series)
