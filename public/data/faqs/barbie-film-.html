FAQs: Barbie (film)

General Information
Who directed the 2023 'Barbie' film?
The 2023 'Barbie' film was directed by Greta Gerwig.
Related topics: Greta Gerwig
Plot
What is the plot of the 2023 'Barbie' film?
The film follows Barbie, played by Margot Robbie, who embarks on a journey of self-discovery after experiencing an existential crisis. Along with Ken, played by Ryan Gosling, she travels from Barbieland to the real world, encountering various challenges and revelations about identity and society.
Related topics: Barbie, Existential crisis, Ken (doll)
Cast
Which actors starred in the 'Barbie' film?
The film stars Margot Robbie as Barbie and Ryan Gosling as Ken. The supporting cast includes America Ferrera, Kate McKinnon, Issa Rae, Rhea Perlman, Will Ferrell, and more.
Related topics: Margot Robbie, Ryan Gosling, America Ferrera, Kate McKinnon, Issa Rae, Rhea Perlman, Will Ferrell
Production
How did the 'Barbie' film overcome its development challenges?
The film's development saw numerous changes in writers and directors before settling with Greta Gerwig, who wrote the screenplay with Noah Baumbach. Production hurdles included shifting from Universal Pictures to Warner Bros. Pictures and overcoming rights issues before casting Margot Robbie as Barbie.
Related topics: Warner Bros. Pictures, Greta Gerwig, Noah Baumbach, Margot Robbie
Themes and Interpretations
What themes are explored in 'Barbie'?
'Barbie' explores themes such as feminism, existentialism, and identity. The film addresses societal norms, challenges gender roles, and depicts the journey of self-awareness and empowerment.
Related topics: Feminism, Existentialism
Box Office and Reception
How was 'Barbie' received financially and critically?
'Barbie' was a box office hit, grossing $1.446 billion worldwide, making it the highest-grossing film of 2023. It was praised for its humor, visual style, and performances, especially of Margot Robbie and Ryan Gosling. It also sparked discussions on its portrayal of gender politics.
Related topics: 2023 in film, List of highest-grossing films, Margot Robbie, Ryan Gosling
Cultural Impact
What impact did 'Barbie' have on popular culture?
The 'Barbie' film became a cultural phenomenon, influencing fashion, discussions on gender roles, and even its marketing collaborations with major brands. It also played a significant role in the 'Barbenheimer' phenomenon, being released the same day as 'Oppenheimer'.
Related topics: Barbenheimer, Oppenheimer (film)
Soundtrack
Who curated the music for the 'Barbie' film?
The soundtrack was curated by Mark Ronson, featuring artists like Dua Lipa, Billie Eilish, and Ryan Gosling. The soundtrack 'Barbie the Album' contributed significantly to the film's cultural impact.
Related topics: Barbie the Album, Mark Ronson, Dua Lipa, Billie Eilish, Ryan Gosling
Controversies
Were there any controversies surrounding the 'Barbie' film?
The film faced controversies including international censorship concerns, notably related to a map shown in the movie that was interpreted as depicting China's disputed nine-dash line. Some countries debated banning the film over this perceived political statement.
Related topics: Nine-dash line, Territorial disputes in the South China Sea
