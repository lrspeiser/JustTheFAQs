FAQs: Dunki (film)

Overview
What is the film 'Dunki' about?
'Dunki' is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language comedy-drama film directed and co-written by Rajkumar Hirani. It revolves around the illegal immigration technique known as 'donkey flight' and follows the story of a middle-aged woman, Manu Randhawa, as she navigates her struggles to return to India. The film stars popular actors including Shah Rukh Khan, Taapsee Pannu, and Vicky Kaushal. It explores themes of migration and personal sacrifice, against a backdrop of humor and emotion.
Related topics: Dunki (film), Rajkumar Hirani, Shah Rukh Khan, Illegal immigration
Production
Who directed and produced 'Dunki'?
The film 'Dunki' was directed by Rajkumar Hirani, who also co-wrote and produced it. The production was in collaboration with Jio Studios and Red Chillies Entertainment. The film's cinematography was handled by C. K. Muraleedharan, and the music was composed by Pritam and Shekhar Ravjiani. This project marked a significant collaboration as it brought together Shah Rukh Khan and Rajkumar Hirani for the first time.
Related topics: Rajkumar Hirani, Production companies, Shah Rukh Khan
Release
When was 'Dunki' released?
'Dunki' was theatrically released worldwide on December 21, 2023. The film was highly anticipated due to the collaboration of Rajkumar Hirani and Shah Rukh Khan, making it one of the most talked-about releases of the year.

Story
What is the plot of 'Dunki'?
The story of 'Dunki' follows Manu Randhawa, who, in her middle age, seeks to return to India after a tumultuous life in England. With the help of her friends, she attempts to navigate illegal immigration routes, the challenges of migration, and the sacrifices involved on their journey to reunite with loved ones.

Themes
What themes does 'Dunki' explore?
'Dunki' explores themes of illegal immigration, personal sacrifice, resilience, and the dream of a better life. It highlights the struggles faced by individuals seeking new opportunities while also addressing the emotional toll of migration and separation from family.

Awards
Has 'Dunki' received any awards or nominations?
At the 69th Filmfare Awards, 'Dunki' received nine nominations, including Best Actor for Shah Rukh Khan and Best Actress for Taapsee Pannu, with Vicky Kaushal winning the award for Best Supporting Actor. The film's music also garnered nominations for Best Music Director and Best Lyricist.
