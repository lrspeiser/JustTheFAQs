Personal Information
What is Amon-Ra St. Brown's background and family history?
Amon-Ra St. Brown was born in Anaheim Hills, California, and comes from a unique multicultural background. His father, John Brown, is a two-time amateur Mr. Universe bodybuilder, and his mother, Miriam Brown (née Steyer), is from Leverkusen, Germany. Name
What is the origin of Amon-Ra St. Brown's name?
Amon-Ra St. Brown was named by his father, who was inspired by names he believed had African, especially Egyptian, significance. "Amon-Ra" refers to the ancient Egyptian god of the air, sun, and creation – a deity venerated as the king of the gods and the creator of all things. This name signifies grandeur and reverence in Egyptian mythology.

Professional Career
How has Amon-Ra St. Brown performed in the NFL, and what are some of his key career highlights?
Since being drafted by the Detroit Lions in the fourth round of the 2021 NFL Draft, Amon-Ra St. Brown has become a standout wide receiver. He was voted to the Pro Bowl in 2022 and 2023, and was named a first-team All-Pro in 2023. St. Brown achieved the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Month title in December 2021 and broke the Lions' rookie receiving record with 912 yards. As of 2024, he holds several Lions franchise records, including being the youngest player with a 1,000-yard receiving season and having the most consecutive games with a touchdown reception.
