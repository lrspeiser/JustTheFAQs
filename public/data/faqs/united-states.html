Political Framework
What type of government does the United States have?
The United States is a federal presidential republic, which includes a system of checks and balances across three branches: legislative, executive, and judicial.

Economy
What is the economic status of the United States?
The United States has the world's largest economy by nominal GDP, valued at over $29 trillion as of 2024, and it accounts for over 25% of global nominal GDP. It is a leading innovator in several sectors.

Biodiversity
What is unique about the biodiversity of the United States?
The U.S. is one of 17 megadiverse countries, with large numbers of endemic species, including over 17,000 species of vascular plants and 428 mammal species. It contains diverse habitats across its vast landmass.

Military
How is the U.S. military structured?
The U.S. military is led by the President, who is the commander-in-chief. It includes the Army, Marine Corps, Navy, Air Force, Space Force, and Coast Guard, operating under the Department of Defense and Homeland Security.
