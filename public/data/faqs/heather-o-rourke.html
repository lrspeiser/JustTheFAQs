FAQs: Heather O'Rourke

About Heather O'Rourke
Who was Heather O'Rourke?
Heather Michele O'Rourke was an American child actress best known for her role as Carol Anne Freeling in the 1982 supernatural horror film "Poltergeist". She gained fame and recognition through her significant contributions to the horror genre and appeared in several television shows before her untimely death at the age of 12.
Related topics: Poltergeist (1982 film), Carol Anne Freeling, Happy Days, Webster (TV series), Young Artist Award
Early Life
Where was Heather O'Rourke born and raised?
Heather O'Rourke was born on December 27, 1975, in San Diego, California. She grew up with her family in a trailer park in Anaheim, California, and later moved to Big Bear Lake, California, where she attended Big Bear Elementary School and served as class president.
Related topics: San Diego, Anaheim, California, Big Bear Lake, California
Acting Career
How did Heather O'Rourke start her acting career?
Heather O'Rourke began her acting career after being discovered by Steven Spielberg while having lunch with her mother. Spielberg cast her for the role of Carol Anne Freeling in the horror film "Poltergeist" (1982), which became a critically acclaimed success. Despite being initially frightened by some aspects of filming, Spielberg made accommodations to ensure her comfort, and she went on to reprise her iconic role in two sequels.
Related topics: Steven Spielberg, Poltergeist (1982 film), Poltergeist II: The Other Side, Poltergeist III
Illness and Death
What were the circumstances surrounding Heather O'Rourke's death?
Heather O'Rourke died on February 1, 1988, due to complications from congenital stenosis of the intestine and septic shock. Her health issues began with flu-like symptoms, leading to cardiac arrest and eventually emergency surgery. Her case was described as "distinctly unusual" due to the absence of prior symptoms of the bowel defect. A private funeral was held, and she was entombed at Westwood Village Memorial Park Cemetery.
Related topics: Congenital disorder, Stenosis, Septic shock, Westwood Village Memorial Park Cemetery
Filmography and Accolades
What are some of Heather O'Rourke's notable film and TV roles?
Heather O'Rourke is most celebrated for her role in the "Poltergeist" film series, portraying Carol Anne Freeling. She also had television roles in "Happy Days" as Heather Pfister and "Webster" as Melanie. Her work in "Webster" earned her a Young Artist Award. She continued to impress with her performances in TV specials and other dramas.
