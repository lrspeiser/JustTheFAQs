Presidential Role
Who is Javier Milei?
Javier Milei is an Argentine economist and politician who has been serving as the President of Argentina since 2023. Known for his distinct views on economics and politics, Milei has also hosted radio shows and taught university courses. His ideological stance separates him from mainstream Argentine politics.

Election and Political Impact
How did Milei become notable in Argentine politics?
Milei gained recognition through debates on Argentine television marked by his aggressive rhetoric and insults towards rivals, which led some to label him as 'antipolitical' or disruptive. His notoriety increased significantly during his 2023 presidential campaign, where his flamboyant personality and bold statements attracted both national and international attention.

Presidential Role
What were some of Milei's early actions as President?
Upon taking office, Milei pledged economic reforms including decrees to reduce the number of ministries from 18 to 9 and an initiative to deregulate the Argentine economy, known as Decree 70/2023. His foreign policy shifted Argentina's stance on BRICS and increased alignment with nations like the United States, the UK, and Israel.

Political Positions
What are Milei's main political positions?
Javier Milei is known for his right-wing populist and libertarian viewpoints. He advocates for laissez-faire economics, opposes abortion and euthanasia, and supports gun ownership, drug policy freedom, and same-sex marriage. His foreign policy includes closer ties with the United States and has criticized Argentina's relationship with China.
