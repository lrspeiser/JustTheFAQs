FAQs: Saltburn (film)

Film Overview
What is the film 'Saltburn' about?
'Saltburn' is a 2023 black comedy thriller film written, directed, and co-produced by Emerald Fennell. It stars Barry Keoghan, Jacob Elordi, Rosamund Pike, Richard E. Grant, and others. The story revolves around an Oxford student who becomes obsessed with a wealthy fellow student, eventually being invited to spend the summer at the student's eccentric family estate in Oxford and Northamptonshire.
Related topics: Emerald Fennell, Barry Keoghan, University of Oxford
Plot
What is the main plot of 'Saltburn'?
The film follows Oliver Quick, a scholarship student at the University of Oxford, who struggles to fit in due to his less privileged background. Oliver befriends Felix Catton, a popular, affluent student, and after some dramatic twists, is invited to Felix's family estate, Saltburn. The plot thickens as Oliver's obsession leads to a series of tragic events, culminating in deaths within the Catton family, which Oliver manipulates to his advantage.
Related topics: Scholarship, University of Oxford, English country house
Cast
Who stars in the film 'Saltburn'?
The film stars Barry Keoghan as Oliver Quick, Jacob Elordi as Felix Catton, Rosamund Pike as Lady Elspeth Catton, and Richard E. Grant as Sir James Catton. Other notable cast members include Alison Oliver, Archie Madekwe, and Carey Mulligan.
Related topics: Barry Keoghan, Jacob Elordi, Rosamund Pike, Richard E. Grant
Production
Where was 'Saltburn' filmed?
Filming for 'Saltburn' took place in the University of Oxford and at Drayton House in Northamptonshire. Drayton House, which had never been used for any film before, served as a primary filming location, aligning with the film's narrative about a grand country estate.
Related topics: University of Oxford, Drayton House, Northamptonshire
Reception
How was 'Saltburn' received by critics?
'Saltburn' received generally positive reviews. On Rotten Tomatoes, 72% of critics gave the film a positive review with an average rating of 6.8/10. Metacritic assigned it a score of 61 out of 100, indicating 'generally favorable' reviews. Audiences were more mixed, with some praising the film's unique style and story, while others criticized it for certain plot elements.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic
Music
What music is featured in 'Saltburn'?
The film's soundtrack includes a score by Anthony Willis, known for his work on 'Promising Young Woman'. It features the song "Murder on the Dancefloor" by Sophie Ellis-Bextor and tracks by Ladytron, Mason and Princess Superstar, and Tomcraft, which became popular on TikTok following the film's release.
