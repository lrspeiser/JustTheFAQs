FAQs: My Life with the Walter Boys

General Information
What is 'My Life with the Walter Boys' about?
'My Life with the Walter Boys' is a teen drama television series based on Ali Novak's 2014 novel, originally serialized on Wattpad. The series follows Jackie Howard, a teenage girl from Manhattan who moves to rural Colorado after losing her family in a car accident. She faces challenges as she adjusts to her new life with the Walters, a family with seven sons and one daughter. The show highlights her journey of healing and friendship amid a complicated family dynamic.

Production
Who developed 'My Life with the Walter Boys'?
The series was developed by Melanie Halsall, who also serves as the showrunner. It is produced by Sony Pictures Television and iGeneration Studios, with filming locations primarily in Alberta, Canada, including Calgary and Cochrane.
