FAQs: Prince Joachim of Denmark

Introduction
Who is Prince Joachim of Denmark?
Prince Joachim of Denmark, Count of Monpezat, is a member of the Danish royal family. Born on June 7, 1969, he is the younger son of Queen Margrethe II and is fifth in the line of succession to the Danish throne. He holds various titles and honors including Knight of the Order of the Elephant and Grand Commander of the Order of the Dannebrog.
Related topics: Danish royal family, Order of the Elephant, Order of the Dannebrog, Succession to the Danish throne
Early Life and Education
Where was Prince Joachim educated?
Prince Joachim was educated at several prestigious institutions. He began his education as a private pupil at Amalienborg Palace before attending Krebs' Skole in Copenhagen. Later, he attended Œregaard Gymnasium and studied agrarian economics at Den Classenske Agerbrugskole Næsgaard. His education also extended internationally as he studied at École des Roches in Normandy, France. He speaks multiple languages including Danish, French, English, and German.
Related topics: Amalienborg Palace, Krebs School, Normandy, Œregaard Gymnasium
Schackenborg and Family
What is the significance of Schackenborg Castle to Prince Joachim?
Schackenborg Castle was granted to Prince Joachim in 1993 as part of the will of Count Hans Schack. It became a family residence with his first wife, Alexandra, until their divorce in 2005, after which he lived there with his second wife, Princess Marie, until the estate was handed over to a foundation in 2014. Prince Joachim and his family continue to visit for holidays.
Related topics: Schackenborg Castle, Princess Marie of Denmark, Møgeltønder
Military Career
What roles has Prince Joachim held in the Danish military?
Prince Joachim has served in various capacities in the Danish military. He started as a platoon commander in the Prince's Life Regiment and later became a captain. He was also a staff officer at Danske Division and served as a liaison officer. In 2015, he was appointed as a special advisor to the Chief of Defense. He reached the rank of Brigadier General and served as Denmark's military attaché to France and later to the United States.
Related topics: Royal Danish Military Academy, Danske Division, Chief of Defence (Denmark), Royal Danish Army
Marriages and Children
Who are Prince Joachim's wives and children?
Prince Joachim was first married to Alexandra Christina Manley in 1995, with whom he had two sons, Count Nikolai and Count Felix. After their divorce in 2005, he married Princess Marie of Denmark in 2008, and they have two children, Count Henrik and Countess Athena. None of his children have been styled as Royal Highnesses since 1 January 2023, when they were given the titles of Count or Countess of Monpezat.

Interests and Hobbies
What are some of Prince Joachim's interests?
Prince Joachim has a keen interest in historic motor sports and has participated multiple times in the Copenhagen Historic Grand Prix. Apart from racing, in 2019 he co-produced a documentary series for Danmarks Radio exploring Denmark's history.
Related topics: Historic motorsport, Copenhagen Historic Grand Prix, DR (broadcaster)
Titles and Honors
What are the significant titles and honors received by Prince Joachim?
Throughout his life, Prince Joachim has received numerous titles and honors, including Knight of the Order of the Elephant and Grand Commander of the Order of the Dannebrog. He has also been awarded several medals such as the Silver Jubilee Medal of Her Majesty The Queen and various international honors from countries like Belgium, Brazil, and Luxembourg.
Related topics: Order of the Elephant, Order of the Dannebrog
