Early life and Education
Where did Rosamund Pike attend college and what did she study?
Rosamund Pike attended Wadham College, Oxford, where she studied English literature. Despite initially being rejected from drama schools, she gained a place at Oxford due to her impressive acting abilities which were noticed during her performance in a National Youth Theatre production.
Related topics: Wadham College, Oxford, National Youth Theatre
Overview
Who is Rosamund Pike and what is she known for?
Rosamund Mary Ellen Pike is a British actress born in 1979, renowned for her portrayal of morally complex characters in psychological thrillers and dramas. She has received numerous accolades, including a Primetime Emmy Award and a Golden Globe Award, with nominations for an Academy Award and two BAFTA Awards. Pike rose to fame with her role as a Bond girl in 'Die Another Day' and is most celebrated for her leading role in the movie 'Gone Girl.'
Related topics: List of awards and nominations received by Rosamund Pike, Golden Globe Award, Academy Award, BAFTA Awards
Activism
What causes does Rosamund Pike support or engage in?
Rosamund Pike has supported gender equality and development causes, signing a 2015 letter urging leaders to focus on women as they set future development priorities. She is also the first Ambassador for the Mines Advisory Group, a Nobel Peace Prize-winning NGO working to clear landmines and unexploded ordnance.
Related topics: ONE Campaign, Mines Advisory Group
Career Highlights
What are some notable films Rosamund Pike has starred in?
Rosamund Pike has starred in various acclaimed films including 'Gone Girl' (2014), for which she received widespread recognition and several award nominations; 'Pride & Prejudice' (2005), 'An Education' (2009), 'Made in Dagenham' (2010), and 'I Care a Lot' (2020), for which she won a Golden Globe Award.
Related topics: Gone Girl (film), Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a Motion Picture – Drama
Personal Life
Who has Rosamund Pike been in relationships with?
Rosamund Pike had a two-year relationship with Simon Woods during her time at Oxford. She was later engaged to film director Joe Wright, although the engagement ended. Since December 2009, Pike has been with businessman Robie Uniacke, with whom she has two children.
Related topics: Simon Woods, Joe Wright
Career Highlights
What was Rosamund Pike's breakthrough role?
Rosamund Pike's breakthrough role was as the James Bond girl, Miranda Frost, in the film 'Die Another Day' (2002). This role established her as a notable actress in Hollywood.
