FAQs: Barry Keoghan

Early Life
What challenges did Barry Keoghan face in his early life?
Barry Keoghan was born on October 18, 1992, in Dublin, Ireland. His early life was marked by adversity; his mother struggled with drug addiction and passed away when he was just 12 years old. Subsequently, Keoghan spent seven years in foster care, living in 13 different foster homes along with his brother Eric, until they were raised by their grandmother and other relatives. He began acting in school plays but was banned for 'messing about', and later found his initial film education by sneaking into cinema screenings.

Career
What are some of Barry Keoghan's most notable film roles?
Barry Keoghan began his acting career in 2011 and gained acclaim for his performances in a number of films. Some of his notable roles include George Mills in 'Dunkirk' (2017), Martin Lang in 'The Killing of a Sacred Deer' (2017), and Druig in 'Eternals' (2021). He also portrayed Dominic Kearney in 'The Banshees of Inisherin' (2022), a performance that won him a BAFTA for Best Supporting Actor. Furthermore, Keoghan played the Joker in 'The Batman' (2022) and was part of the psychological drama 'Saltburn' (2023), for which he received critical acclaim.

In the Media
How has Barry Keoghan been recognized within the film industry and media?
Barry Keoghan has been described by 'The Hollywood Reporter' as 'the next big thing' for his film work. He was nominated for the BAFTA Rising Star Award in 2019. Additionally, he received several accolades throughout his career, including a BAFTA Film Award and nominations for an Academy Award and Golden Globe Awards.
Related topics: The Hollywood Reporter, BAFTA Rising Star Award
Personal Life
What personal interests and lifestyle choices does Barry Keoghan have outside of acting?
Outside of acting, Barry Keoghan is an amateur boxer and is also known as a supporter of Manchester United, the English football club. He appeared in a promotional campaign for the 2024-25 season kit alongside his second cousin Frank Stapleton. Additionally, he has been a brand ambassador for luxury fashion brands like Dior and Barretstown, and more recently, Burberry. In his personal life, Keoghan has faced legal issues including an arrest for public intoxication in Dublin in 2022, where he was later released without charge.
