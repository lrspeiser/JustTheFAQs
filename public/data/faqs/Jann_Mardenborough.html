FAQs: Jann Mardenborough

Film and Media
What is the 'Gran Turismo' film about?
The 2023 'Gran Turismo' film is a biopic loosely based on Jann Mardenborough's life, highlighting his journey from a gamer to a professional racer. Mardenborough was involved in the production as a co-producer, stunt driver, and consultant.
Related topics: Gran Turismo (film)
Introduction
Who is Jann Mardenborough?
Jann Mardenborough is a British professional racing driver who gained fame by winning the GT Academy in 2011, which was a competition that transformed sim racers into professional racing drivers. He is notable for competing in various racing series such as Super GT, Super Formula, and the FIA World Endurance Championship.
Racing Highlights
What achievements did Jann Mardenborough garner in the Super GT series?
In the Super GT series, Jann Mardenborough won a race in the GT300 class and finished fourth overall in 2016. He moved to the GT500 class in 2017, where he scored a podium finish and contended for wins.
Related topics: Super GT
Personal Life
How did Jann Mardenborough start racing?
Jann Mardenborough developed an interest in motorsport through sim racing, particularly playing 'Gran Turismo' on PlayStation. With no prior motorsport experience, he entered and won the GT Academy competition, which launched his professional racing career.
Related topics: Sim racing, Gran Turismo (series)
Racing Highlights
What notable events did Jann Mardenborough participate in the 24 Hours of Le Mans?
Jann Mardenborough competed in the 24 Hours of Le Mans between 2013 and 2015. He finished third in the LMP2 class in his debut year, 2013, and returned in subsequent years although faced challenges such as a competitive car and eventual team withdrawal in 2015.
Related topics: 24 Hours of Le Mans
Career
What is notable about Jann Mardenborough's racing career?
Jann Mardenborough's career is notable for its unique beginning, starting from video gaming to professional racing. He became a champion GT Academy winner, joined Nissan in professional racing, and has participated in prestigious events like the 24 Hours of Le Mans, achieving podium finishes in the LMP2 class. His career spans GT3 racing, junior formula cars, and high-level competitions in Super GT and Super Formula.
Related topics: GT Academy, 24 Hours of Le Mans, Super GT, Super Formula, FIA World Endurance Championship
Personal Life
Who are Jann Mardenborough's family?
Jann Mardenborough is the son of former professional footballer Steve Mardenborough. Though born in Darlington, he spent most of his childhood in Cardiff, Wales.
Related topics: Steve Mardenborough, Darlington, Cardiff
