FAQs: Val Kilmer

Health
What health challenges has Val Kilmer faced?
Val Kilmer faced a battle with throat cancer starting in 2015, which required a procedure on his trachea, chemotherapy, and two tracheotomies. This impacted his vocal cords, making it difficult for him to speak.
Related topics: Laryngeal cancer, Trachea, Chemotherapy, Tracheotomy
Career Challenges
What challenges did Val Kilmer face in his acting career?
Val Kilmer is known for being difficult to work with, having disputes with co-stars like Marlon Brando on 'The Island of Dr. Moreau' and Tom Sizemore during 'Red Planet'. He also replaced Michael Keaton as Batman in 'Batman Forever', but did not return for further sequels due to a troubled relationship with director Joel Schumacher.
Related topics: The Island of Dr. Moreau (1996 film), Red Planet (film), Batman Forever
Health
What health challenges has Val Kilmer faced?
Val Kilmer was diagnosed with throat cancer in 2015, which significantly affected his vocal cords after a trachea procedure. Despite his Christian Science beliefs, he opted for chemotherapy and surgeries. He announced being cancer-free in 2020 but continues to manage health-related challenges, including using a feeding tube.
Related topics: Laryngeal cancer, Chemotherapy, Christian Science
Early Life
What is Val Kilmer's background and early life?
Val Kilmer was born on December 31, 1959, in Los Angeles, California, and is of Swedish, Irish, German, and Cherokee descent. He attended Chatsworth High School with Kevin Spacey, and his high school girlfriend was Mare Winningham. Kilmer was the youngest person to be admitted to the Juilliard School's Drama Division.
Related topics: Los Angeles, California, Juilliard School
Introduction
Who is Val Kilmer?
Val Kilmer is an American actor known for his roles in stage and film. Rising to fame in the 1980s, he starred in popular films such as 'Top Secret!', 'Real Genius', 'Top Gun', and 'Willow'. He gained critical acclaim for portraying Jim Morrison in 'The Doors' and has played iconic characters like Batman and Doc Holliday.
Related topics: Top Secret!, Real Genius, Top Gun, Willow, The Doors (film), Batman Forever
Personal Life
Who has Val Kilmer dated or been married to?
Val Kilmer has had relationships with several high-profile figures, including Cher, Lesley Ann Warren, Cindy Crawford, Angelina Jolie, Daryl Hannah, and Ellen Barkin. He was married to actress Joanne Whalley from 1988 to 1996, with whom he has two children, Mercedes and Jack Kilmer.
Related topics: Joanne Whalley, Cher, Cindy Crawford, Daryl Hannah, Ellen Barkin
Acting Career
What are some of Val Kilmer's most notable roles?
Val Kilmer gained fame with roles in films such as 'Top Secret!' (1984), 'Real Genius' (1985), and 'Top Gun' (1986) where he played Iceman. He is also well-known for his portrayal of Jim Morrison in Oliver Stone's 'The Doors' (1991), Doc Holliday in the western 'Tombstone' (1993), and Batman in 'Batman Forever' (1995).
Related topics: Top Secret!, Real Genius, Top Gun, The Doors (film), Tombstone (film), Batman Forever
