FAQs: Wordle

Overview
What is Wordle?
Wordle is a web-based word game developed by Josh Wardle, where players have six attempts to guess a five-letter word. Feedback is given in the form of colored tiles indicating letter matches and correct positions. All players attempt to guess the same word each day.
Related topics: Word game
Gameplay
How does the gameplay of Wordle work?
Each day, players must guess a five-letter word in six tries. After each guess, letters are marked as green (correct and in position), yellow (in the word but in the wrong position), or gray (not in the word). The game's daily word is the same for every player, creating a shared challenge.
Related topics: Jotto, Mastermind (board game)
History
Who created Wordle and why?
Wordle was created by Josh Wardle for his partner, inspired by games like Mastermind and Jotto. It was made public in October 2021 and gained popularity due to its simple gameplay and the viral sharing of results.
Related topics: Josh Wardle, Mastermind (board game)
Popularity
How did Wordle gain its popularity?
Wordle gained popularity in December 2021 when the shareability of results as emoji grids spread on Twitter. This share feature, coupled with its challenging yet straightforward gameplay, attracted millions in a short span, further amplified by social media.
Related topics: Emoji, Twitter
Acquisition by The New York Times
Why did The New York Times acquire Wordle?
The New York Times acquired Wordle in January 2022 for an undisclosed seven-figure sum because of its massive popularity and to enhance their collection of digital games. The purchase aimed to draw more subscribers to their games section.
Related topics: The New York Times, The New York Times Games
Adaptations and Clones
What are some notable clones and adaptations of Wordle?
Following Wordle's success, many clones appeared, such as Absurdle, which changes the target word to avoid discovery, and Sweardle targeting swear words. Wordle-like games have been adapted into various languages and media.
Related topics: Absurdle, Video game clone
Usage
How did Wordle influence Google searches in 2022?
Wordle was the top Google search term globally in 2022. Its daily words influenced search trends, as people looked up definitions for words used as Wordle answers, affecting overall search ranking lists.

Innovations
What unique features does Wordle offer for accessibility?
Wordle includes a dark theme and a high-contrast color scheme for colorblind players, turning the original colors from green and yellow to orange and blue to ensure accessibility and ease of play.
