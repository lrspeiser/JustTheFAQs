Geography and Climate
What is the geographical size of the United States?
The United States is the world's third-largest country by total area, covering 3,796,742 square miles (9,833,520 km²). It includes 50 states and various territories and has the largest exclusive economic zone.

Military
How is the U.S. military structured?
The U.S. military is led by the President, who is the commander-in-chief. It includes the Army, Marine Corps, Navy, Air Force, Space Force, and Coast Guard, operating under the Department of Defense and Homeland Security.
