FAQs: Killers of the Flower Moon (film)

Themes
What are the central themes of "Killers of the Flower Moon"?
The film examines themes of greed, murder, and systemic exploitation of Indigenous peoples. It provides a narrative contrasting the idealized freedom in traditional Westerns, focusing instead on the community devastated by the conspiracies of powerful white men. The film shifts from the broader historical events to emphasize personal relationships, such as the complex bond between Mollie and Ernest Burkhart.
Related topics: Martin Scorsese, Osage Nation
Overview
What is the premise of "Killers of the Flower Moon"?
"Killers of the Flower Moon" is a 2023 American epic crime drama film directed by Martin Scorsese and co-written with Eric Roth. The film is based on the 2017 book by David Grann about the Osage Murders in 1920s Oklahoma, where members of the Osage Nation were murdered after oil was discovered on their land. The film explores a series of murders aimed at exploiting the wealth of the Osage people.
Related topics: Osage Indian murders, Osage Nation, David Grann
Production
Who directed "Killers of the Flower Moon" and what was unique about its production?
The film was directed by Martin Scorsese, marking another collaboration with actors Leonardo DiCaprio and Robert De Niro. Notably, cinematographer Rodrigo Prieto shot the film mainly on film stock instead of digital, and production employed Osage language and cultural consultants for authenticity. The involvement of the Osage Nation was significant throughout the film's production.
Related topics: Martin Scorsese, Leonardo DiCaprio, Robert De Niro, Rodrigo Prieto
Cultural and Indigenous Impact
How did "Killers of the Flower Moon" involve the Osage Nation, and what was the response?
Director Martin Scorsese's film made efforts to genuinely involve the Osage Nation, casting Osage actors and employing members as cultural consultants. Osage language and traditions were integrated into the film for authenticity. While the portrayal in the film was praised for capturing Osage culture and language, it received mixed reactions regarding representation, with some Indigenous voices feeling it didn't fully convey the Osage perspective in favor of focusing on white perpetrators.
Related topics: Osage Nation, Martin Scorsese
Cast
Which actors are part of the "Killers of the Flower Moon" cast?
The film stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Ernest Burkhart, Robert De Niro as William King Hale, and Lily Gladstone as Mollie Burkhart. The cast also includes Jesse Plemons, Tantoo Cardinal, John Lithgow, and Brendan Fraser, among others.
Related topics: Leonardo DiCaprio, Robert De Niro, Lily Gladstone
Release and Reception
How was "Killers of the Flower Moon" received by audiences and critics?
The film premiered at the 2023 Cannes Film Festival to a nine-minute standing ovation and went on to gross $158.8 million worldwide. It received critical acclaim, praised for its direction, screenplay, performances, and musical score. However, there was minor criticism regarding its runtime. The film was nominated for several prestigious awards and particularly noted for Lily Gladstone's performance, which earned her a Best Actress nomination at the Academy Awards.
Related topics: Cannes Film Festival, Academy Awards, Lily Gladstone
