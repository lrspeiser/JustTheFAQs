What records has CeeDee Lamb set in his NFL career?

NFL Career
How did CeeDee Lamb start his professional career with the Dallas Cowboys?
CeeDee Lamb was drafted by the Dallas Cowboys as the 17th overall pick in the 2020 NFL Draft. He signed a 4-year, $14.01 million contract including a $7.7 million signing bonus. Lamb made an impressive debut in his rookie season, tallying 74 receptions for 935 yards and 5 touchdowns.

Recent Developments
What was CeeDee Lamb's contract extension with the Dallas Cowboys?
On August 26, 2024, CeeDee Lamb signed a lucrative four-year, $136 million contract extension with the Dallas Cowboys, including $100 million guaranteed and a $38 million signing bonus, securing his position with the team through the 2028 season.

College Career
What were CeeDee Lamb's notable achievements in college?
CeeDee Lamb had an outstanding college career with the Oklahoma Sooners. In 2019, he was named a consensus All-American, caught 62 passes for 1,327 yards, and achieved a record 21.4 yards per reception. Lamb was also a finalist for the Biletnikoff Award, marking him as one of the best college football receivers.
