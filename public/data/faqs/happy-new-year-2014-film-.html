FAQs: Happy New Year (2014 film)

General Information
Who directed the 2014 film 'Happy New Year'?
The 2014 film 'Happy New Year' was directed by Farah Khan. It is a Hindi-language heist comedy thriller that includes an ensemble cast featuring Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, and others.
Related topics: Farah Khan, Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone
Cast and Crew
Which actors starred in 'Happy New Year'?
The film starred Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Abhishek Bachchan, Sonu Sood, Boman Irani, Vivaan Shah, and Jackie Shroff.
Related topics: Shah Rukh Khan, Deepika Padukone, Abhishek Bachchan, Sonu Sood, Boman Irani, Vivaan Shah, Jackie Shroff
Plot
What is the plot of 'Happy New Year'?
'Happy New Year' follows Charlie, a street fighter who assembles a team to execute a heist during the World Dance Championship in Dubai, aiming to clear his father's name and take revenge on a businessman, Charan Grover.
Related topics: Dubai, World Dance Championship
Production
Where was 'Happy New Year' filmed?
The film was primarily shot in Dubai, with significant scenes filmed at Atlantis, The Palm. Some parts were also filmed in Mumbai at locations like Mehboob Studios and Wilson College.
Related topics: Dubai, Atlantis, The Palm, Mehboob Studios, Wilson College, Mumbai
