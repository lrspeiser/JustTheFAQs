Personal Life
What are some personal aspects of Dak Prescott's life outside of football?
Outside of football, Dak Prescott is known as a Christian and has been active in charitable endeavors, establishing the Faith Fight Finish Foundation in honor of his mother, who died of colon cancer in 2013. How was Dak Prescott's early life and college career?
Dak Prescott was born in Sulphur, Louisiana, the youngest of three sons, and was raised by his mother after his parents divorced. NFL Career Beginnings and Achievements
How did Dak Prescott start his career in the NFL, and what were his initial achievements?
Dak Prescott was drafted by the Dallas Cowboys in the 4th round of the 2016 NFL Draft. Originally intended as a backup, he became the starting quarterback after Tony Romo suffered an injury. Prescott had a standout rookie season, setting multiple rookie records, including the highest passer rating by a rookie quarterback. He led the Cowboys to a 13-3 record and was named the NFL Offensive Rookie of the Year.

Has Dak Prescott faced any significant injuries?
Yes, Dak Prescott has faced several significant injuries during his career. Notably, in 2020, he suffered a compound fracture and dislocation to his right ankle, which ended his season. In 2024, he injured his hamstring, leading to season-ending surgery.

Basic Information
Who is Dak Prescott?
Dak Prescott, born Rayne Dakota Prescott on July 29, 1993, in Sulphur, Louisiana, is an American professional football quarterback for the Dallas Cowboys of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football at Mississippi State before being selected by the Cowboys in the fourth round of the 2016 NFL Draft.
