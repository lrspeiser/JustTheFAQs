FAQs: Confidence Man

Background
What is the origin and genre of Confidence Man?
Confidence Man is an Australian indie electro-pop band originating from Brisbane, Queensland, Australia. They are known for their electropop music style.
Related topics: Brisbane, Queensland, Electropop
Discography
How has Confidence Man's music evolved over time?
Starting with their debut 'Confident Music for Confident People', Confidence Man incorporated a fun and energetic electro-pop vibe. With their second album 'Tilt', they ventured into a more club-focused sound, a direction continued in their third album '3AM (La La La)', which draws on British rave culture influences.
Related topics: Confident Music for Confident People, Tilt, 3AM (La La La)
Formation and Early Work
How did Confidence Man form and what led to their first album?
Formed in 2016, the members of Confidence Man met in Brisbane's music scene and had been part of other bands like The Belligerents and The Jungle Giants. They initially started writing dance songs as a joke but realized the potential. They released their debut album, 'Confident Music for Confident People', in April 2018 after signing with Heavenly Records.
Related topics: Brisbane, The Belligerents, The Jungle Giants, Confident Music for Confident People, Heavenly Records
Music and Style
What kind of music does Confidence Man produce?
Confidence Man produces electropop music, characterized by its energetic dance beats and catchy hooks. Their work has evolved into a more club-focused sound, especially with the shift beginning with their album 'Tilt'.
Related topics: Electropop, Confident Music for Confident People, Tilt (Confidence Man album)
Members
Who are the members of Confidence Man?
The band consists of Janet Planet (Grace Stephenson) and Sugar Bones (Aidan Moore) on vocals, Reggie Goodchild (Lewis Stephenson) on production, and Clarence McGuffie, whose current identity is unknown but was previously Sam Hales and Lachlan McGeehan.
Related topics: Sam Hales, Lachlan McGeehan
Discography
What are some notable albums and singles by Confidence Man?
Confidence Man debuted with their album 'Confident Music for Confident People' in 2018. Their follow-up album 'Tilt' was released in 2022, which peaked at number 7 on the ARIA charts. Their notable singles include 'Bubblegum', 'Does It Make You Feel Good?', and 'First Class Bitch'. They are known for their vibrant, upbeat tracks and unique style.
Related topics: Confident Music for Confident People, Tilt (Confidence Man album)
Albums and Singles
What are the notable albums and singles by Confidence Man?
Notable albums by Confidence Man include 'Confident Music for Confident People', 'Tilt', and '3AM (La La La)'. Key singles include "Boyfriend (Repeat)", "Bubblegum", and "First Class Bitch". Their music is known for its upbeat and engaging sound, often gaining attention at festivals such as Glastonbury.
Related topics: Confident Music for Confident People, Tilt, 3AM (La La La), Glastonbury Festival

What awards and nominations has Confidence Man received?
Confidence Man has received numerous nominations and awards, including wins at the Queensland Music Awards for 'Electronic Song of the Year' and at the Rolling Stone UK Awards for 'The Live Act Award'. They have been recognized for both their artistic and commercial achievements across various music award platforms.
Related topics: Queensland Music Awards, Rolling Stone UK Awards

Background and Formation
What is Confidence Man and how did they start?
Confidence Man is an Australian indie electropop band formed in 2016 in Brisbane, Queensland. The band members, originally part of the Brisbane music scene, came from other bands including The Belligerents, Moses Gunn Collective, and The Jungle Giants. They chose to use stage names taken from tombstones and began by writing dance songs as a joke, which eventually led them to realize the material's potential.
Related topics: Brisbane, Electropop, The Belligerents, The Jungle Giants
Members
Who are the members of Confidence Man and what are their roles?
The band consists of Janet Planet (Grace Stephenson) and Sugar Bones (Aidan Moore) on vocals. Reggie Goodchild (Lewis Stephenson) is responsible for production, while Clarence McGuffie was initially developed by members Lachlan McGeehan and Sam Hales, with the current identity of the member known as McGuffie undisclosed.
Awards and Nominations
What awards and nominations has Confidence Man received?
Confidence Man has been nominated for several prestigious awards. They won the Best Independent Dance or Electronica Album award for 'Tilt' at the AIR Awards in 2023 and have been nominated for numerous others, including the ARIA Music Awards and MTV Europe Music Awards.
Related topics: AIR Awards, ARIA Music Awards, MTV Europe Music Awards
