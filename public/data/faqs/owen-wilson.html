FAQs: Owen Wilson

General Information
Who is Owen Wilson?
Owen Cunningham Wilson, born on November 18, 1968, in Dallas, Texas, is an American actor and comedian known for his collaboration with filmmaker Wes Anderson and his role in films such as 'Midnight in Paris,' 'Wedding Crashers,' and 'The Royal Tenenbaums.' He has received multiple award nominations, including an Academy Award nomination for Best Original Screenplay.

Early Life
What influenced Owen Wilson's early life?
Owen Wilson was born to photographer Laura Cunningham Wilson and advertising executive Robert Andrew Wilson. Growing up in Dallas, Texas, he was the middle child in a family with Irish descent. Owen and his brothers, Andrew and Luke, who are also actors, have always been under the influence of their creative family environment. He attended the New Mexico Military Institute after being expelled from school for cheating and then pursued a Bachelor of Arts degree in English at the University of Texas at Austin, where he met frequent collaborator Wes Anderson.
Related topics: Laura Wilson (photographer), New Mexico Military Institute, University of Texas at Austin
Career
What are the highlights of Owen Wilson's film career?
Owen Wilson has enjoyed a versatile film career distinguished by early collaborations with Wes Anderson on films like 'Bottle Rocket' and 'The Royal Tenenbaums.' His career skyrocketed with his role in 'Wedding Crashers,' and he became known for contributing to comedic hits alongside the Frat Pack, with 'Zoolander,' 'Starsky & Hutch,' and the 'Night at the Museum' series. Additionally, Wilson has lent his voice to animated characters, such as Lightning McQueen in Pixar's 'Cars' series.
Related topics: Bottle Rocket, The Royal Tenenbaums, Wedding Crashers, Zoolander, Frat Pack, Lightning McQueen
Personal Life
What led to struggles in Owen Wilson's personal life?
In 2007, Owen Wilson attempted suicide, leading to treatment for depression. This event attracted significant media attention and resulted in Wilson maintaining a more private personal life. Despite personal struggles, Wilson speaks highly of the support from his family, especially his brothers during his recovery, and in recent years, he has opened up more about this phase in interviews.
Related topics: Suicide attempt, Major depressive disorder
Achievements
What accolades has Owen Wilson received?
Owen Wilson's accolades include an Academy Award nomination for Best Original Screenplay for 'The Royal Tenenbaums,' as well as Golden Globe and Screen Actors Guild (SAG) nominations for his performances in 'Midnight in Paris' and 'The Grand Budapest Hotel.' His work in various genres has cemented his status as a versatile actor in Hollywood.
Related topics: Academy Award for Best Original Screenplay, Golden Globe Award, Screen Actors Guild Award
Filmography
What are some notable films in Owen Wilson's filmography?
Owen Wilson's extensive filmography includes critically acclaimed movies like 'Midnight in Paris,' 'The Royal Tenenbaums,' 'The Grand Budapest Hotel,' and successful comedies such as 'Wedding Crashers,' 'Zoolander,' and 'Meet the Parents.' He is also known for the action comedy 'Shanghai Noon' alongside Jackie Chan and the family-friendly 'Marley & Me.' He voices Lightning McQueen in Pixar's 'Cars' series.
Related topics: Midnight in Paris, The Grand Budapest Hotel, Wedding Crashers, Shanghai Noon, Marley & Me, Cars (franchise)
Recent Work
What recent projects has Owen Wilson been involved in?
Recently, Owen Wilson has continued to work on a mix of projects. In 2021, he appeared in the film 'Bliss' alongside Salma Hayek and reunited with Wes Anderson for 'The French Dispatch.' He stars in the Marvel Cinematic Universe series 'Loki' on Disney+ and reprised his role as Mobius M. Mobius in 'Ant-Man and the Wasp: Quantumania.' Additionally, he was involved in the UK sofa retailer Sofology's advertising campaign.
