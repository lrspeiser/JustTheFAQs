FAQs: Mangalavaaram

Release
When was 'Mangalavaaram' released and in what languages?
The film was released on November 17, 2023, and was available in Telugu, Kannada, Hindi, Tamil, and Malayalam languages. Its Telugu version was digitally premiered on Disney Plus Hotstar on December 26, 2023, while the Hindi version premiered on Zee Cinema on August 9, 2024.
Related topics: Disney+ Hotstar, Hindi language
Music
Who composed the music for 'Mangalavaaram'?
The music and background score for 'Mangalavaaram' were composed by B. Ajaneesh Loknath. The soundtrack album was released on December 15, 2023, featuring songs like 'Ganagana Mogalira', 'Emayyindho Emito', and 'Appadappada Thaandra'.
Related topics: B. Ajaneesh Loknath, Soundtrack album
Production
How was 'Mangalavaaram' developed?
Principal filming for 'Mangalavaaram' began on December 9, 2022, and wrapped up on June 13, 2023. Payal Rajput played the principal lead, collaborating for the second time with director Ajay Bhupathi after their previous film 'RX 100'.
Related topics: RX 100 (film)
Plot
What is the plot of 'Mangalavaaram'?
Set in 1996, 'Mangalavaaram' revolves around the investigation of a series of four mysterious suicides in the rural town of Mahalakshmipuram. The story reveals a web of intrigue involving illicit affairs, social stigma, and vengeance, with the dedicated police officer Maaya unveiling the truth behind these deaths. The film involves a complex narrative about a woman's struggle with her sexual desires and societal judgment, leading to a murder that triggers a series of revenge killings.
Related topics: Psychological thriller, Mystery film
Cast
Who are the main actors in 'Mangalavaaram'?
The film stars Payal Rajput as Sailaja, Nandita Swetha as Sub-Inspector Maaya, Priyadarshi Pulikonda as Ravi, Chaitanya Krishna as Zamindar Prakasam Babu, and Divya Pillai as Rajeswari Devi. The supporting cast includes Ajmal Ameer, Ravindra Vijay, and several others.
Related topics: Payal Rajput, Nandita Swetha, Priyadarshi Pulikonda, Ajmal Ameer
General Information
What is 'Mangalavaaram'?
'Mangalavaaram' is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language erotic psychological mystery thriller film. It was written and directed by Ajay Bhupathi and produced by Swathi Reddy Gunupati, Suresh Varma M., and Ajay Bhupathi under Mudhra Media Works and A Creative Works. The film features an ensemble cast, including Payal Rajput, Nandita Swetha, and Priyadarshi Pulikonda, among others.
Related topics: Ajay Bhupathi, Telugu language
