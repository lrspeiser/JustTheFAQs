FAQs: XXX: Return of Xander Cage

Box Office Performance
How did XXX: Return of Xander Cage perform at the box office?
The film was a commercial success, grossing over $346 million worldwide against an $85 million production budget. It performed exceptionally well in China, where strategic marketing efforts helped it become a box office hit, earning $61.9 million in its opening weekend alone.

General Information
What is XXX: Return of Xander Cage?
XXX: Return of Xander Cage is the third installment in the XXX film series, released in 2017. Directed by D.J. Caruso, the film stars Vin Diesel in the title role and features a high-octane storyline involving extreme sports and espionage. The plot follows Xander Cage as he comes out of exile to retrieve a powerful weapon called Pandora's Box.
Related topics: XXX (film series), D.J. Caruso, Vin Diesel
Production
What was the production process for XXX: Return of Xander Cage?
Production for the film involved filming at various locations, including Toronto, the Dominican Republic, and the Philippines. Initially, director Rob Cohen was to return, but eventually, D.J. Caruso took the helm. Filming officially began on February 2, 2016, and concluded by May 20, 2016.

Critical Reception
How was XXX: Return of Xander Cage received by critics?
The film received mixed reviews from critics. While it holds a 45% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes and a score of 42 out of 100 on Metacritic, many reviewers appreciated the high-octane action sequences, although they critiqued the storyline for being repetitive and lacking innovation. Despite this, audiences gave it a CinemaScore of "A-".
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic, CinemaScore
Plot
What is the plot of XXX: Return of Xander Cage?
The plot revolves around Xander Cage coming out of self-imposed exile to face off against Xiang, a fellow XXX agent, to recover a device known as Pandora's Box, capable of controlling satellites. The narrative unfolds with a series of action-packed events leading to a battle to save the world from chaos.

Sequel Plans
Is there a sequel to XXX: Return of Xander Cage?
A sequel to XXX: Return of Xander Cage is planned, with Vin Diesel expected to return as Xander Cage. The project has encountered delays due to financial issues with The H Collective, which holds the film rights.
