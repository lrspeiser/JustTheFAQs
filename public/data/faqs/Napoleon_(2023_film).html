FAQs: Napoleon (2023 film)

Overview
What is the 2023 film 'Napoleon' about?
The 2023 film 'Napoleon' is an epic biographical war film directed by Ridley Scott, focusing on the life of Napoleon Bonaparte. It primarily depicts Napoleon's rise to power, his military campaigns, and his complex relationship with his wife, Joséphine. Joaquin Phoenix stars as Napoleon, and Vanessa Kirby plays Joséphine.
Related topics: Napoleon, Ridley Scott, Joaquin Phoenix, Vanessa Kirby, Joséphine de Beauharnais
Reception and Reviews
How was 'Napoleon' received by critics and at the box office?
'Napoleon' received mixed reviews. Critics praised its epic scale, battle scenes, and performances of Joaquin Phoenix and Vanessa Kirby, but criticized its pacing and historical inaccuracies. The film grossed $221.4 million worldwide, which did not cover its budget of $130–200 million, leading to an estimated loss of around $165 million.
Related topics: Joaquin Phoenix, Vanessa Kirby
Plot Summary
How does 'Napoleon' depict the French leader's life and rise to power?
The film begins in 1793 during the French Revolution, showcasing key events like the Siege of Toulon and Napoleon's rise as First Consul following the Coup of 18 Brumaire. It follows his coronation as Emperor, major battles including Austerlitz and Waterloo, personal relationships, and his eventual exile. Through these events, Napoleon's strategic brilliance and personal struggles are portrayed.
Related topics: Siege of Toulon (1793), Coup of 18 Brumaire, Battle of Austerlitz, Battle of Waterloo
Production
Where was 'Napoleon' filmed and what were some of the challenges in its production?
'Napoleon' was filmed in various locations across England, including Lincoln Cathedral and Blenheim Palace, and in international locations like Malta and Morocco. The film featured six major battle sequences and faced challenges such as the COVID-19 pandemic impacting casting and scheduling.
Related topics: Lincoln Cathedral, Blenheim Palace, COVID-19 pandemic
Cast and Characters
Who stars in the film 'Napoleon'?
The film stars Joaquin Phoenix as Napoleon Bonaparte and Vanessa Kirby as Empress Joséphine. Other notable cast members include Tahar Rahim as Paul Barras, Rupert Everett as Arthur Wellesley, and Ben Miles as Armand de Caulaincourt.
Related topics: Joaquin Phoenix, Vanessa Kirby, Tahar Rahim, Rupert Everett, Ben Miles
Historical Accuracy
What historical inaccuracies are present in the film 'Napoleon'?
The film faces criticism for several historical inaccuracies, such as depicting Napoleon firing on the Pyramids of Giza, which never happened, and fictional meetings between Napoleon and Wellington post-defeat. It also doesn't fully represent important aspects of Napoleon's life, like his legal reforms and non-military achievements.
Related topics: Pyramids of Giza, Duke of Wellington
