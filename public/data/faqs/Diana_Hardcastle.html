FAQs: Diana Hardcastle

Filmography
What are some films in which Diana Hardcastle has acted?
Diana Hardcastle has acted in several films, including If Only (2004) as Claire, A Good Woman (2004) as Lady Plymdale, Chromophobia (2005) as Mrs. Wharton, The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel (2011) and its sequel as Carol Parr, and The Boy (2016) as Mrs. Heelshire.
Related topics: If Only (2004 film), A Good Woman (film), Chromophobia (film), The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel, The Boy (2016 film)
Personal Life
What is known about Diana Hardcastle's family?
Diana Hardcastle was married to actor Tom Wilkinson until his death in December 2023. The couple had two daughters, Alice, born in 1988, and Molly, born in 1991.
Related topics: Tom Wilkinson
Career
Has Diana Hardcastle collaborated with her husband Tom Wilkinson in any projects?
Yes, Diana Hardcastle and her husband Tom Wilkinson have appeared together in multiple projects. They played the real-life couple Joe and Rose Kennedy in the miniseries The Kennedys. They were also both cast in the film The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel; however, their characters did not interact. Additionally, they portrayed a couple in the film Good People and siblings-in-law in the TV series Belgravia.
Related topics: Tom Wilkinson, The Kennedys (miniseries), The Best Exotic Marigold Hotel, Good People (film), Belgravia (TV series)
Introduction
Who is Diana Hardcastle?
Diana Hardcastle is an English actress born on 12 July 1949 in London, England. She has predominantly worked in television and has been active in the acting industry since 1982. She was married to the English actor Tom Wilkinson, with whom she has two daughters.
Career
In which notable TV series has Diana Hardcastle appeared?
Diana Hardcastle has appeared in various notable TV series such as Midsomer Murders, Inspector Lynley, Taggart, First Among Equals, and Fortunes of War. She also played Rose Kennedy in the miniseries The Kennedys.
Related topics: Midsomer Murders, Inspector Lynley, Taggart, First Among Equals (TV series), Fortunes of War (TV series), The Kennedys (miniseries)
