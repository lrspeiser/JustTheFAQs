FAQs: Migration (2023 film)

General Information
What is the premise of the film 'Migration'?
'Migration' is a 2023 animated adventure comedy film that revolves around a family of mallards trying to convince their overprotective father, Mack, to go on a vacation of a lifetime. They plan to migrate from New England, through New York City, to Jamaica. Along their journey, they encounter various characters and challenges that test their family's resilience and bonds.
Related topics: Mallard, New England, New York City, Jamaica
Release
How did 'Migration' perform at the box office and in its critical reception?
'Migration' grossed $299.9 million worldwide, with $127.3 million from the United States and Canada and $172.6 million from other territories. Critically, the film received generally positive reviews, scoring 73% on Rotten Tomatoes with praise for its animation and voice cast, although some criticism was directed at the story's originality. It was noted for its beautiful animation and strong voice performances.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes
Production
Who are the key creators behind the movie 'Migration'?
The film 'Migration' was directed by Benjamin Renner, with a screenplay by Mike White. Benjamin Renner is known for traditional animation and had to adapt his style for this computer-animated film. Mike White, a seasoned writer, contributed to both the screenplay and the story. The music was composed by John Powell, marking his second collaboration with Illumination.
Related topics: Benjamin Renner, Mike White (filmmaker), John Powell (film composer)
Voice Cast
Who are the main voice actors in 'Migration'?
The main voice actors in 'Migration' include Kumail Nanjiani as Mack Mallard, Elizabeth Banks as Pam Mallard, Keegan-Michael Key as Delroy, Awkwafina as Chump, and Danny DeVito as Uncle Dan. Caspar Jennings and Tresi Gazal provided the voices for Dax and Gwen Mallard, respectively.
Related topics: Kumail Nanjiani, Elizabeth Banks, Keegan-Michael Key, Awkwafina, Danny DeVito
Plot
What obstacles do the Mallard family face during their migration?
The Mallard family faces several obstacles during their migration journey, including convincing Mack, the family's overprotective father, to leave their pond. They encounter a crazed elderly heron named Erin, a gang of pigeons in New York City, and even rescue a macaw named Delroy in Jamaica. They also find themselves trapped in a duck farm run by a chef intent on capturing them, but ultimately band together to escape and reach Jamaica safely.
Related topics: Catfish, Scarlet macaw, American Pekin
