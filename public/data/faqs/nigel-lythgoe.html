FAQs: Nigel Lythgoe

Biography and Career
Who is Nigel Lythgoe and what is he best known for?
Nigel Lythgoe is an English television and film director and producer, well known for his role as a judge on dance competition shows and as a producer of major talent competitions like Pop Idol and American Idol. He created Superstars of Dance and So You Think You Can Dance, serving as a permanent judge for the first sixteen seasons.
Related topics: Pop Idol, American Idol, So You Think You Can Dance
Biography and Career
What was Nigel Lythgoe's early life and background in dance?
Nigel Lythgoe was born in Wallasey, Cheshire, England into a family where his father worked as a dockworker. He developed an interest in dance at age ten, studying various dance styles such as tap, ballet, and modern jazz. His first professional role was with the English National Ballet. Lythgoe's technical expertise in choreography and filming techniques contributed to his successful television career.
Related topics: Wallasey, English National Ballet, The Young Generation
Contributions to Television
How did Nigel Lythgoe influence television reality shows?
Nigel Lythgoe played a significant role in global television through his work as a producer and judge on critical reality and talent shows. He was integral to the success of Pop Idol, which revolutionized the music talent show format and inspired the global Idol franchise. Lythgoe also took American Idol to great heights, being pivotal during his time as executive producer.
Related topics: American Idol, Popstars, Idol (TV series)
Contributions to Television
What is BritWeek, and how is Nigel Lythgoe connected to it?
Nigel Lythgoe co-founded BritWeek in 2007, an annual series of events in Los Angeles and Orange County that celebrate the British influence in various sectors like entertainment and business in California.

Personal Life
Who is Nigel Lythgoe's family?
Nigel Lythgoe was married to Bonita Shawe, a fellow dancer, from 1974 until their divorce in 2010. They have two sons, Simon and Kris Lythgoe, who are also involved in the entertainment industry. Lythgoe owns a vineyard in Paso Robles, California, named Villa San Juliette.
Related topics: Bonnie Lythgoe, Simon Lythgoe, Kris Lythgoe
Controversies
What were the sexual assault allegations made against Nigel Lythgoe?
Nigel Lythgoe faced multiple allegations of sexual assault, including a lawsuit filed by Paula Abdul and two contestants from the show All American Girl. Abdul accused Lythgoe of inappropriate actions during her time on American Idol and So You Think You Can Dance. Lythgoe denied these claims, and the lawsuit with Abdul was settled privately. However, other lawsuits continued to emerge, leading to his departure from So You Think You Can Dance.
Related topics: So You Think You Can Dance, American Idol
Awards and Honors
What recognitions has Nigel Lythgoe received in his career?
Nigel Lythgoe was awarded the Officer of the Order of the British Empire (OBE) in 2015 and the Ellis Island International Medal of Honor in 2014. He also received the Founders Award at the International Emmy Awards in 2011, highlighting his contributions to the television industry.
Related topics: Order of the British Empire, International Emmy Founders Award
