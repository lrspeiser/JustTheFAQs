FAQs: Kevin Von Erich

Later Career and Retirement
What has Kevin Von Erich done post-retirement?
Post-retirement, Kevin Von Erich has made sporadic wrestling appearances, notably with his sons. He participated in the 2009 WWE Hall of Fame ceremony, representing the Von Erich family. Kevin has been involved in documentaries about his family's legacy and licensing World Class Championship Wrestling footage to WWE. In recent years, he has supported his sons in their wrestling endeavors.

Personal Life
How is Kevin Von Erich's family life?
Kevin Von Erich married Pam Adkisson on August 1, 1980, and they reside in Hawaii, where they run a family investment business. They have four children, including sons Ross and Marshall Von Erich, who are both wrestlers. Kevin is the last surviving son of Fritz Von Erich, with his brothers having faced untimely deaths.
Related topics: Marshall and Ross Von Erich, Fritz Von Erich
Personal Life and Family
Who is Kevin Von Erich?
Kevin Von Erich, born Kevin Ross Adkisson on May 15, 1957, is a retired American professional wrestler best known for his time in World Class Championship Wrestling (WCCW). He is a member of the legendary Von Erich family, a prominent wrestling family. Kevin was trained by his father, Fritz Von Erich, and started wrestling in the late 70s. He is the last surviving son of Fritz Von Erich.
Related topics: Fritz Von Erich, Von Erich family, World Class Championship Wrestling
Wrestling Career Highlights
What are some of Kevin Von Erich's notable achievements in wrestling?
Kevin Von Erich enjoyed a successful wrestling career, becoming a crowd favorite in the Dallas territory of the National Wrestling Alliance (NWA). He was known particularly for wrestling barefoot and his high-flying moves. Kevin held numerous titles, including the NWA American Heavyweight Championship, and was part of the iconic Von Erich- Freebird feud, considered one of the best rivalries in wrestling history.
Related topics: NWA American Heavyweight Championship, Von Erich family, Fabulous Freebirds
Professional Wrestling Career
What is the significance of the Freebird-Von Erich feud?
The Freebird-Von Erich feud, spanning 1982-1984, is a legendary rivalry in professional wrestling, known for its intense matches and high drama. It began when the Fabulous Freebirds cost Kerry Von Erich the NWA World Heavyweight Championship in a controversial cage match. This feud elevated both teams' statuses and is considered a major storyline in wrestling history.
