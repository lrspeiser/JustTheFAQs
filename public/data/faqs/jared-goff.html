FAQs: Jared Goff

Introduction
Who is Jared Goff?
Jared Thomas Goff, born on October 14, 1994, is an American professional football quarterback for the Detroit Lions of the National Football League (NFL). He played college football for the California Golden Bears, where he set numerous passing records, and was selected first overall by the Los Angeles Rams in the 2016 NFL Draft. Goff is known for his strong arm and ability to lead teams to notable victories, including leading the Rams to Super Bowl LIII.
Related topics: California Golden Bears football, Los Angeles Rams, 2016 NFL draft, NFL
Collegiate Career
What were some highlights from Jared Goff's college football career?
Jared Goff had a standout college football career at the University of California, Berkeley. He set 26 Cal records, including passing yards, touchdown passes, and completions. Known for his prowess in reading the field and his precision throwing, Goff completed his college career second only to Aaron Rodgers on Cal's all-time list for passing efficiency.
Related topics: California Golden Bears football, Pac-12 Conference, Aaron Rodgers
Professional Career: Los Angeles Rams
How did Jared Goff perform after being drafted by the Los Angeles Rams?
Drafted first overall in the 2016 NFL Draft by the Los Angeles Rams, Jared Goff faced initial challenges but eventually led the team to the playoffs in 2017, helping end their long postseason drought. He gained reputation for comebacks and an ability to thrive in high-pressure situations, including leading the Rams to the NFC Championship and an appearance in Super Bowl LIII against the New England Patriots.
Related topics: Los Angeles Rams, 2016 NFL draft, Super Bowl LIII
Professional Career: Detroit Lions
What impact did Jared Goff have on the Detroit Lions?
Jared Goff was traded to the Detroit Lions in 2021, where he struggled initially but showcased resilience and leadership skills. By his third season, Goff led the Lions to their first playoff win since 1991 and helped the team reach the NFC Championship Game, breaking a long-standing playoff win drought for the franchise.
Related topics: Detroit Lions, NFC Championship Game
NFL Records and Achievements
What NFL records and achievements does Jared Goff hold?
Jared Goff holds several NFL records, including tying the record for most completions in a regular season game (45) with Drew Bledsoe. He also set the record for most pass attempts with a perfect passer rating and became the first quarterback in NFL history to achieve a 100% completion rate in a game with more than 10 pass attempts. Additionally, Goff has been selected to the Pro Bowl multiple times and was named the PFWA Most Improved Player in 2017.
Related topics: NFL records, Pro Bowl, Drew Bledsoe
Personal Life
What is known about Jared Goff's personal life?
Outside of football, Jared Goff has a notable personal life. He launched a clothing line, JG16, and his engagement and subsequent marriage to Christen Harper, a model, have been spotlighted. Goff also made headlines for his philanthropic efforts, such as donating $250,000 to the Los Angeles Regional Food Bank during the COVID-19 pandemic.
