FAQs: Margot Robbie

Early Life and Education
Where was Margot Robbie born and raised?
Margot Elise Robbie was born on July 2, 1990, in Dalby, Queensland, Australia. She was raised by her single mother, Sarie Kessler, on her grandparents' farm in Currumbin Valley, located in the Gold Coast hinterland. Robbie has two older siblings, Anya and Lachlan, and a younger brother, Cameron. Her parents separated when she was five, and she had minimal contact with her father.
Related topics: Dalby, Queensland, Sarie Kessler, Doug Robbie
Early Life and Education
What kind of education did Margot Robbie receive?
Margot Robbie excelled in trapeze at circus school, receiving a certificate at age eight. She attended Somerset College, where she studied drama. During her teenage years, she worked three jobs to support herself, including bar tending, house cleaning, and working at Subway. After graduating high school, Robbie relocated to Melbourne to pursue acting professionally.
Related topics: Somerset College
Career
How did Margot Robbie begin her acting career?
Margot Robbie began her acting career in high school, appearing in two low-budget independent films, "Vigilante" and "I.C.U." Her first television job was a guest role on "City Homicide" in 2008, followed by an arc in "The Elephant Princess." Her breakthrough came on the Australian soap opera "Neighbours," where she played Donna Freedman, a character initially intended to be a guest but was made regular due to her performance.
Related topics: Neighbours, City Homicide, The Elephant Princess
Breakthrough and Recognition
What was Margot Robbie's breakout role?
Margot Robbie's breakout role was as Naomi Lapaglia in Martin Scorsese's film "The Wolf of Wall Street" (2013), where she starred alongside Leonardo DiCaprio. Her performance received critical acclaim, with praise for her convincing Brooklyn accent and screen presence. The film was a box office success and established Robbie as a prominent actress in Hollywood.
Related topics: The Wolf of Wall Street (2013 film), Martin Scorsese, Leonardo DiCaprio
Established Actress and Producer
What are some notable roles and achievements Margot Robbie has had from 2016 to present?
Since her breakout, Margot Robbie has gained worldwide recognition with roles like Harley Quinn in the "Suicide Squad" film series and was critically acclaimed for her performance as Tonya Harding in "I, Tonya." As a producer, she co-founded LuckyChap Entertainment, which produced successful films like "I, Tonya" and "Barbie." Other significant roles include "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood," "Bombshell," and "Barbie," with the latter earning over $1.4 billion worldwide.
Related topics: Suicide Squad (2016 film), Once Upon a Time in Hollywood, LuckyChap Entertainment
Personal Life
Who is Margot Robbie's husband and when did they get married?
Margot Robbie is married to Tom Ackerley, a British assistant director. They met on the set of "Suite Française" in 2013 and began dating in 2014. The couple got married in a private ceremony in December 2016 in Byron Bay, Australia. They currently reside in Venice Beach, California.
Related topics: Tom Ackerley, Suite Française (film)
Philanthropic Efforts
What causes and philanthropic efforts is Margot Robbie known for supporting?
Margot Robbie actively supports human rights, women's rights, gender equality, and LGBT rights. She participates in fundraising for the Motion Picture & Television Fund and has been involved in events raising funds globally. Robbie also stood for same-sex marriage in Australia and supports the Time's Up initiative.
Related topics: Motion Picture & Television Fund, Time's Up (organization)
