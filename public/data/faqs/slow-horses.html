FAQs: Slow Horses

Overview
What is "Slow Horses" about?
"Slow Horses" is a British spy thriller television series based on Mick Herron's "Slough House" series of novels. The show revolves around a group of MI5 agents who have been marginalized due to professional blunders, relegated to a forgotten branch of MI5 called Slough House. Despite being deemed the "slow horses," these agents end up entangled in significant investigations.
Related topics: MI5, Spy fiction, Slough House (novel series)
Premise
What is the premise of "Slow Horses"?
The series centers around Slough House, an administrative exiled center for MI5 rejects. These 'slow horses' are agents who have erred significantly but were not outright fired. Under the direction of their irritable leader, Jackson Lamb, they continue perform mundane tasks while occasionally being drawn into dangerous and important cases.
Related topics: MI5
Cast and Characters
Who stars in the "Slow Horses" series?
The series stars Gary Oldman as Jackson Lamb, Jack Lowden as River Cartwright, Kristin Scott Thomas as Diana Taverner, and includes other notable actors such as Sophie Okonedo, Jonathan Pryce, and Hugo Weaving among others. The cast portrays various past-their-prime MI5 agents and their leaders within Slough House.
Related topics: Gary Oldman, Jack Lowden, Kristin Scott Thomas, Sophie Okonedo, Jonathan Pryce, Hugo Weaving
Episodes
What is the episode structure of the "Slow Horses" series?
"Slow Horses" has aired four series each deriving its storyline from Mick Herron's novels, with each series containing six episodes. Each episode follows a case or thematic element nodding towards espionage errors, espionage intrigue, and the resulting personal fallout.
Related topics: Mick Herron
Production
How did "Slow Horses" come to be, and who is involved in its production?
The series was ordered by Apple TV+ in October 2019. Gary Oldman was originally announced to star, along with later additions Jack Lowden, Kristin Scott Thomas, and more. The show's theme music includes a collaboration with Mick Jagger and composer Daniel Pemberton.
Related topics: Apple TV+, Mick Jagger, Daniel Pemberton
Reception
How has "Slow Horses" been received by critics and audiences?
"Slow Horses" has been critically acclaimed, with each series receiving high scores on Rotten Tomatoes, ranging from 95% to 100%, and similarly positive reviews on Metacritic. The show has been praised for reinvigorating the espionage genre with a mix of humor and drama, bolstered by Gary Oldman's performance.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic
Awards and Nominations
Has "Slow Horses" received any awards or nominations?
Yes, "Slow Horses" has garnered various nominations and awards, including BAFTA nominations for Best Actor and Best Supporting Actor, and several Emmy nominations such as for Outstanding Drama Series. The show has won awards for Best Adapted Screenplay and other technical categories.
Related topics: BAFTA, Emmy Awards
