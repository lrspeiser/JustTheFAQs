FAQs: Carey Mulligan

Personal Life
Who is Carey Mulligan married to and how did they meet?
Carey Mulligan is married to Marcus Mumford, lead singer of Mumford & Sons. They were childhood pen pals who lost touch and reconnected as adults. They married on April 21, 2012, shortly after working together on the film 'Inside Llewyn Davis'.
Related topics: Marcus Mumford, Mumford & Sons, Inside Llewyn Davis
Early Life and Background
Where was Carey Mulligan born and what is her family background?
Carey Mulligan was born in London on May 28, 1985. Her father, Stephen Mulligan, is a hotel manager of Irish descent from Liverpool, and her mother, Nano Mulligan, is a university lecturer from Llandeilo, Wales. Carey's maternal grandfather, Denzil Booth, served as a naval radar artillery officer during World War II.
Related topics: Nano Mulligan, Stephen Mulligan, Denzil Booth
Philanthropy
What charitable causes does Carey Mulligan support?
Carey Mulligan is an ambassador for the Alzheimer's Society and War Child. She has been involved in raising awareness and funding for Alzheimer's research and efforts to help children affected by war.
Related topics: Alzheimer's Society, War Child
Awards and Honors
What notable awards has Carey Mulligan won?
Carey Mulligan has won numerous awards, including a British Academy Film Award and a Critics' Choice Movie Award. She has also received three Academy Award nominations and four Golden Globe nominations. In 2024, she was appointed Commander of the Order of the British Empire (CBE) for her services to drama.
Related topics: British Academy Film Award, Critics' Choice Movie Award, Academy Awards, Golden Globe Awards, Commander of the Order of the British Empire
Career Highlights
What was Carey Mulligan's breakthrough role?
Carey Mulligan's breakthrough role was as Jenny in the 2009 film 'An Education'. Her performance was highly praised, earning her a BAFTA Award for Best Actress and nominations for an Academy Award, a Golden Globe, and a Screen Actors Guild Award.
Related topics: An Education, BAFTA Award for Best Actress
Filmography
Which film marked Carey Mulligan's highest-grossing release?
Carey Mulligan's highest-grossing film is 'The Great Gatsby' (2013), in which she starred as Daisy Buchanan. The film was directed by Baz Luhrmann and co-starred Leonardo DiCaprio.
