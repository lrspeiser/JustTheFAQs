FAQs: Michael Irvin

Career Overview
Who is Michael Irvin?
Michael Jerome Irvin is an American sports commentator and former professional football player, known for his tenure as a wide receiver for the Dallas Cowboys in the NFL. He is a Pro Football Hall of Famer and was nicknamed 'The Playmaker' for his ability to make big plays.
Related topics: American football, Dallas Cowboys, Pro Football Hall of Fame
College Career
How did Michael Irvin perform during his college career?
At the University of Miami, Irvin set school records for career receptions, receiving yards, and touchdown receptions and was part of the 1987 national championship team. He announced he was entering the 1988 NFL draft after his junior year.
Related topics: Miami Hurricanes football, 1988 NFL draft
Professional Career
What achievements did Michael Irvin have in his NFL career?
Michael Irvin was instrumental in leading the Dallas Cowboys to three Super Bowl victories. He was a five-time Pro Bowler, and he set franchise records for receptions and receiving yards during his 12-year career. He retired after a cervical spine injury in 1999.
Related topics: Dallas Cowboys, Super Bowl, Pro Bowl
Records and Honors
What are Michael Irvin's most notable records and honors?
Irvin holds the Dallas Cowboys franchise records for catches and receiving yards. He was inducted into both the Dallas Cowboys Ring of Honor and the Pro Football Hall of Fame. He was also named to the NFL's 1990s All-Decade Team.
Related topics: Dallas Cowboys Ring of Honor, Pro Football Hall of Fame, NFL 1990s All-Decade Team
Life After NFL
What has Michael Irvin been involved in after his NFL career?
Michael Irvin has worked as a football analyst for the NFL Network and ESPN. He participated in 'Dancing with the Stars' and co-hosts the FS1 debate show 'Speak'.
Related topics: NFL Network, ESPN
Personal Life
What is known about Michael Irvin's personal life?
Michael Irvin is married to Sandy Harrell, and they have three children together. He is a Christian and cites turning his life over to Christ in 2001.
Related topics: Christianity
Controversies
What controversies has Michael Irvin been involved in?
Irvin has faced several controversies, including accusations of sexual assault and drug-related incidents. Notably, a 1996 allegation was proven false, and he was arrested for cocaine possession in 2001, though charges were later dropped.
Related topics: Sexual assault, Cocaine
Entertainment Career
What roles has Michael Irvin taken in the entertainment industry?
Michael Irvin has ventured into acting, with roles in films like the remake of 'The Longest Yard' and as a host of the reality show '4th and Long'. He has also appeared on 'Pros vs. Joes'.
Related topics: The Longest Yard (2005 film), Pros vs. Joes
