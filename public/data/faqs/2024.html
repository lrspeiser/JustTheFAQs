What were the significant political changes in Bangladesh and South Korea in 2024?
In Bangladesh, Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina resigned following national protests, which led to the interim government led by Nobel laureate Muhammad Yunus. In South Korea, the attempts of President Yoon Suk Yeol to declare martial law resulted in a political crisis leading to his impeachment in December 2024.

Significant Events
What major armed conflicts continued in 2024?
The year 2024 saw the continuation of major armed conflicts such as the Russian invasion of Ukraine, the Myanmar civil war, and the Sudanese civil war. Additionally, heavy fighting resumed in the Syrian civil war, culminating in the fall of Ba'athist Syria and the departure of Bashar al-Assad from Syria by December.

Scientific Achievements
What discoveries led to Nobel Prizes in 2024?
The Nobel Prize in Chemistry was awarded for advancements in computational and protein design along with protein structure prediction. The Nobel Peace Prize was recognized for efforts in achieving a world free of nuclear weapons. The Nobel Prize in Physics celebrated foundational discoveries enabling machine learning with artificial neural networks.
Related topics: Nobel Prize in Chemistry, Nobel Peace Prize, Nobel Prize in Physics
Natural Disasters
What was the impact of the 2024 Noto Earthquake in Japan?
The 2024 Noto Earthquake, with a magnitude of 7.5, struck the western coast of Japan, specifically the Noto Peninsula, causing significant destruction, at least 462 fatalities, and extensive injuries. It was notable for subsequent fires in the city of Wajima.
Related topics: 2024 Noto earthquake, Japan, Noto Peninsula
Significant Events
How did the 2024 U.S. presidential election conclude?
In the 2024 United States presidential election, Donald Trump was elected again for a non-consecutive term, repeating history similar to Grover Cleveland's second term election in 1892. He was the first U.S. president to be elected to a non-consecutive second term in 132 years.
Related topics: 2024 United States presidential election, Donald Trump

Natural Disasters
How did Hurricane Helene impact the United States?
Hurricane Helene, marked as the deadliest Atlantic hurricane since 2017's Hurricane Maria, made landfall in Florida, causing 236 casualties and leaving over 685 missing. It was a category four hurricane upon landfall.
Related topics: Hurricane Helene, Hurricane Maria
