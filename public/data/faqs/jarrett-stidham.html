FAQs: Jarrett Stidham

Personal Information
Who is Jarrett Stidham?
Jarrett Ryan Stidham, born August 8, 1996, is an American professional football quarterback for the Denver Broncos of the NFL. He was born in Corbin, Kentucky, and later moved to Stephenville, Texas.

Early Life
Where did Jarrett Stidham grow up and attend high school?
Jarrett Stidham grew up in Corbin, Kentucky, before moving to Stephenville, Texas, where he attended Stephenville High School. As a senior, he completed 183 of 260 passes for 2,934 yards and 35 touchdowns and also rushed for 969 yards with 15 touchdowns.
Related topics: Stephenville High School, Corbin, Kentucky, Stephenville, Texas
College Career
Where did Jarrett Stidham play college football?
Jarrett Stidham played college football at Baylor University in 2015 and then transferred to Auburn University, where he played from 2017 to 2018.
Related topics: Baylor Bears football, Auburn Tigers football
Professional Career
How did Jarrett Stidham begin his NFL career?
Jarrett Stidham was selected by the New England Patriots in the fourth round of the 2019 NFL Draft and spent his first three seasons there as a backup quarterback. He later played for the Las Vegas Raiders before joining the Denver Broncos in 2023.
Related topics: New England Patriots, Las Vegas Raiders, Denver Broncos, 2019 NFL draft
Career Highlights
What are some of Jarrett Stidham's career highlights?
Jarrett Stidham's career highlights include being named SEC Newcomer of the Year in 2017, Second-team All-SEC in 2017, and MVP of the 2018 Music City Bowl.

Personal Life
Who is Jarrett Stidham married to?
Jarrett Stidham is married to Kennedy Stidham, the daughter of Harris Blitzer Sports & Entertainment CEO Tad Brown. They were married in 2019 and have a daughter born in 2022.
