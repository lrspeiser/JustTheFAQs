FAQs: Rick Astley

Discography
What are some of Rick Astley's popular albums?
Some of Rick Astley's popular albums include 'Whenever You Need Somebody' (1987), 'Hold Me in Your Arms' (1988), and '50' (2016), the latter marking his return to the music scene after a long break.
Related topics: Whenever You Need Somebody, Hold Me in Your Arms (album), 50 (Rick Astley album)
Personal Life
Who is Rick Astley married to?
Rick Astley has been married to film producer Lene Bausager since 2003. They met in 1987 while she was working as a promoter with RCA.
Introduction
Who is Rick Astley?
Rick Astley, born Richard Paul Astley on February 6, 1966, is a British singer known for his deep voice and hits like "Never Gonna Give You Up." He is also a radio DJ and podcaster.
Related topics: Never Gonna Give You Up
Internet Phenomenon
How did Rick Astley become an internet sensation?
In 2007, Rick Astley became an internet sensation through the 'Rickrolling' meme, which involves tricking someone into watching the music video for "Never Gonna Give You Up," reigniting his career with renewed fame.
Related topics: Rickrolling
Career Highlights
What was Rick Astley's breakthrough song?
Rick Astley's breakthrough song was "Never Gonna Give You Up," which became a number one hit in 25 countries and was awarded Best British Single at the 1988 Brit Awards.
