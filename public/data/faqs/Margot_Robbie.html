FAQs: Margot Robbie

Philanthropy and Other Work
What charitable causes does Margot Robbie support?
Margot Robbie is a vocal supporter of human rights, gender equality, and LGBT rights. Through her production company, she promotes female storytellers. Personal Life
Who is Margot Robbie married to and where do they reside?
Margot Robbie is married to Tom Ackerley, whom she met on the set of 'Suite Française' in 2013. They began dating in 2014, and married in a private ceremony in Byron Bay, Australia, in December 2016. The couple lives in Venice Beach, California.

Breakthrough and Recognition
What was Margot Robbie's breakout role?
Margot Robbie's breakout role was as Naomi Lapaglia in Martin Scorsese's film "The Wolf of Wall Street" (2013), where she starred alongside Leonardo DiCaprio. Her performance received critical acclaim, with praise for her convincing Brooklyn accent and screen presence. The film was a box office success and established Robbie as a prominent actress in Hollywood.
Related topics: The Wolf of Wall Street (2013 film), Martin Scorsese, Leonardo DiCaprio
Breakthrough and Success
What was Margot Robbie's breakthrough role in Hollywood?
Margot Robbie's breakthrough role in Hollywood came with Martin Scorsese's 'The Wolf of Wall Street' (2013), where she played Naomi Lapaglia, the wife of the protagonist Jordan Belfort, portrayed by Leonardo DiCaprio. Her audition was notable for an improvised slap on DiCaprio, which helped her secure the role. Robbie's performance was critically acclaimed and led to several award nominations and wins.
Related topics: The Wolf of Wall Street (2013 film), Leonardo DiCaprio, Jordan Belfort
Early Life and Education
What kind of education did Margot Robbie receive?
Margot Robbie excelled in trapeze at circus school, receiving a certificate at age eight. She attended Somerset College, where she studied drama. During her teenage years, she worked three jobs to support herself, including bar tending, house cleaning, and working at Subway. After graduating high school, Robbie relocated to Melbourne to pursue acting professionally.
Related topics: Somerset College
Early Life and Education
Where was Margot Robbie born and raised?
Margot Elise Robbie was born on July 2, 1990, in Dalby, Queensland, Australia. She was raised by her single mother, Sarie Kessler, on her grandparents' farm in Currumbin Valley, located in the Gold Coast hinterland. Robbie has two older siblings, Anya and Lachlan, and a younger brother, Cameron. Her parents separated when she was five, and she had minimal contact with her father.
Related topics: Dalby, Queensland, Sarie Kessler, Doug Robbie

Established Actress and Producer
What are some notable roles and achievements Margot Robbie has had from 2016 to present?
Since her breakout, Margot Robbie has gained worldwide recognition with roles like Harley Quinn in the "Suicide Squad" film series and was critically acclaimed for her performance as Tonya Harding in "I, Tonya." As a producer, she co-founded LuckyChap Entertainment, which produced successful films like "I, Tonya" and "Barbie." Other significant roles include "Once Upon a Time in Hollywood," "Bombshell," and "Barbie," with the latter earning over $1.4 billion worldwide.

Career Beginnings
How did Margot Robbie start her acting career?
Margot Robbie's acting career began with minor roles in low-budget independent thriller films, including 'Vigilante' and 'I.C.U.', during her high school years. She gained her first significant television exposure with a guest role in the series 'City Homicide' and a two-episode stint in 'The Elephant Princess'. After persistent calls to FremantleMedia, she landed the role of Donna Freedman in the soap opera 'Neighbours', which was initially a guest role but became a regular part due to her performance.

Career
How did Margot Robbie begin her acting career?
Margot Robbie began her acting career in high school, appearing in two low-budget independent films, "Vigilante" and "I.C.U." Established Actress and Producer
How did Margot Robbie establish herself as a producer in Hollywood?
Margot Robbie, alongside her husband Tom Ackerley and friends, co-founded the production company LuckyChap Entertainment in 2014. The company focuses on female-driven stories and has produced several successful films, including 'I, Tonya', 'Promising Young Woman', and 'Barbie'. These ventures have solidified Robbie's status as a prominent producer in Hollywood.
Related topics: LuckyChap Entertainment, Promising Young Woman, Barbie (film)
