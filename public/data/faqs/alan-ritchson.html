Introduction
Who is Alan Ritchson?
Alan Michael Ritchson, born on November 28, 1982, in Grand Forks, North Dakota, is an American actor known for his roles in television and film. He gained fame as Aquaman on the TV series 'Smallville' and later appeared in series like 'Blue Mountain State' and 'Titans'. Ritchson is widely recognized for portraying the title character in the Amazon Prime Video series 'Reacher'.

Early life and education
What is known about Alan Ritchson's early life and education?
Alan Ritchson was born to a high school teacher and a U.S. Air Force chief master sergeant in Grand Forks, North Dakota. He grew up moving around because of his father's military career, finally settling in Niceville, Florida. He graduated from Niceville High School in 2001 and attended Okaloosa Walton Community College, earning an Associate of Arts degree. He initially started college on a full music scholarship but left to pursue modeling in Los Angeles.
