FAQs: List of Dallas Cowboys Head Coaches

Historical Highlights
What are some significant achievements of Tom Landry as a head coach?
Tom Landry was the all-time leader in games coached and wins for the Cowboys. He won two Super Bowls, five NFC Championships, and 12 NFC East Championships. Landry was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 1990.
Related topics: Tom Landry, Pro Football Hall of Fame, Super Bowl VI, Super Bowl XII, NFC East
Introduction
How many head coaches have the Dallas Cowboys had?
The Dallas Cowboys have had nine head coaches. Three of these coaches have won Super Bowls with the team: Tom Landry, Jimmy Johnson, and Barry Switzer.
Related topics: Dallas Cowboys, Tom Landry, Jimmy Johnson (American football coach), Barry Switzer
Key Metrics Explained
What does the 'W%' represent in the coaching records?
The 'W%' represents the win-loss percentage of each head coach during their tenure with the Dallas Cowboys.
Introduction
Who was the first head coach of the Dallas Cowboys?
Tom Landry was the first head coach of the Dallas Cowboys. He coached the team from 1960 to 1988 and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 1990.
Related topics: Tom Landry, Pro Football Hall of Fame
Notable Coaches
Which Dallas Cowboys coaches have won Super Bowl titles?
Three Dallas Cowboys head coaches have won Super Bowl titles. Tom Landry won Super Bowl VI and XII, Jimmy Johnson won Super Bowl XXVII and XXVIII, and Barry Switzer won Super Bowl XXX.
Related topics: Super Bowl VI, Super Bowl XII, Super Bowl XXVII, Super Bowl XXVIII, Super Bowl XXX
Coaching Changes
Who had the shortest tenure as a head coach for the Dallas Cowboys?
Chan Gailey and Dave Campo had the shortest tenures as head coaches for the Cowboys, serving for just two and three seasons respectively.
Related topics: Chan Gailey, Dave Campo
Performance Metrics
Who is the current head coach of the Dallas Cowboys and how has he performed?
As of 2020, Mike McCarthy is the current head coach of the Dallas Cowboys. He leads all coaches in regular season winning percentage with .627.
Related topics: Mike McCarthy
