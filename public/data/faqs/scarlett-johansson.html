FAQs: Scarlett Johansson

Early Life
Where was Scarlett Johansson born and raised?
Scarlett Johansson was born on November 22, 1984, in the Manhattan borough of New York City. She was raised there and has ties to Denmark through her father and Jewish heritage through her mother.
Related topics: New York City, Copenhagen, Jewish
Career Beginnings
How did Scarlett Johansson begin her acting career?
Scarlett Johansson began her acting career at a young age, debuting on stage in an off-Broadway play. Her film debut was in the fantasy comedy 'North' (1994) when she was just nine years old.
Related topics: North (1994 film), Off-Broadway, Manny & Lo
Transition to Adulthood Roles
Which films marked Scarlett Johansson's transition to adult roles?
Scarlett Johansson transitioned to adult roles with prominent films like 'Lost in Translation' (2003), where she played a young wife named Charlotte, and 'Girl with a Pearl Earring' (2003), where she portrayed Griet, a young servant. Both roles garnered significant attention and acclaim.
Related topics: Lost in Translation (film), Girl with a Pearl Earring (film)
Rise to Prominence
What is Scarlett Johansson's involvement with the Marvel Cinematic Universe?
Scarlett Johansson plays Natasha Romanoff, also known as Black Widow, in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. She began portraying this character in 'Iron Man 2' (2010) and continued in several other films, including her solo feature 'Black Widow' (2021). Her role has significantly contributed to her global stardom.
Related topics: Marvel Cinematic Universe, Natasha Romanoff (Marvel Cinematic Universe), Iron Man 2
Recent Years
What recent prominent roles has Scarlett Johansson played?
In recent years, Scarlett Johansson has gained acclaim for her roles in 'Marriage Story' (2019) and 'Jojo Rabbit' (2019), receiving Academy Award nominations for both. These roles showcased her versatility as an actress, playing a divorcee and a mother in Nazi Germany, respectively.
Related topics: Marriage Story, Jojo Rabbit
Music Career
Has Scarlett Johansson pursued a music career?
Yes, Scarlett Johansson has pursued a music career. She released an album titled 'Anywhere I Lay My Head' in 2008, featuring covers of Tom Waits songs. She has also worked on a collaborative album with Pete Yorn and performed with The Jesus and Mary Chain.
Related topics: Scarlett Johansson discography, Anywhere I Lay My Head, Pete Yorn
Philanthropy and Activism
What are some of Scarlett Johansson's contributions to philanthropy and activism?
Scarlett Johansson has been involved in various charitable organizations, including Oxfam, for which she served as an ambassador. She has also supported the Time's Up initiative, participated in the Women's March, and contributed to hurricane relief efforts.
Related topics: Oxfam, Time's Up (organization), Hurricane Maria
Personal Life
Who has Scarlett Johansson been married to?
Scarlett Johansson has been married three times. Her first marriage was to actor Ryan Reynolds from 2008 to 2011. She then married journalist Romain Dauriac from 2014 to 2017, and she has been married to comedian Colin Jost since 2020.
Related topics: Ryan Reynolds, Romain Dauriac, Colin Jost
Public Image
How is Scarlett Johansson perceived in the public eye?
Scarlett Johansson is often regarded as a sex symbol and has been named the 'Sexiest Woman Alive' by Esquire magazine twice. Despite her bombshell image, Johansson has expressed concern about being overly sexualized and emphasized the importance of her acting abilities.
Related topics: Sex symbol, Esquire (magazine)
