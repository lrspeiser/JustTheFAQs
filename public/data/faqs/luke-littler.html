FAQs: Luke Littler

Career Highlights
Who is Luke Littler and what are his major achievements?
Luke Littler is an English professional darts player, known by his nickname 'The Nuke'. He has achieved significant success in the Professional Darts Corporation (PDC) events, where he is ranked World No. 4. He is the reigning champion of the Premier League Darts, Grand Slam of Darts, and World Series of Darts Finals. Notably, he won the 2024 Bahrain Darts Masters, 2024 Poland Darts Masters, 2024 Belgian Darts Open, 2024 Austrian Darts Open, and the 2023 PDC World Youth Championship. Littler is renowned for his remarkable rise in the darts world, having won his first senior title at 14 and becoming the youngest player to hit a televised 9-dart finish.

Personal Life
What is known about Luke Littler's personal life?
Luke Littler was born on January 21, 2007, in Warrington, England. He gained attention during the 2024 PDC World Darts Championship, notably for his relationship with Eloise Milburn, a darts player in the PDC Women's Series. Their relationship received public attention due to their age difference, which led to online backlash. They ended their relationship in July 2024. Littler is a fan of Manchester United FC and Warrington Wolves. He planned to tour Old Trafford and use his tournament winnings to celebrate with friends. Littler is also involved in fashion sponsorships and darts promotions.

Early Life and Career Beginnings
How did Luke Littler start playing darts?
Luke Littler began playing darts at the age of 18 months after his father bought him a magnetic dartboard. By the age of six, he hit his first 180, and at 13, he achieved a nine-dart finish. He joined St Helens Darts Academy at age 10 and played in under-21 leagues, showcasing his talent from a young age.
Related topics: St Helens Darts Academy, Nine-dart finish
Major Tournament Performances
What are Luke Littler's notable performances in major darts tournaments?
Luke Littler's notable performances include reaching the final of the 2024 and 2025 PDC World Darts Championships, his victory in the 2024 Grand Slam of Darts, and wins in the 2024 Premier League Darts and World Series of Darts Finals. He also performed remarkably in the UK Open, World Matchplay, and World Grand Prix, maintaining high averages and reaching advanced stages.

Significant Achievements and Records
What records has Luke Littler set in his darts career?
Luke Littler holds several records, such as being the youngest player to hit a televised 9-dart finish and to win a PDC Major Title. He achieved this feat at the Premier League Darts, setting records for high averages in tournaments and becoming a widely celebrated 17-year-old champion.
Related topics: Nine-dart finish, PDC Major Title
Cultural Impact
How has Luke Littler influenced the sport of darts?
Luke Littler's rapid ascent in the darts world contributed to a surge in the sport's popularity, a phenomenon known as 'Littlermania.' His unexpected performance in the 2024 PDC World Championship drew widespread attention, resulting in record viewership and increased participation in darts.
