FAQs: Kaatera

Overview
What is 'Kaatera'?
'Kaatera' is a 2023 Indian Kannada-language action drama film co-written and directed by Tharun Sudhir. Inspired by real-life incidents in Karnataka during the 1970s, the film stars Darshan, with Aradhana Ram, Jagapathi Babu, and others. The film was produced by Rockline Venkatesh, with music by V. Harikrishna.
Related topics: Karnataka
Production
Who were involved in the making of 'Kaatera'?
'Kaatera' was directed by Tharun Sudhir and co-written with Jadesh Kumar Hampi. It was produced by Rockline Venkatesh under Rockline Entertainments, with cinematography by Sudhakar S. Raj and editing by K. M. Prakash. The music was composed by V. Harikrishna.
Related topics: Tharun Sudhir, Rockline Venkatesh, V. Harikrishna
Plot
What is the story behind 'Kaatera'?
Set in the 1970s and 1989, 'Kaatera' tells the story of a blacksmith who fights to implement land reforms to protect farmers from feudal landlords. It involves themes of love, betrayal, and justice as Kaatera tackles caste discrimination and seeks justice for the death of his beloved.
Related topics: Land reform in India, Caste discrimination in India, Forensic science
Soundtrack
Who composed the music for 'Kaatera'?
The music for 'Kaatera' was composed by V. Harikrishna, a frequent collaborator of the lead actor Darshan.
Related topics: V. Harikrishna
Release and Reception
How was 'Kaatera' received upon release?
'Kaatera' was released on December 29, 2023, to positive reviews from critics. It became the highest-grossing Kannada film of 2023 and was released in multiple countries including Dubai and in nine more countries worldwide.
Related topics: List of Kannada films of 2023, Dubai
Awards and Nominations
What accolades did 'Kaatera' receive?
'Kaatera' received several nominations at the 69th Filmfare Awards South, including Best Film, Best Director, and Best Actor. It won awards at the 12th South Indian International Movie Awards, including Best Film and Best Music Director.
Related topics: 69th Filmfare Awards South, 12th South Indian International Movie Awards
