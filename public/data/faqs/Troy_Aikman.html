FAQs: Troy Aikman

Career Achievements
What are some of Troy Aikman's career highlights?
Troy Aikman is a six-time Pro Bowl selection and has led the Dallas Cowboys to three Super Bowl Championships (XXVII, XXVIII, and XXX). He was the MVP of Super Bowl XXVII and was inducted into the Dallas Cowboys Ring of Honor. Aikman is also a member of the College Football Hall of Fame.
Related topics: Super Bowl XXVII, Super Bowl XXVIII, Super Bowl XXX, Dallas Cowboys
Post-Retirement
What has Troy Aikman been doing since retiring from the NFL?
After retiring from the NFL, Troy Aikman became a prominent NFL game analyst, working for NFL on Fox and ESPN's Monday Night Football. He also has ventures in sports radio and business, including launching a beer brand called Eight.
Related topics: NFL on Fox, Monday Night Football
Personal Life
Is Troy Aikman involved in any philanthropic activities?
Yes, Troy Aikman is actively involved in philanthropic activities. He chaired the Troy Aikman Foundation, focusing on building playplaces for children's hospitals and has merged the foundation with United Way Foundation of Metropolitan Dallas, donating $1 million in the process.
Related topics: Children's hospital
NFL Career
Which NFL team did Troy Aikman play for?
Troy Aikman played his entire NFL career for the Dallas Cowboys from 1989 to 2000. During his tenure, he became one of the most successful quarterbacks in the franchise's history.
Related topics: Dallas Cowboys
Overview
Who is Troy Aikman?
Troy Kenneth Aikman is a former professional American football quarterback who played in the NFL for 12 seasons with the Dallas Cowboys. He is known for leading the Cowboys to three Super Bowl victories and was inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame in 2006.
College Career
Which colleges did Troy Aikman attend?
Troy Aikman started his college career at the University of Oklahoma and then transferred to UCLA, where he excelled as a quarterback, earning Consensus All-America honors and the Davey O'Brien Award.
Related topics: Oklahoma Sooners football, UCLA Bruins football
Statistics
What are Troy Aikman's career NFL statistics?
Troy Aikman's career NFL statistics include 32,942 passing yards, 165 touchdowns, and a 61.5% pass completion percentage. He achieved a passer rating of 81.6 throughout his career.
