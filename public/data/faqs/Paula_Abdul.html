General Information
Who is Paula Abdul?
Paula Julie Abdul is an American singer, dancer, choreographer, actress, and television personality born on June 19, 1962, in San Fernando, California. She became famous in the late 80s with her debut album 'Forever Your Girl' and was a judge on 'American Idol.'
Philanthropy
What philanthropic efforts has Paula Abdul been involved in?
Paula Abdul has been active in raising awareness for National Guide Dog Month and has worked with organizations to support the independence of people with blindness. She does not wear real fur, advocating against it.
Tours and Residencies
What notable tours and residencies has Paula Abdul performed?
Paula Abdul has conducted several tours including the 'Forever Your Girl' tour and 'Straight Up Paula!' tour. She also had a Las Vegas residency titled 'Paula Abdul: Forever Your Girl' from 2019 to 2020.
Television Personality
What television shows has Paula Abdul been a judge on?
Paula Abdul became a household name as one of the original judges on 'American Idol'. She has also judged on shows like 'The X Factor,' 'Live to Dance,' 'So You Think You Can Dance,' and 'The Masked Dancer.'
Awards and Recognitions
What awards has Paula Abdul received?
Paula Abdul has received numerous awards, including a Grammy for Best Music Video for 'Opposites Attract' in 1991, five MTV Video Music Awards, and two Primetime Emmy Awards for Outstanding Choreography.
Career
How did Paula Abdul start her career?
Paula Abdul began her career as a cheerleader for the Los Angeles Lakers at age 18 and rose to fame as the head choreographer for the Laker Girls. Her talent was recognized by The Jacksons, leading to her role as a choreographer for their video 'Torture'.
Personal Life
What is known about Paula Abdul's personal relationships?
Paula Abdul was married to actor Emilio Estevez from 1992 to 1994 and then to clothing designer Brad Beckerman from 1996 to 1998. Both marriages ended in divorce.
Music
What are some of Paula Abdul's top hits?
Some of Paula Abdul's top hits include 'Straight Up,' 'Forever Your Girl,' 'Cold Hearted,' and 'Opposites Attract.' Her debut album 'Forever Your Girl' set a record for the most number-one singles from a debut album.

Health
What health challenges has Paula Abdul faced?
Paula Abdul has faced several health challenges, including a reported plane crash in 1992, bulimia nervosa, and reflex sympathetic dystrophy (RSD), a type of chronic pain disorder.
