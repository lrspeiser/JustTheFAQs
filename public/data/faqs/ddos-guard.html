FAQs: DDoS-Guard

Overview
What is DDoS-Guard?
DDoS-Guard is a Russian Internet infrastructure company specializing in DDoS protection and web hosting services. Founded in 2011, it operates predominantly from Russia and is known for servicing clients associated with controversial and criminal activities, including phishing sites and far-right forums. Notably, it has been linked to clients like Hamas, 8kun, and Parler.
Related topics: Denial-of-service attack, Web hosting service, Hamas, Parler, 8kun
Company Background
When was DDoS-Guard founded and who are its founders?
DDoS-Guard was founded in 2011 by Evgeny Marchenko and Dmitry Sabitov. Initially registered in Sevastopol in 2014, the company later relocated to Russia amidst concerns regarding regulatory investigations in Ukraine due to its activities in hosting forums associated with cybercrime.
Related topics: Evgeny Marchenko, Dmitry Sabitov, Sevastopol
Clientele
Who are some notable clients of DDoS-Guard?
DDoS-Guard has serviced a range of controversial clients, including the militant movement Hamas, the online stalking forum Kiwi Farms, and the far-right imageboard 8kun (formerly 8chan). Their services were also employed by the alt-tech platform Parler following its deplatforming from other service providers.
Related topics: Hamas, Kiwi Farms, 8kun, Parler
Controversies
What controversies surround DDoS-Guard's operations?
DDoS-Guard has faced numerous controversies due to its affiliation with clients linked to illegal activities, such as phishing and drug trafficking. Investigative reports have suggested that a significant number of websites hosted by DDoS-Guard are involved in criminal activities. The company has also been included in the European Commission's "Piracy Watchlist" based on claims it facilitates online piracy.
Related topics: Phishing, European Commission, Online piracy
Government Links
Does DDoS-Guard have any ties to the Russian government?
Yes, DDoS-Guard has established relationships with various Russian state entities, including the Ministry of Defence, since around 2016. Additionally, the company conducted tests for the Russian government's deep packet inspection capabilities.
Related topics: Ministry of Defence (Russia), Deep packet inspection
Projects and Products
What services does DDoS-Guard provide?
DDoS-Guard offers services including DDoS protection, web hosting, and custom solutions for content delivery networks. They also integrate their software with web hosting control panels, enhancing operational efficiency for their clients.
Related topics: Content delivery network
