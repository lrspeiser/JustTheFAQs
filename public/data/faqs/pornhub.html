What is Pornhub?

History and Development
How did Pornhub respond to the 2020 New York Times exposé?
Following a New York Times exposé in December 2020, which raised concerns about non-consensual pornography and sex trafficking, Pornhub's payment processors, Mastercard and Visa, cut ties with the site. In response, Pornhub removed all videos uploaded by unverified users, reducing its content from 13 million to 4 million videos.
