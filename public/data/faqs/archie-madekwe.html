FAQs: Archie Madekwe

Personal Background
What is Archie Madekwe's ethnic background?
Archie Madekwe is of English, Nigerian, and Swiss descent. He was born in South London.
Related topics: Lambeth, Ashley Madekwe
Personal Background
Where did Archie Madekwe receive his education?
Archie Madekwe attended the BRIT School and the National Youth Theatre before furthering his studies at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA). He left LAMDA early to join a West End production.
Related topics: London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art, BRIT School, National Youth Theatre
Career
What notable television roles has Archie Madekwe played?
Archie Madekwe is best known for his role as Kofun in the Apple TV+ series "See" (2019–2022). He also appeared in the BBC adaptation of "Les Misérables," played Luca in "Fresh Meat," and had a role in "Hang Ups."
Related topics: See (TV series), Apple TV+, Les Misérables (British TV series)
Career
In which films has Archie Madekwe appeared?
Archie Madekwe's film credits include roles in "Midsommar" (2019), "Gran Turismo" (2023), "Saltburn" (2023), "Teen Spirit" (2018), and "Voyagers" (2021).
Related topics: Midsommar, Gran Turismo (film), Saltburn (film), Teen Spirit (2018 film), Voyagers (film)
Stage Performances
What was Archie Madekwe's stage debut?
Archie Madekwe made his stage debut in the West End production "The Goat, or Who Is Sylvia?" at the Theatre Royal Haymarket in 2017, where he acted alongside Damian Lewis and Sophie Okonedo.
Related topics: The Goat, or Who Is Sylvia?, Theatre Royal Haymarket
