FAQs: Happy New Year (song)

Overview
What is the background of the song "Happy New Year" by ABBA?
"Happy New Year" is a song by the Swedish group ABBA from their 1980 album Super Trouper, with lead vocals by Agnetha Fältskog. Although it was initially released with a limited distribution as a single in December that year, it has gained popularity over time. The song's working title was "Daddy Don't Get Drunk on Christmas Day."
Related topics: ABBA, Super Trouper (album)
Versions and Releases
Are there different versions of "Happy New Year"?
Yes, there is a Spanish-language version called "Felicidad" which was released in Spanish-speaking territories in 1980. It charted in the top 5 in Argentina, and was included on the South American versions of the Super Trouper album. The song was also re-released in English in 1999, 2008, and on various occasions, charting in multiple countries.
Related topics: Felicidad, Argentina, Super Trouper (album), ABBA Oro: Grandes Éxitos
Chart Performance
How did "Happy New Year" perform on the charts over the years?
The English version of "Happy New Year" charted at number 27 in Sweden, number 15 in the Netherlands, and number 75 in Germany when re-released in 1999. It reached number 4 in Sweden and number 6 in Norway in 2008. It has consistently charted on various occasions, particularly around the New Year.
Related topics: Sverigetopplistan, Dutch Single Top 100, GfK Entertainment charts
Certifications
What certifications has "Happy New Year" received?
The song has been certified Gold in Denmark with over 45,000 sales. These figures include both sales and streaming counts based on the certification alone.
Related topics: IFPI Danmark
A-Teens Version
Did any other artists cover "Happy New Year"?
Yes, the song was covered by the A-Teens and released as a single in 1999 to commemorate the New Millennium. Their version peaked at number 4 on the Swedish charts and was certified Gold shortly after its release.
Related topics: A-Teens, Sverigetopplistan
Cultural Impact
Why is "Happy New Year" considered significant?
"Happy New Year" is a recurring hit that finds renewed popularity during the New Year period. It is played globally during this time, especially in countries like Vietnam.
Related topics: Vietnam
