FAQs: Hugo Lloris

Personal Information
Who is Hugo Lloris and what is his position in football?
Hugo Lloris is a French professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper. He currently plays for Los Angeles FC in Major League Soccer.
Related topics: France national football team, Goalkeeper (association football), Major League Soccer, Los Angeles FC
International Career
How successful was Hugo Lloris with the French national team?
Hugo Lloris had a distinguished career with the France national team, becoming its most-capped player with 145 appearances. He led the team as captain to a victory in the 2018 FIFA World Cup and runner-up positions in the 2016 UEFA European Championship and 2022 FIFA World Cup.
Related topics: France national football team, FIFA World Cup, UEFA European Championship, 2022 FIFA World Cup
Club Career
Which clubs has Hugo Lloris played for and what are his notable club achievements?
Hugo Lloris began his career with OGC Nice, then joined Olympique Lyonnais where he won domestic awards, including being named Ligue 1 Goalkeeper of the Year three times. He became a key figure at Tottenham Hotspur, captaining the team to the 2019 UEFA Champions League final. Currently, he is with Los Angeles FC.
Related topics: OGC Nice, Olympique Lyonnais, Trophées UNFP du football, Tottenham Hotspur F.C., UEFA Champions League
Early Life and Background
What is known about Hugo Lloris's early life and family?
Hugo Lloris was born on December 26, 1986, in Nice, France, into an upper-class family. His mother was a lawyer, and his father is a banker of Catalan Spanish descent. He has a younger brother, Gautier, who also played football. Lloris was initially talented in tennis before focusing on football at age 13.
Related topics: Nice, Monte Carlo, Gautier Lloris, Yoann Gourcuff
Style of Play
What are Hugo Lloris's key strengths as a goalkeeper?
Hugo Lloris is known for his lightning reflexes, good decision-making, and being formidable in one-on-one situations. He is also seen as a 'sweeper-keeper' for his ability to come off his line quickly. Despite occasional high-profile mistakes, he is considered one of the best goalkeepers in the world, appreciated for his acrobatic shot-stopping ability.
Related topics: Goalkeeper (association football), Sweeper-keeper, José Mourinho
Honours
What major honours has Hugo Lloris achieved in his football career?
Hugo Lloris has an impressive list of achievements including winning the 2018 FIFA World Cup with France, the 2020-21 UEFA Nations League, and reaching the final of Euro 2016 and the 2022 FIFA World Cup. At club level, he reached the Champions League final with Tottenham Hotspur in 2018-19 and won the 2024 US Open Cup with Los Angeles FC.
Related topics: FIFA World Cup, UEFA Nations League, UEFA European Championship, UEFA Champions League
