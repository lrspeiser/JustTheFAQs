FAQs: May December

Production
Who were the key people involved in the production of 'May December'?
The screenplay for 'May December' was written by Samy Burch, developed from a story by Burch and Alex Mechanik. Todd Haynes directed the film, which was produced by a team including Natalie Portman, Sophie Mas, Christine Vachon, and others. The film was shot in Savannah, Georgia, over 23 days. Christopher Blauvelt handled cinematography following an injury to Edward Lachman.
Related topics: Samy Burch, Todd Haynes, Natalie Portman, Christine Vachon, Savannah, Georgia
Plot
What is the core premise of 'May December'?
The story centers around actress Elizabeth Berry who travels to Savannah, Georgia, to study Gracie for an upcoming role. Gracie is known for a scandalous affair from when she was 36 and Joe was 13. As Elizabeth delves into their past, meeting with various people from Gracie's life, she discovers contradictions in their narratives. The film blurs lines between reality and representation as Elizabeth becomes emotionally entangled in the story she's set to portray.

Accolades
What awards did 'May December' achieve or get nominated for?
'May December' achieved significant recognition, notably being selected in the top ten films of 2023 by the American Film Institute. It was nominated for four Golden Globe Awards and received a nomination for Best Original Screenplay at the 96th Academy Awards. Charles Melton's performance earned multiple nominations and wins for supporting actor categories at various film festivals.
Related topics: American Film Institute, Golden Globe Awards, 96th Academy Awards
Overview
What is the movie 'May December' about?
'May December' is a 2023 American black comedy drama directed by Todd Haynes. The film follows an actress, played by Natalie Portman, who travels to learn about Gracie Atherton-Yoo, portrayed by Julianne Moore, infamous for her controversial relationship with her husband Joe, played by Charles Melton. This relationship began when Joe was just 13 years old and Gracie was 36. The film explores themes of morality, manipulation, and the complexity of human relationships.
Related topics: Black comedy, Comedy drama, Todd Haynes, Natalie Portman, Julianne Moore, Charles Melton
Reception
How was 'May December' received by critics?
'May December' was critically acclaimed, with a 91% approval rating on Rotten Tomatoes from 322 critic reviews, and a score of 86 out of 100 on Metacritic, indicating universal acclaim. Critics praised the film for its complex storytelling and the performances of Natalie Portman and Julianne Moore, highlighting the film's ability to tackle difficult topics with humor and nuance.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic
Critical Response
What are some critical opinions about 'May December'?
Critics widely acclaimed 'May December' as a fascinating and layered film. Peter Debruge from Variety praised it as an 'endlessly fascinating movie,' while David Ehrlich from IndieWire called it a 'heartbreakingly sincere piece of high camp.' The film's unique blend of humor and drama, along with the standout performances of its lead actors, were particularly highlighted.
