Development
How did 'Monarch: Legacy of Monsters' come to be?
Following the success of the movie 'Godzilla vs. Kong,' Legendary Entertainment explored ways to expand the Monsterverse beyond films. They proposed a live-action series, which led to a collaboration with Apple TV+. The series was created by Chris Black and Matt Fraction, with Matt Shakman directing the initial episodes. It was greenlit by Apple TV+ in early 2022.
