FAQs: XXX Film Series

Cast and Crew
Who are some of the notable cast members in the "XXX" film series?
The series stars Vin Diesel as Xander Cage, Ice Cube as Darius Stone in the second film, and Samuel L. Jackson as NSA Agent Augustus Gibbons throughout. Other notable cast members include Asia Argento, Marton Csokas, and Deepika Padukone.
Related topics: Vin Diesel, Ice Cube, Samuel L. Jackson
Short Film
What role does the short film "The Final Chapter: The Death of Xander Cage" play in the series?
The short film "The Final Chapter: The Death of Xander Cage," included with the 2005 director's cut DVD of the first film, serves as a prequel to "XXX: State of the Union." It details the alleged death of Xander Cage and his efforts to protect himself from an attack orchestrated by villains aligned with Deckert.
Related topics: XXX: State of the Union, Vin Diesel
Films
Who are the directors of the "XXX" film series?
The "XXX" film series was directed by three different directors. Rob Cohen directed the first film, "XXX" (2002). Lee Tamahori took over for the second film, "XXX: State of the Union" (2005). D.J. Caruso directed the third film, "XXX: Return of Xander Cage" (2017), and is expected to return for the untitled fourth film.
Related topics: Rob Cohen, Lee Tamahori, D.J. Caruso
Video Games
Was there ever a video game based on the "XXX" series?
Yes, a video game featuring Xander Cage was released for the Game Boy Advance in 2002. There was also another game for the Xbox in development by Warthog Games that was cancelled, though a prototype was leaked in 2022.
Related topics: Game Boy Advance, Warthog Games, XXX (video game)
Films
What is the main plot of "XXX" (2002)?
In "XXX" (2002), Xander Cage, portrayed by Vin Diesel, is an extreme sports enthusiast and rebellious athlete turned NSA spy. He is tasked with infiltrating a group of possible Russian terrorists in Central Europe and is known for his thrill-seeking and anti-establishment demeanor. The film also stars Asia Argento and Samuel L. Jackson.
Related topics: XXX (2002 film), Vin Diesel, Asia Argento, Samuel L. Jackson
Overview
What is the "XXX" film series about?
The "XXX" (stylized as "xXx") film series is an American spy fiction action series centered around the character Xander Cage. Created by Rich Wilkes, the series includes three full-length films: "XXX" (2002), "XXX: State of the Union" (2005), "XXX: Return of Xander Cage" (2017), and a short film "The Final Chapter: The Death of Xander Cage." The series has grossed $694 million worldwide, focusing on thrill-seeking, extreme sports, and espionage missions.
Related topics: XXX (2002 film), XXX: State of the Union, XXX: Return of Xander Cage
Box Office
How well did the "XXX" films perform at the box office?
The "XXX" film series has been a financial success with a total box office gross of $694 million worldwide. The first film, "XXX" (2002), grossed $277 million, "XXX: State of the Union" (2005) grossed $71 million, and "XXX: Return of Xander Cage" (2017) grossed $346 million.
