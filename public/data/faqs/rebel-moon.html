FAQs: Rebel Moon

General Information
What is 'Rebel Moon'?
'Rebel Moon' is a 2023 American epic space opera film directed by Zack Snyder. It's the first part of a planned franchise and follows the story of Kora, a former soldier who assembles a group of warriors to defend a moon from imperial forces. It was released on December 15, 2023, in the U.S., and on Netflix on December 22, 2023. The film received generally negative reviews from critics.
Related topics: Epic film, Space opera, Netflix, Zack Snyder
Plot
What is the plot of 'Rebel Moon'?
The plot centers on Kora, a former soldier of the oppressive Imperium, who leads a mission to recruit fighters to defend her home on the moon Veldt from an impending attack. The story explores themes of resistance and redemption as Kora assembles a diverse group of warriors, including a disgraced general, a smuggler, and a cyborg swordswoman.
Related topics: Militaristic, Regent, Astral plane
Cast
Who stars in 'Rebel Moon'?
The film features an ensemble cast including Sofia Boutella as Kora, Djimon Hounsou as Titus, Ed Skrein as Atticus Noble, Michiel Huisman as Gunnar, Bae Doona as Nemesis, Ray Fisher as Darrian Bloodaxe, Charlie Hunnam as Kai, and Anthony Hopkins as the voice of Jimmy. Each character brings their unique backstory and skills to the fight against the Imperium.
Related topics: Sofia Boutella, Djimon Hounsou, Ed Skrein, Anthony Hopkins
Development and Production
How did 'Rebel Moon' come about?
Conceptualized by Zack Snyder in college, 'Rebel Moon' was inspired by Akira Kurosawa's works and Star Wars. Snyder pitched the idea to Lucasfilm but developed it as an independent story afterward. Netflix greenlit the project, with filming taking place in California to benefit from tax incentives.
Related topics: Akira Kurosawa, Star Wars, Netflix
Release and Reception
How was 'Rebel Moon' received and released?
Released in theaters in select cities on 70 mm film, 'Rebel Moon' appeared on Netflix on December 22, 2023. It faced criticism for its reliance on established sci-fi tropes, achieving a Rotten Tomatoes score of 22%. However, it received notable viewership numbers on Netflix.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Netflix
Sequels and Future
What are the future plans for 'Rebel Moon'?
'Rebel Moon' is intended to form the basis of a six-film series. The sequel, 'Rebel Moon – Part Two: The Scargiver,' was released on April 19, 2024. Plans include expanding the universe with additional films, a graphical novel, an RPG game, and a potential TV series.
