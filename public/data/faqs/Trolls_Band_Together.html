What is "Trolls Band Together" about?
"Trolls Band Together" is a 2023 American animated jukebox musical comedy film produced by DreamWorks Animation and distributed by Universal Pictures. It serves as the sequel to "Trolls World Tour" (2020) and is the third installment in the Trolls franchise. The story follows Poppy and Branch, now a couple, who attempt to rescue Floyd while reuniting Branch's brothers after the boyband phenomenon, BroZone, was disbanded.

Production
How was 'Trolls Band Together' created and produced?
Production for 'Trolls Band Together' began after the release of 'Trolls World Tour.' Justin Timberlake expressed interest in continuing the franchise, leading to DreamWorks Animation officially confirming the third film's development in November 2021. Directed by Walt Dohrn and co-directed by Tim Heitz, the film incorporates both CGI and 2D animation, with some segments inspired by the classic styles of 'Yellow Submarine' and 'Fantasia'. The music score was composed by Theodore Shapiro.

Plot
What is the plot of 'Trolls Band Together'?
The film follows Branch, a survivalist troll, and Queen Poppy as they attempt to rescue Branch's brother Floyd from a duo of pop star hopefuls, Velvet and Veneer. The siblings have trapped Floyd to extract his musical essence. Branch and Poppy must reunite Branch's old group, BroZone, to perform a Perfect Family Harmony capable of freeing Floyd. The journey includes exploring various troll communities and dealing with sibling tensions, ultimately showcasing themes of family and unity.

Release
How was 'Trolls Band Together' released and distributed?
Trolls Band Together was released theatrically in Denmark on October 12, 2023, and in the United States on November 17, 2023, distributed by Universal Pictures. It reverted to a theater-exclusive release model after its predecessor was released simultaneously on video on demand due to the COVID-19 pandemic.
