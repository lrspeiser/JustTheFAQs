FAQs: Lamar Jackson

Introduction
Who is Lamar Jackson?
Lamar Demeatrice Jackson Jr., born on January 7, 1997, is an American professional football quarterback for the Baltimore Ravens in the NFL. Known for his dual-threat playstyle, he became the second unanimous NFL Most Valuable Player (MVP) and the fourth African-American quarterback to win the award. He gained fame with outstanding performances, including leading the league in touchdown passes in 2019.
Related topics: Baltimore Ravens, National Football League, Quarterback
Early Life and High School
How did Lamar Jackson begin his football career?
Lamar Jackson grew up in Pompano Beach, Florida, in an economically distressed area. At eight years old, he could already throw a football 20 yards. He began playing high school football in his junior year at Boynton Beach High School in Florida. A versatile player, he could both throw the ball effectively and run like a top athlete, accumulating significant stats and performances that gained him attention.
Related topics: Boynton Beach, Florida, Boynton Beach Community High School
College Career
What achievements did Lamar Jackson have during his college football career?
Lamar Jackson played for the Louisville Cardinals from 2015 to 2017, earning the Heisman Trophy in 2016 as the youngest recipient ever at age 19. He also received the Maxwell Award and the Walter Camp Award in the same year and was a unanimous All-American. Jackson finished his college career with significant passing and rushing statistics, solidifying his reputation as a dynamic quarterback.
Related topics: Louisville Cardinals football, Heisman Trophy
Professional Career
How did Lamar Jackson start his NFL career?
Lamar Jackson was drafted by the Baltimore Ravens in the first round, 32nd overall, in the 2018 NFL Draft. He became the starting quarterback during his rookie season after an injury to Joe Flacco. Jackson clinched a division title with the Ravens and became the youngest NFL quarterback to start a playoff game at age 21. He rapidly developed a unique offensive strategy centered on his skills, earning numerous accolades and breaking records.
Related topics: 2018 NFL draft, Joe Flacco
NFL Achievements
What are some of Lamar Jackson's notable NFL achievements?
Throughout his NFL career, Lamar Jackson has set multiple records, such as career rushing yards by a quarterback (6,110) and single-season rushing yards by a quarterback (1,206). He is the first quarterback in NFL history to post four perfect passer rating games with at least 15 passing attempts. Jackson won the NFL MVP award twice, in 2019 and 2023, showcasing his exceptional talent and contribution to football.
Related topics: Perfect passer rating, Rushing yards
Off the Field
What involvement does Lamar Jackson have outside of football?
Outside of football, Lamar Jackson is notable for his business ventures, like launching his sportswear line, Era 8 Apparel, and owning a soul food restaurant. He has also been a cover athlete for both Madden NFL 21 and a virtual reality game. Jackson has engaged in legal actions to protect his brand and image, such as filing a lawsuit against Amazon for unauthorized use of his likeness.
Related topics: Amazon (company), Madden NFL 21
Personal Life
What are some personal details about Lamar Jackson's life?
Lamar Jackson is a Christian and lives in Owings Mills, Maryland, close to the Ravens' training complex. He has a daughter named Milan, nicknamed 'Lani.' As a child, his favorite player was Michael Vick, who inspired him to develop his dual-threat quarterback style.
Related topics: Christianity, Michael Vick
