FAQs: Brad Allen

Career
What controversy did Brad Allen face during the 2023 NFL season?
During the 2023 season, Brad Allen's officiating crew faced controversy after nullifying a Detroit Lions two-point conversion in a game against the Dallas Cowboys. The call, deemed questionable, led to the Lions' loss and resulted in the NFL downgrading Allen's crew and preventing them from officiating playoff games.

What roles does Brad Allen hold outside of officiating NFL games?

Who is Brad Allen and what notable achievement did he accomplish in his first year as an NFL referee?
Brad Allen is an American professional football official in the National Football League (NFL) since 2014. Notably, he was given the referee position in his first year of officiating, a rare honor last achieved in 1962 by Tommy Bell, after Mike Carey announced his retirement.
