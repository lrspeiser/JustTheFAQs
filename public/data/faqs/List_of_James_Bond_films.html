FAQs: List of James Bond Films

Notable Films and Details
What is significant about the film 'Dr. No' (1962)?
'Dr. No' is significant as it was the first James Bond film produced by Eon Productions, released in 1962. It introduced audiences to Sean Connery as James Bond and established several cinematic conventions for the series.
Related topics: Dr. No (film), Sean Connery
Awards and Recognition
How many Academy Awards have James Bond films won?
The James Bond films have won several prestigious awards, including six Academy Awards across various categories. Notable wins include 'Best Sound Editing' for 'Skyfall' (2012) and 'Best Original Song' for 'Skyfall' (2012), 'Spectre' (2015), and 'No Time to Die' (2021).
Related topics: Academy Awards
Critical and Public Reception
Which James Bond film scored the highest on Rotten Tomatoes?
'Goldfinger' (1964) is the highest-rated James Bond film on Rotten Tomatoes, with a 99% approval rating from critics. It is considered a landmark film in the series for its impactful storyline and introduction of iconic elements.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Goldfinger (film)
Eon Productions Films
How many James Bond films have been produced by Eon Productions?
Eon Productions has produced twenty-five James Bond films, from 'Dr. No' (1962) to 'No Time to Die' (2021). These films collectively grossed over $7.8 billion and, when adjusted for inflation, over $19.2 billion.
Related topics: Eon Productions, No Time to Die
Non-Eon Films
What are the non-Eon James Bond films?
The two non-Eon Bond films are 'Casino Royale' (1967), a comedic spoof, and 'Never Say Never Again' (1983), which were produced by different companies. These films have grossed over $200 million, or approximately $895 million when adjusted for inflation.
Related topics: Casino Royale (1967 film), Never Say Never Again
Introduction to James Bond Films
Who created the character James Bond?
James Bond is a fictional character created by British novelist Ian Fleming in 1953. He is a British secret agent working for MI6 under the codename 007.
