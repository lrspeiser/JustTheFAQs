FAQs: Anyone but You

Introduction
What is the movie 'Anyone but You' about?
'Anyone but You' is a 2023 American romantic comedy film directed by Will Gluck, who also co-wrote the screenplay with Ilana Wolpert. It is loosely based on 'Much Ado About Nothing' by William Shakespeare and stars Sydney Sweeney and Glen Powell. The story revolves around Beatrice 'Bea' Messina, a law student, and Ben, an employee at Goldman Sachs, who cross paths multiple times leading to comedic and romantic situations.
Related topics: Much Ado About Nothing, William Shakespeare, Romantic comedy, Will Gluck
Production
Who composed the score for 'Anyone but You'?
The film's score was produced by Este Haim and Christopher Stracey. The title track was released by Still Woozy and Natasha Bedingfield's song 'Unwritten' was prominently featured.
Related topics: Este Haim, Chris Stracey, Still Woozy, Unwritten (song), Natasha Bedingfield
Plot
How does 'Anyone but You' relate to Shakespeare's 'Much Ado About Nothing'?
The film is a loose adaptation of Shakespeare's 'Much Ado About Nothing'. It reflects the original play's themes of romance and misunderstanding. The characters in 'Anyone but You', such as Bea (inspired by Beatrice) and Ben (inspired by Benedick), experience a series of misunderstandings and romantic entanglements similar to those in the play.
Related topics: Much Ado About Nothing, Beatrice (Much Ado About Nothing), Benedick
Cast
Who are the leading actors in 'Anyone but You'?
The film stars Sydney Sweeney as Beatrice 'Bea' Messina and Glen Powell as Ben. It features a supporting cast including Alexandra Shipp, GaTa, Hadley Robinson, Michelle Hurd, Dermot Mulroney, Darren Barnet, Bryan Brown, and Rachel Griffiths.
Related topics: Sydney Sweeney, Glen Powell, Alexandra Shipp, Dermot Mulroney, Rachel Griffiths
Filming Locations
Where was 'Anyone but You' filmed?
Filming took place in New South Wales, Australia, with locations including the Sydney Opera House, Marks Park in Bondi, Palm Beach, Jones Bay Wharf, the Queen Victoria Building, and Barrenjoey Lighthouse.
Related topics: New South Wales, Sydney Opera House, Palm Beach (New South Wales)
Box Office & Reception
How successful was 'Anyone but You' at the box office?
'Anyone but You' grossed $220.3 million worldwide, making it a sleeper hit. Despite mixed reviews from critics, the film was praised for bringing romantic comedies back to the theaters. It is the highest-grossing Shakespeare film adaptation as of 2024.
Related topics: Sleeper hit, Box office, Shakespeare adaptations
Critical Response
What was the critical reception of 'Anyone but You'?
On Rotten Tomatoes, 54% of 115 critics' reviews are positive, with an average rating of 5.6/10. Metacritic scored the film 52 out of 100, indicating mixed or average reviews. Audience reactions were more favorable, with a CinemaScore of 'B+' and PostTrak reporting a 57% recommendation rate.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Metacritic, CinemaScore, PostTrak
