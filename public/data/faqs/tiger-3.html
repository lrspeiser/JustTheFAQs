FAQs: Tiger 3

Critical Response
What were the critics' opinions about 'Tiger 3'?
On Rotten Tomatoes, the film has a 57% approval rating based on 21 critic reviews. While the performances and action sequences were applauded, the plot and pacing were seen as weaknesses. Some critics highlighted Salman Khan's performance and the chemistry between him and Katrina Kaif.
Related topics: Rotten Tomatoes, Salman Khan, Katrina Kaif
Plot Details
What are the main themes explored in 'Tiger 3'?
'Tiger 3' explores themes of espionage, betrayal, and patriotism as it delves into the efforts of Tiger and Zoya to thwart a rogue ISI agent's plot. It also examines the personal connections and relationships within the spy universe, especially between Tiger and Zoya.
Related topics: YRF Spy Universe, ISI, Espionage
Accolades
Did 'Tiger 3' receive any award nominations or wins?
'Tiger 3' received nominations at the 69th Filmfare Awards for Best Supporting Actor (Emraan Hashmi) and Best Action but did not win. At the IIFA Awards, it was nominated for Best Villain for Emraan Hashmi.
Related topics: Filmfare Awards, IIFA Awards, Emraan Hashmi
Film Development
How did 'Tiger 3' come into production?
A sequel to 'Tiger Zinda Hai' was reported to be in development in early 2020. Maneesh Sharma was finalized as the director in late 2020, replacing Ali Abbas Zafar. The project was officially announced by Yash Raj Films through a teaser video on 4 March 2022.
Related topics: Tiger Zinda Hai, Ali Abbas Zafar, Yash Raj Films
Soundtrack
Who composed the music for 'Tiger 3'?
The soundtrack for 'Tiger 3' was composed by Pritam, with lyrics by Irshad Kamil and Amitabh Bhattacharya. The background score was provided by Tanuj Tiku. The album was released under the YRF Music label.

General Information
What is 'Tiger 3' about?
'Tiger 3' is a 2023 Indian Hindi-language action thriller film directed by Maneesh Sharma and produced by Aditya Chopra under Yash Raj Films. It is the fifth installment in the YRF Spy Universe and serves as a circumquel to 'Ek Tha Tiger' and 'Tiger Zinda Hai'. The plot follows spies Tiger and Zoya as they track down and combat a rogue ISI agent who is planning a destructive coup against the Government of Pakistan with a hidden agenda against India.
Related topics: Maneesh Sharma, Aditya Chopra, Yash Raj Films
